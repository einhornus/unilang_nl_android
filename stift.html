stift (26916)


Noun

stift f (plural stiften, diminutive stiftje n)

  1. graphite core of a pencil
  2. a felt-tip pen, a marker
  3. a chip (in football).

Verb

stift

  1. first-, second- and third-person singular present indicative of stiften
