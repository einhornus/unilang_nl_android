toestemmen (34622)


Etymology

Compound of toe (“to(wards)”) + | stemmen (“to speak”), perfectly parallel to the cognate German equivalent zustimmen.

Verb

toestemmen

  1. (intransitive) To consent, agree
  2. (unusual) (transitive) To award
  3. (unusual) (transitive) To allow
  4. (obsolete, transitive) To admit, acknowledge

Synonyms

  • (consent): instemmen
  • (award): toekennen
  • (allow): toelaten
  • (admit): erkennen

Derived terms

  • toestem
  • toestemmend (adjective)
  • toestemmer m
  • toestemmig
  • toestemming

Related terms

  • afstemmen

Werkwoord

toestemmen

  1. inergatief iets inwilligen of goedkeuren.
