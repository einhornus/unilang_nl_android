boerenzoon (54934)


Etymology

Compound of boer (“farmer”) + | -en- + | zoon (“son”).

Noun

boerenzoon m (plural boerenzonen or boerenzoons, diminutive boerenzoontje n)

  1. (literally) a farmer's son
  2. (by extension, rather rare) farmboy, country-boy
