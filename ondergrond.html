ondergrond (31681)


Etymology

From onder + | grond. Compare English underground and German Untergrund.

Noun

ondergrond m (plural ondergronden, diminutive ondergrondje n)

  1. underground, subsoil, subsurface, bottom
  2. ground, basis, foundation, groundwork
  3. (heraldry) compartment (mound beneath the shield in a coat of arms on which the supporters stand)

Related terms

  • ondergronds
