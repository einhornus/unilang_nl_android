aanroepen (35381)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch aenroepen. Equivalent to aan + | roepen.

Verb

aanroepen

  1. (transitive) to invoke (call upon someone for help etc.)
  2. (transitive, computing) to call, to invoke (a subroutine, etc.)
Inflection
