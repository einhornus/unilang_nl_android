argeloos (45896)


Etymology

Borrowed from German arglos. Equivalent to contemporary erg (“bad, evil”) + | -loos (“less”). First attested in the late 18th century.

Adjective

argeloos (comparative argelozer, superlative meest argeloos or argeloost)

  1. unsuspecting
