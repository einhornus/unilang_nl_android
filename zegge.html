zegge (66455)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch *segge, from Old Dutch *seggi, from Proto-Germanic *sagjaz.

Noun

zegge f (plural zeggen, diminutive zeggetje n)

  1. sedge, plant of the genus Carex
