koortsachtig (56970)


Etymology

From koorts (“fever”) + | -achtig (“-ly, -ish”).

Adjective

koortsachtig (comparative koortsachtiger, superlative koortsachtigst)

  1. (literally) feverous, suffering from fever
  2. (figurative) (by far the most common use) frenetic, feverish, nervous, haste-stressed
  3. resembling fever
  4. causing fever

Inflection

Synonyms

  • (suffering from fever) koortsig
  • (frenetic) haastig, jachtig

Related terms

  • koortslijder m
