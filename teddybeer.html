teddybeer (12661)


Etymology

Borrowed from English teddybear. Named after Theodore Roosevelt, who was nicknamed "Teddy".

Noun

teddybeer m (plural teddyberen, diminutive teddybeertje n)

  1. A teddy bear.
    Synonym: knuffelbeer

Zelfstandig naamwoord

teddybeer m

  1. zachte pluizige speelgoedbeer
