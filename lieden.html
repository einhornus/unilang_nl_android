lieden (15795)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch liede, originally a singular collective noun. From Old Dutch *liudi, from Proto-Germanic *liudiz.

Cognate with English lout, lede, Middle English leod and leed, and German Leute.

Noun

lieden pl (plural only)

  1. men; people

Synonyms

  • lui
  • mensen

Zelfstandig naamwoord
