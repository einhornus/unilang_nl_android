spruitje (79378)


Noun

spruitje n (plural spruitjes)

  1. Diminutive of spruit
  2. (commonly used in the plural form) A Brussels sprout, a variant of Brassica oleracea of the gemmifera group.
    Synonym: spruitkool

spruitje o
