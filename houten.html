houten (5112)


Etymology

For the adjective, hout + | -en

Adjective

houten (not comparable)

  1. wooden, made of wood
    een houten been | a wooden leg

Inflection

Noun

houten

  1. (obsolete) Plural form of hout.

Verb

houten

  1. to paint something, so as to give the impression that it is made of beautifully figured wood.

Inflection

Related terms

  • marmeren
