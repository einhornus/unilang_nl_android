eer (999)

From Middle Dutch ere, from Old Dutch ēra, from Proto-Germanic *aizō.

Noun

eer f (uncountable)

  1. honour

From Middle Dutch eer, from Old Dutch ēr, from Proto-Germanic *airiz.

Preposition

eer

Related terms

Etymology 3

From Middle Dutch eer, from Old Dutch *ēr, from Proto-Germanic *aiz.

Noun

eer n (uncountable)

  1. (obsolete) copper
  2. (obsolete) bronze
