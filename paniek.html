paniek (1630)


Etymology

Borrowed from French panique, from Middle French panique, from Ancient Greek πανικός (panikós).

Noun

paniek f (uncountable, diminutive paniekje n)

  1. panic

Derived terms

  • paniekerig
  • paniekreactie
  • paniekstemming
  • paniekvoetbal

Related terms

  • panisch

Adjective

paniek (not comparable)

  1. (obsolete) panic [17th - 19th c.]
    Synonym: panisch

Zelfstandig naamwoord
