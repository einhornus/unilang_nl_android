krullenkop (74830)


Etymology

From krullen (“curls”) + | kop (“head”).

Noun

krullenkop m (plural krullenkoppen, diminutive krullenkopje n)

  1. a head with lots of curls
  2. a curly-headed person, a curly

Synonyms

  • (head) krullenbol
  • (person) krullenbol
