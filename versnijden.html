versnijden (28415)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch versniden, versnyden. Equivalent to ver- + | snijden.

Verb

versnijden

  1. (transitive) to cut up, to cut in pieces
  2. (transitive, obsolete) to destroy by cutting, to ruin by cutting
  3. (transitive) to dilute, to mix, to adulterate, to step on
  4. (transitive, of old beer) to blend or mix (with younger beer) (done to certain beers, like gueuze)

Inflection

Werkwoord

versnijden | 1 |

  1. overgankelijk in stukken snijden
  2. overgankelijk aanlengen met iets van mindere kwaliteit
