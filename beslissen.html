beslissen (2014)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch beslissen. Equivalent to be- + | slissen.

Verb

beslissen

  1. (transitive) to decide, to determine
  2. (transitive) to decide, to make a definitive choice (between options)

Synonyms

  • besluiten

Derived terms

  • besliskunde
  • beslissing
