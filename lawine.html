lawine (14821)


Etymology

From German Lawine, from Alemannic German Lauwin, from Middle High German lowin, from Old High German lowin, from Late Latin labina, derived from Latin lābī.

Noun

lawine f (plural lawines or lawinen, diminutive lawinetje n)

  1. avalanche, landslide
