overspelen (80591)


Etymology 1

From over + | spelen.

Verb

overspelen

  1. to play again, to replay
  2. (soccer) to pass a ball to another player

Etymology 2

From over- + | spelen.

Verb

overspelen

  1. to overplay, to overact
