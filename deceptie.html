deceptie (107775)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch deceptie (“deceit”).

Noun

deceptie f (plural decepties)

  1. disappointment, disillusion (quality of being disappointed)
    Synonym: teleurstelling
  2. something that causes disappointment
    Synonym: teleurstelling
  3. (obsolete) deception, deceit
    Synonyms: bedrieging, bedrog
