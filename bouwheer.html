bouwheer (108595)


Etymology

From bouwen (“to build”) + | heer (“lord”).

Noun

bouwheer m (plural bouwheren, diminutive bouwheertje n)

  1. (historical) A master mason.
  2. (Belgium) A man who commissions an architect for an architectural design.
