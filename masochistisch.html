masochistisch (68827)


Etymology

From masochist + | -isch.

Adjective

masochistisch (comparative masochistischer, superlative meest masochistisch)

  1. Masochistic, masochist.
  2. (informal) Pertaining to or characteristic of a glutton for punishment.
    Waarom heb je dat paupermerk gekozen? Ben je masochistisch ofzo?
    Why did you go for that cheap rip-off brand? Are you a glutton for punishment or what?

Related terms

  • masochist
