afzakken (38580)


Etymology

From af + | zakken.

Verb

afzakken

  1. (intransitive) to drop down, to come down
    Hee! Je broek zakt af!
    Hey! Your trousers/pants are coming down!
  2. (intransitive) to sag
  3. (intransitive) to decrease, to lessen
  4. (intransitive, of rain) to slowly disperse, to float away
  5. (intransitive, of crowd) to disperse
  6. (intransitive, figuratively) to sink to a lower level, to fall from grace

Inflection

Werkwoord

afzakken

  1. ergatief naar beneden glijden
    • Die grote broek bleef maar afzakken.
  2. ergatief alcohol drinken
    • Na de film gingen we nog even afzakken in de stad.
