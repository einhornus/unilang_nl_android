getrouwe (60901)


Etymology

Inflected form of getrouw.

Adjective

getrouwe

  1. Inflected form of getrouw

Noun

getrouwe m or f (plural getrouwen)

  1. (chiefly plural) A loyal servant, follower or associate, a true person (i.e. who is faithful, loyal).
  2. (chiefly plural) A bosom friend.
