uitnodiging (3090)


Etymology

From uitnodigen (“to invite”) + | -ing

Noun

uitnodiging f (plural uitnodigingen, diminutive uitnodigingetje n)

  1. invitation; the act of inviting; solicitation, the requesting of a person's company
    • Dutch Wikipedia, Linkin Park
      Rond deze tijd kreeg de band veel uitnodigingen voor optredens met grote artiesten, waaronder Ozzfest, Family Values toer en KROQ Almost Acoustic Christmas.
      Around this time the band received many invitations for performances with big artists, among which Ozzfest, Family Values tour and KROQ Almost Acoustic Christmas.
  2. A document written or printed, or spoken words, conveying the message by which one is invited.

Synonyms

  • invitatie

Zelfstandig naamwoord

uitnodiging v
