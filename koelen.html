koelen (8934)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch coelen, a merger of two Old Dutch verbs:

  • *kuolen (“make cooler”), from Proto-Germanic *kōlijaną.
  • *kuolon (“become cooler”), from Proto-Germanic *kōlōną.

Equivalent to koel + | -en.

Verb

koelen

  1. (ergative) to cool

Derived terms

  • koeler
  • koelkast
  • afkoelen
  • bekoelen
  • verkoelen
