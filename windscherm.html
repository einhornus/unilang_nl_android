windscherm (58526)


Etymology

From wind (“wind”) + | scherm (“screen”). The sense “windscreen of an aircraft” derives from English windscreen.

Noun

windscherm n (plural windschermen, diminutive windschermpje n)

  1. A windbreak (screen to protect against the wind).
  2. (aviation) The windscreen of an aircraft.

Related terms

  • regenscherm
  • zonnescherm
