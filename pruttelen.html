pruttelen (43185)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch protelen. This etymology is incomplete. You can help Wiktionary by elaborating on the origins of this term.

Verb

pruttelen

  1. to simmer, cook slowly over low heat
  2. (engine etc...) to make a low repeating noise, hum, purr
