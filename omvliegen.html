omvliegen (126556)


Etymology 1

From om + | vliegen.

Verb

omvliegen

  1. (transitive) to fly around
  2. (intransitive) to turn around while flying

Etymology 2

From om- + | vliegen.

Verb

omvliegen

  1. (transitive) to surround while flying
