zwartmaken (41224)


Etymology

Compound of zwart (“black”) + | maken (“to make”).

Verb

zwartmaken

  1. (literally) To blacken, to make black(er)
  2. (figuratively) To blacken, to defame, to present (too) badly

Inflection

Werkwoord
