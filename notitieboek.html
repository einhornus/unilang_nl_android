notitieboek (30345)


Etymology

From notitie + | boek. Compare German Notizbuch, Danish notesbog.

Noun

notitieboek n (plural notitieboeken, diminutive notitieboekje n)

  1. notebook (book in which notes are written)

Synonyms

  • cahier
  • schrift
