bajes (8865)


Etymology

Borrowed from Yiddish בית | (bajes, “house”), which is borrowed from Hebrew בית | (báyit, “house”).

Noun

bajes f (plural bajesen, diminutive bajesje n)

  1. (Netherlands, informal) slammer, jail, prison

Synonyms

  • gevangenis

Derived terms

  • bajesklant
