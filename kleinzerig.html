kleinzerig (54110)


Etymology

From klein (“small”) + | zeer (“hurt”) + | -ig (“-ish”).

Adjective

kleinzerig (comparative kleinzeriger, superlative kleinzerigst)

  1. petty in relation to pain or hardship, with a low pain threshold, with little endurance

Inflection
