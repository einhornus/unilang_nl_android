franciscaner (106717)


Etymology

From franciscaan + | -er.

Adjective

franciscaner (not comparable)

  1. Franciscan
    Synonym: franciscaans

Usage notes

Chiefly used of people, organisations and buildings, having a narrower range than franciscaans.

Noun

franciscaner m (plural franciscaners)

  1. (rare) A Franciscan.
    Synonym: franciscaan
