dievegge (26730)


Etymology

Equivalent to dief (“thief”) + | -egge (“suffix appended to words to make a female form”). From Middle Dutch diefegge. This suffix is no longer productive.

Noun

dievegge f (plural dievegges or dieveggen, diminutive dieveggetje n, masculine dief)

  1. thief (female)

Zelfstandig naamwoord

dievegge v

  1. vrouwelijk persoon die steelt
