inkopen (12301)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch incopen. Equivalent to in + | kopen.

Verb

inkopen

  1. (transitive) to purchase, to buy, to acquire through purchase
    Synonyms: inslaan, kopen
  2. (reflexive) to buy oneself [+ in (into)] (something, e.g. an enterprise)

inkopen
