bevriezen (8905)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch bevriesen. Equivalent to be- + | vriezen.

Verb

bevriezen

  1. (ergative) to freeze (solid)
  2. (intransitive) to freeze, to be completely still and immobile
  3. (transitive) to stop, to halt

Inflection

Werkwoord

bevriezen

  1. ergatief door afkoeling in vaste toestand komen
    • Water bevriest bij nul graden Celsius.
