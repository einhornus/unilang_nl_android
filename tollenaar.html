tollenaar (87274)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch tollenâer. Equivalent to tol + | -en- + | -aar.

Noun

tollenaar m (plural tollenaars, diminutive tollenaartje n)

  1. (historical) A tax collector, in particular a collector of tolls; a publican.
