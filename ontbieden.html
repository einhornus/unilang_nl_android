ontbieden (51085)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch ontbieden. Equivalent to ont- + | bieden.

Verb

ontbieden

  1. (transitive) To summon, send for someone
  2. (obsolete, transitive) To notify, tell, inform.
  3. (obsolete, transitive) To order, tell what to do.

Synonyms

  • gebieden

Derived terms

  • afontbieden
  • ontbieding

Related terms

  • verbieden
