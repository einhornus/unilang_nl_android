castratie (31319)


Etymology

Borrowed from Latin castrātiō.

Noun

castratie f (plural castraties, diminutive castratietje n)

  1. A castration, emasculation by surgical removal of the testicles.

Synonyms

  • lubbing
  • ontmanning

Related terms

  • castraat
  • castreren
