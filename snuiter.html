snuiter (18915)


Noun

snuiter m (plural snuiters, diminutive snuitertje n)

  1. snuffer
    1. type of scissors used for extinguishing candles
    2. conical extinguisher
  2. (by extension) (curious) fellow, chap, (often) a strange chap

Usage notes

  • When denoting a person, the word is very often preceded by the pleonastic epithets raar or vreemd.
