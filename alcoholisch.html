alcoholisch (95843)


Etymology

Probably borrowed from German alkoholisch or English alcoholic, etc. Equivalent to alcohol + | -isch.

Adjective

alcoholisch (comparative alcoholischer, superlative meest alcoholisch or alcoholischt)

  1. alcoholic [from early 19th c.]

Inflection
