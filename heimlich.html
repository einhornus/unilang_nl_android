heimlich (40726)


Etymology

From Middle High German heimlich, heimelich, from Old High German heimilīh, from Proto-Germanic *haimalīkaz, equivalent to Heim + | -lich. Compare Dutch heimelijk, English homely, Danish hemmelig, Swedish hemlig. More at homely.

Adjective

heimlich (comparative heimlicher, superlative am heimlichsten)

  1. secret, clandestine (done or kept in secret)

Synonyms

  • klammheimlich
  • geheim

Related terms

  • geheim
  • unheimlich
