tegenwerping (98947)


Etymology

From tegenwerpen (“to (make) object(ions)”) + | -ing (“-ion”)

Noun

tegenwerping f (plural tegenwerpingen, diminutive tegenwerpinkje n)

  1. objection, statement expressing opposition
