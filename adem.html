adem (1284)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch âdem, from Old Dutch *āthom, from Proto-Germanic *ēþmaz.

Noun

adem m (uncountable)

  1. breath

Etymology 2

See the etymology of the main entry.

Verb

adem

  1. first-person singular present indicative of ademen
  2. imperative of ademen
