bestendigen (109061)


Etymology

From bestendig + | -en, calque of German beständigen.

Verb

bestendigen

  1. (transitive) to perpetuate, to prolong, to reinforce, to consolidate [from late 17th c.]

Inflection

Synonyms

  • consolideren
  • in stand houden

Werkwoord

bestendigen
