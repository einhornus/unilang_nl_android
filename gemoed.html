gemoed (32644)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch ghemoede. Analyzable as ge- + | moed. Cognate with German Gemüt, Old English gemēde.

Noun

gemoed n (plural gemoederen, diminutive gemoedje n)

  1. feeling; heart; soul; mind
