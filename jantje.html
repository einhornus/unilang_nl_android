jantje (57116)


Etymology

From the given name Jan (Dutch equivalent of John), via its derivate jan or directly from Jan's diminutive Jantje

Noun

jantje n (plural jantjes)

  1. A (Dutch) marine or sailor
  2. the diminutive of jan (in all other senses)

Synonyms

  • (sailor) janmaat m

Related terms

  • jantje-van-leiden n
  • Jantje Contrarie n
  • Jantje Precies n
