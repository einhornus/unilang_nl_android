sufferd (6812)


Etymology

From suf + | -erd.

Noun

sufferd m (plural sufferds, diminutive sufferdje n)

  1. fool, idiot
  2. (in the diminutive) local newspaper
  3. (obsolete) dotard, person with mental impairments

Synonyms

  • domoor
