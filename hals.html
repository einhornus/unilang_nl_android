hals (5853)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch hals, from Old Dutch hals, from Proto-Germanic *halsaz.

Noun

hals m (plural halzen, diminutive halsje n)

  1. (narrow sense) Front side of the neck; throat.
    Synonym: keel
  2. (broad sense) Neck.
    Synonym: nek

Related terms

  • halsstarrig
