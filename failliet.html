failliet (5288)


Etymology

Borrowed form French faillite, from Italian fallito.

Adjective

failliet (comparative faillieter, superlative faillietst)

  1. bankrupt

Inflection

Noun

failliet n (plural faillieten)

  1. (chiefly Belgium) bankruptcy
  2. (figuratively) bankruptcy, failure

Synonyms

  • bankroet
  • faillissement
  • faling

Noun

failliet m (plural faillieten)

  1. bankrupt person

Synonyms

  • bankroetier

Related terms

  • faillissement
