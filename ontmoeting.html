ontmoeting (2825)


Etymology

From ontmoeten (“to meet”) + | -ing.

Noun

ontmoeting f (plural ontmoetingen, diminutive ontmoetinkje n)

  1. a meeting (an encounter between people - not an event or gathering of business people)
