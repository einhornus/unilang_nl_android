Noun

mijl f (plural mijlen, diminutive mijltje n)

  1. a mile
    1. Imperial mile (approx. 1.6 km)
    2. (obsolete) Dutch mile, league (approx. 5 to 6 km)
  2. (obsolete, Dutch metric system) a kilometre [19th c.]
