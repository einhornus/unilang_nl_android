blinken (25441)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch blinken, from Old Dutch *blinkan, from Proto-Germanic *blinkanÄ….

Verb

blinken

  1. (intransitive) to shine, to glitter (reflect light)
    Synonyms: glinsteren, schitteren
