ginds (12932)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch ginds, gins, gens, guns, akin to Middle Low German junt, English yond, Gothic 𐌾𐌰𐌹𐌽𐌳 (jaind). More at yonder.

Adverb

ginds

  1. Over there, yonder
