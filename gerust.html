gerust (1832)


Adjective

gerust (comparative geruster, superlative meest gerust or gerustst)

  1. at ease; free of anxiety, without fear or worry

Derived terms

  • geruststellen

Participle

gerust

  1. past participle of rusten

Werkwoord

gerust

  1. voltooid deelwoord van rusten
