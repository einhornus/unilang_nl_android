bijknippen (45804)


Etymology

From bij (indicates repetition of an action) + | knippen (“to cut”).

Verb

bijknippen

  1. (transitive) to trim

Usage notes

  • Though not proscribed by any authority, bijknippen is often deprecated by pedants on the mistaken assumption that bij means “additional” in this word.

Werkwoord

bijknippen

  1. overgankelijk in model brengen door het overtollige af te knippen
