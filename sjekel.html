sjekel (63004)


Etymology

More recent than sikkel. Ultimately from Hebrew שֶׁקֶל | , see there for further etymology.

Noun

sjekel m (plural sjekels, diminutive sjekeltje n)

  1. (biblical) shekel, ancient Israelite unit of weight
  2. shekel, ancient Judean monetary unit
  3. shekel, modern Israeli monetary unit

Synonyms

  • (ancient units of weight and currency): sikkel
