aartsvijand (24297)


Etymology

From aarts- (“arch-”) + | vijand (“enemy”). Cognate to English archfiend, German Erzfeind.

Noun

aartsvijand m (plural aartsvijanden, diminutive aartsvijandje n, feminine aartsvijandin)

  1. archenemy, a principal enemy

Synonyms

Related terms
