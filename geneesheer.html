geneesheer (35137)


Etymology

From genezen (“to heal”) + | heer (“gentleman, lord”).

Noun

geneesheer m (plural geneesheren, diminutive geneesheertje n)

  1. (formal) doctor

Synonyms

  • arts
  • dokter
  • medicus
