wervel (23971)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch wervel, from Old Dutch *wirvil, from Proto-Germanic *hwirbilaz.

Noun

wervel m (plural wervels, diminutive werveltje n)

  1. vertebra

Etymology 2

See the etymology of the main entry.

Verb

wervel

  1. first-person singular present indicative of wervelen
  2. imperative of wervelen
