viswijf (50362)


Etymology

Compound of vis (“fish”) + | wijf (“woman”, often having pejorative connotations now).

Noun

viswijf n (plural viswijven, diminutive viswijfje n)

  1. (derogatory) fishwife, female fishmonger
  2. (derogatory) scold
