verdomme (271)


Etymology

The present singular subjunctive form of verdommen, likely an alteration of verdoeme, from verdoemen (“to damn”).

Interjection

verdomme

  1. dammit!

Verb

verdomme

  1. (archaic) singular present subjunctive of verdommen

Werkwoord

verdomme

  1. aanvoegende wijs van verdommen
