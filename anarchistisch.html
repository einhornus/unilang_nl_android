anarchistisch (95783)


Etymology

From anarchist + | -isch (“-ish, -ic”).

Adjective

anarchistisch (comparative anarchistischer, superlative meest anarchistisch or anarchistischt)

  1. anarchistic, relating or belonging to anarchism

Inflection

Related terms

  • anarchie
  • anarchisme
  • anarchist
