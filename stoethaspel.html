stoethaspel (67296)


Etymology

Probably a compound of stoetel + | haspel.

Noun

stoethaspel m (plural stoethaspels, diminutive stoethaspeltje n)

  1. (derogatory) A clumsy person; a galoot, klutz, butterfingers.
