kokos (47084)


Etymology

Borrowed from Spanish coco or Portuguese coco. The final s was originally a plural ending.

Noun

kokos n or c (uncountable)

  1. the white flesh of the coconut, often in a desiccated form

Noun

kokos c (plural kokossen)

  1. coconut

Derived terms

  • kokosbrood
  • kokoskrab
  • kokosnoot
  • kokosolie
  • kokospalm
