potentie (15773)


Etymology

Borrowed from Latin potentia.

Noun

potentie f (uncountable)

  1. potency, strength
  2. potential, unrealised ability
    Synonym: potentieel
  3. capability of procreation
  4. power, might, capability
    Synonym: macht
  5. (obsolete, mathematics) power
    Synonym: macht

Related terms

  • potentiaal
  • potentieel
