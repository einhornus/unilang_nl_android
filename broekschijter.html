broekschijter (84541)


Etymology

From broek (“trousers”) + | schijter (“shitter, one who poops”).

Noun

broekschijter m (plural broekschijters, diminutive broekschijtertje n)

  1. (literally) One, usually a child, who shits in one's pants or trousers.
  2. (derogatory) a coward

Synonyms

  • (coward): platbroek, schijthuis m, schijtlaars, schijtlijster, schrikschijter m
