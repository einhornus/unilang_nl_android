bewoonbaar (45811)


Etymology

From bewonen (“to inhabit”) + | -baar.

Adjective

bewoonbaar (comparative bewoonbaarder, superlative bewoonbaarst)

  1. habitable, fit for inhabitation, where humans or other animals can live (or fairly comfortably)

Inflection

Related terms

  • bewoner m
  • bewoning
