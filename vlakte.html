vlakte (11452)


Etymology

From vlak + | -te.

Noun

vlakte f (plural vlakten or vlaktes, diminutive vlaktetje n)

  1. Flatness, levelness.
  2. A plain, an expanse (tract of land with relatively low relief, level terrain).
