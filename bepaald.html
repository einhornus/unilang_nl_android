bepaald (1881)


Etymology 1

From bepaald, the past participle of bepalen.

Adjective

bepaald (comparative bepaalder, superlative bepaaldst)

  1. certain
  2. specific
  3. (grammar) definite
Inflection

Etymology 2

From the adjective bepaald.

Adverb

bepaald

  1. certainly, definitely, absolutely

Etymology 3

See the etymology of the main entry.

Participle

bepaald

  1. past participle of bepalen
Inflection

Werkwoord
