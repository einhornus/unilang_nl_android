hoedanigheid (27125)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch hoedanicheit, calque of Latin qualitās. Equivalent to hoedanig + | -heid.

Noun

hoedanigheid f (plural hoedanigheden, diminutive hoedanigheidje n)

  1. quality, form, appearance
