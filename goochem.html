goochem (69809)


Etymology

Borrowed from Yiddish חכם | (khokhem), from Hebrew חָכָם | (khakhám).

Adjective

goochem (comparative goochemer, superlative goochemst)

  1. (informal) smart, cunning, streetwise

Inflection

Bijvoeglijk naamwoord

goochem

  1. (Jiddisch-Hebreeuws) slim
