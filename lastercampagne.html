lastercampagne (56906)


Etymology

Compound of laster (“defamation”) + | campagne (“campaign”).

Noun

lastercampagne f (plural lastercampagnes)

  1. smear campaign, defamation campaign
    Synonym: lasterveldtocht

Zelfstandig naamwoord
