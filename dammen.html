dammen (15696)


Etymology 1

Borrowed from Middle French dame (“dame, double checkers piece”) + | -en.

Verb

dammen

  1. (intransitive) to play checkers
Inflection
Related terms
  • dam

Etymology 2

Gerund of the verb dammen.

Noun

dammen n (uncountable)

  1. checkers, draughts
Synonyms
  • damspel
