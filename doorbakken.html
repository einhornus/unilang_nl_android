doorbakken (17865)


Etymology 1

Compound of door- + | bakken.

Verb

doorbakken

  1. (transitive) to bake thoroughly
  2. (transitive) to bake completely, bake from beginning till end
Inflection

Etymology 2

Compound of door + | bakken.

Verb

doorbakken

  1. (intransitive) to continue baking
Inflection

Participle

doorbakken

  1. past participle of doorbakken
