bottel (132879)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch buttel. Equivalent to bot (“bud”) + | -el.

Noun

bottel f (plural bottels, diminutive botteltje n)

  1. hip, rosehip
    Synonym: rozenbottel
  2. (obsolete) dogwood
    Synonym: kornoelje

Etymology 2

Borrowed from English bottle.

Noun

bottel f (plural bottels, diminutive botteltje n)

  1. (dated) bottle [from 17th c.]
    Synonym: fles
Related terms
  • bouteille

Zelfstandig naamwoord
