hometrainer (64745)


Etymology

From home + | trainer. A compound word coined by Dutch speakers using English component words. The compound does not exist in English, but does in German as Hometrainer (“stationary bicycle”).

Noun

hometrainer m (plural hometrainers, diminutive hometrainertje n)

  1. exercise bicycle, stationary bicycle

Synonyms

  • kamerfiets
