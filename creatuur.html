creatuur (42435)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch creature, from Latin creātūra, from creō (“to create”).

Noun

creatuur n (plural creaturen, diminutive creatuurtje n)

  1. creature
    Synonym: schepsel
  2. monster, monstrous or loathsome creature
    Synonyms: gedrocht, monster
