meuren (115250)


Etymology

Likely an onomatopoeia.

Verb

meuren

  1. (Netherlands, informal) to sleep
  2. (Netherlands, informal) to reek, to stink
  3. (Netherlands, informal) to fart

Derived terms

  • meur
