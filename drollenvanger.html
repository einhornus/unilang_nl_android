drollenvanger (107364)


Etymology

From drol (“turd”) + | -en- (interfix) + | vangen (“to catch”) + | -er (deverbal agent noun suffix).

Noun

drollenvanger m (plural drollenvangers, diminutive drollenvangertje n)

  1. (colloquial) a type of baggy pants with tight cuffs and often a low crotch, including knickerbockers, harem pants and plus fours.
  2. a nappy/diaper for horses or dogs.
