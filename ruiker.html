ruiker (88589)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch ruker. Equivalent to ruiken + | -er.

Noun

ruiker m (plural ruikers, diminutive ruikertje n)

  1. A flower bouquet.
    Synonyms: boeket, handruiker
  2. One who smells using one's olfactory organs.
