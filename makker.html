makker (2127)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch macker, probably from *gemacke (“companion”). More at make, match.

Noun

makker m (plural makkers, diminutive makkertje n)

  1. A mate, fellow, colleague, companion, (not very close) friend, ally etc.
Synonyms
  • makkerman m, medemakker m
  • gezel m
  • maat m
  • kameraad m
  • collega m
Derived terms
  • (mates by context) klasmakker m, makkerslemper m, schoolmakker m, speelmakker m, studiemakker m, wapenmakker m
  • makkerij
  • makkerschap
  • makkervreugd(e)
