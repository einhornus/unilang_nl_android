legging (60266)


Etymology 1

From leggen + | -ing.

Noun

legging f (plural leggingen, diminutive legginkje n)

  1. (chiefly in compounds) act of placing

Etymology 2

Borrowed from English legging.

Noun

legging m (plural leggings, diminutive legginkje n)

  1. legging (leg covering)
