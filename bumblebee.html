bumblebee (65713)


Etymology

1520s replacing Middle English humbul-be. Merged with Middle English bombeln (“to boom, buzz”), in the late 14th century.

Noun

bumblebee (plural bumblebees)

  1. Any of several species of large bee in the genus Bombus
    Synonym: dumbledore

Related terms

  • bee
  • Cullem's bumblebee
  • Franklin's bumblebee
  • great yellow bumblebee
  • large garden bumblebee
  • short-haired bumblebee
