boog (5325)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch bōge, from Old Dutch bogo, from Proto-Germanic *bugô.

Noun

boog m (plural bogen, diminutive boogje n)

  1. A bow (ranged weapon).
  2. (geometry) An arc.
  3. (architecture) An arch.
  4. (architecture) The arch of a foot.
    Synonym: voetboog
