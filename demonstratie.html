demonstratie (6136)


Etymology

Borrowed from Middle French demonstration. The sense “protest” derived from English demonstration.

Noun

demonstratie f (plural demonstraties, diminutive demonstratietje n)

  1. demonstration, demo (showing of something)
    Synonym: vertoning
  2. demonstration, exposition (explaining of something)
    Synonym: betoog
  3. demonstration (group protest)
    Synonyms: betoging, groepsprotest, protest

Derived terms

  • massademonstratie

Related terms

  • demonstreren
