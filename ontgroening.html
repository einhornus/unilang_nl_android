ontgroening (25230)


Etymology

From ontgroenen + | -ing.

Noun

ontgroening f (plural ontgroeningen, diminutive ontgroeninkje n)

  1. the relative decline of younger age cohorts in a society due to low birth numbers and/or emigration of young people
  2. (Netherlands) hazing

Synonyms

  • (hazing): doop, studentendoop

Related terms

  • (ageing populace): vergrijzing
