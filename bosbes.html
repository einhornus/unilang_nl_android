bosbes (54920)


Etymology

From bos (“forest”) + | bes (“berry”).

Noun

bosbes f (plural bosbessen, diminutive bosbesje n)

  1. bilberry
  2. bilberry plant, member of the genus Vaccinium

Zelfstandig naamwoord

bosbes v/m
