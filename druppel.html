druppel (5303)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch droppel. Equivalent to drop or drup + | -el.

Noun

druppel m (plural druppels, diminutive druppeltje n)

  1. drop, dribble, droplet

Etymology 2

See the etymology of the main entry.

Verb

druppel

  1. first-person singular present indicative of druppelen
  2. imperative of druppelen
