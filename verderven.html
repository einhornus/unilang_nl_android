verderven (72150)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch verderven. Synchronically, it is equivalent to ver- + | derven.

Verb

verderven

  1. (transitive) to spoil, to ruin

Inflection

Related terms

  • verderf

Werkwoord

verderven
