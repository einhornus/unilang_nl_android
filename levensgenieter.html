levensgenieter (69044)


Etymology

From leven + | genieten + | -er.

Noun

levensgenieter m (plural levensgenieters, diminutive levensgenieterje n)

  1. One who enjoys the good things in life (e.g. food, drink, etc.)

Synonyms

  • bon vivant
