bannen (26793)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch bannen, from Old Dutch *bannan, from Proto-Germanic *bannanÄ…. The computational sense is borrowed from, or at least strongly influenced by, English ban.

Verb

bannen

  1. (transitive) to expel, drive off
  2. (transitive, computing) to ban (an IP address)
Inflection
