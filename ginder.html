ginder (8280)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch ginder, from Proto-Germanic *jainar (“over there”). Cognate with English yonder.

Adverb

ginder

  1. yonder; there; used to indicate something that is not here

Synonyms

  • ginds
  • daar
  • daarginds
  • daarginder

Related terms

  • hier
  • er
