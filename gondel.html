gondel (20793)


Etymology

Borrowed from Italian gondola, from Venetian góndoła, which likely derives from Byzantine Greek κοντούρα (kontoúra, “small tail”).

Noun

gondel f (plural gondels, diminutive gondeltje n)

  1. A gondola (Venetian boat).
  2. A gondola of a cable car.
  3. The gondola of a balloon.
  4. A decorated boat used in boat parades.

Derived terms

  • gondelen
  • gondelvaart
