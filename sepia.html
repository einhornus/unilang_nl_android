sepia (88260)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch sepia, via French or Italian from Latin sepia.

Noun

sepia f or m (uncountable)

  1. cuttlefish

Synonyms

  • (cuttlefish): zeekat

Noun

sepia n (uncountable)

  1. the color sepia
  2. a style of yellowish/brownish-and-black photography
