boemel (38489)


Etymology 1

From boemeltrein.

Noun

boemel m (plural boemels, diminutive boemeltje n)

  1. Short for boemeltrein.
  2. another mode of transportation (notably a bus) with many stops

Etymology 2

From boemelen.

Noun

boemel m (plural boemels, diminutive boemeltje n)

  1. (used absolutely, with definite article: de boemel) loitering; partying
