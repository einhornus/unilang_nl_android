opstoot (59737)


Etymology

From opstoten. Equivalent to op + | stoot.

Noun

opstoot m (plural opstoten, diminutive opstootje n)

  1. (chiefly diminutive) uproar, riot, brawl (unruly gathering of people with altercations)
  2. (Belgium) outbreak, outburst (rapid development of a disease or symptoms)
  3. (boxing, martial arts) uppercut
    Synonym: uppercut
