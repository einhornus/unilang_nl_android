gauwdief (93327)


Etymology

gauw (“fast”) + | dief (“thief”)

Noun

gauwdief m (plural gauwdieven, diminutive gauwdiefje n)

  1. A fast-fingered, crafty thief
  2. (by extension) A clever crook, hustler

Synonyms

  • (crook) bedrieger m, oplichter m

Derived terms

  • gauwdieverij
