karavaan (13733)


Etymology

Borrowing from French caravane, replacing forms derived from Middle Dutch carvane.

Noun

karavaan f (plural karavanen, diminutive karavaantje n)

  1. caravan (a convoy or procession of travelers, their vehicles and cargo, and any pack animals, especially camels crossing a desert)

Related terms

  • caravan
