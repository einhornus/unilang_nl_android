attractie (14470)


Etymology

Borrowed from Middle French attraction, from Old French attraction, from Latin attractio from past participle of attrahō (= ad + trahō)

Noun

attractie f (plural attracties, diminutive attractietje n)

  1. attraction

Related terms

  • attractief
