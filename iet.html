iet (25327)


Etymology

From earlier iewet, from Old Dutch *iowiht, from ne lt;lt; Proto-Germanic *ne + io (“ever”) + *wiht lt;lt; Proto-Germanic *wihtą (“thing”).

Pronoun

iet

  1. something, anything

Adverb

iet

  1. to any degree, in any way, at all
  2. a little, somewhat
  3. sometimes, perhaps
