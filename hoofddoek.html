hoofddoek (22229)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch hovetdoek. Equivalent to hoofd (“head”) + | doek (“cloth”).

Noun

hoofddoek m (plural hoofddoeken, diminutive hoofddoekje n)

  1. headscarf, a more or less rectangular cloth worn over the head, mainly by women, now usually by Muslimas

Zelfstandig naamwoord
