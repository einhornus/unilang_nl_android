rolmops (73191)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Rollmops.

Noun

rolmops m (plural rolmopsen, diminutive rolmopsje n)

  1. rollmop, rollmops (rolled herring, often with a filling of pickles and onions)
  2. (derogatory) an obese human or animal
