zadelen (28940)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch sādelen. Equivalent to zadel (“saddle”) + | -en.

Verb

zadelen

  1. (transitive) To saddle, to put a saddle on (a mount)

Derived terms

  • doorzadelen
  • omzadelen
  • opzadelen

Related terms

  • zadeldier n
