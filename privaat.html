privaat (34770)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch privaet (“private; privy”), from Latin privātus (“private”) and privāta (“privy, outhouse”).

Adjective

privaat (not comparable)

  1. private, personal

Inflection

Noun

privaat n (plural privaten)

  1. A privy, an outhouse.
    Synonyms: kakhuis, schijthuis, secreet
  2. (by extension) A toilet.
    Synonyms: toilet, wc
