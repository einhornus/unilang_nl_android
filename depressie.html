Noun

depressie f (plural depressies, diminutive depressietje n)

  1. A physical depression
  2. A psychologically 'low' state of mind, producing serious long-term lowering of enjoyment
  3. A low physical pressure
  4. A meteorological depression, (area of) low air pressure
  5. A long low in the economic situation
  6. A downward pressure, position or movement

Synonyms

  • inzinking
  • (psychological) depressiviteit

Related terms

  • depressief
  • deprimeren
