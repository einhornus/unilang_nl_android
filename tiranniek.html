tiranniek (78958)


Adjective

tiranniek (comparative tirannieker, superlative tiranniekst)

  1. tyrannical, (as) under a tyranny
    Een tiranniek bewind steunt op de afschrikkingskracht van zijn repressie
    A tyrannical regime deponds on the deterrence of its repression
  2. tyrannous, with the (notably abusive) characteristics of a tyrant

Inflection

Synonyms

  • despotisch

Related terms

  • tirannie
