lef (2074)


Etymology

Borrowed from Yiddish לבֿ | (leyv, “courage”), which is borrowed from Hebrew לב | (lev, “heart”).

Noun

lef n or m (uncountable)

  1. courage, bravery, daring
  2. temerity, rashness
  3. arrogance

Related terms

  • lefdoekje
  • lefgozer m

Zelfstandig naamwoord

lef o of m

  1. (Jiddisch-Hebreeuws) durf, branie, moed
