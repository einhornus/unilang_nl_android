armenhuis (25911)


Etymology

Compound of arme (“poor person”) + | -en- + | huis (“house”). First attested in the 18th century in this form, but attested since the 17th century in a different form.

Noun

armenhuis n (plural armenhuizen, diminutive armenhuisje n)

  1. poorhouse

Zelfstandig naamwoord

armenhuis o | 1 |
