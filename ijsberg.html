ijsberg (12092)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch ijsberch, equivalent to ijs (“ice”) + | berg (“mountain”).

Noun

Ijsberg bij Groenland
Iceberg next to Greenland

ijsberg m (plural ijsbergen, diminutive ijsbergje n)

  1. iceberg (mountain-size mass of floating ice)

Related terms

  • bergijs

Zelfstandig naamwoord
