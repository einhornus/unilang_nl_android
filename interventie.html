interventie (18424)


Etymology

From Latin interventiō, from interveniō (“I intervene, come between”). From interveniëren + | -tie

Noun

interventie f (plural interventies, diminutive interventietje n)

  1. Intervention.
    De Vn konden geen akkoord bereiken over militaire interventie.
    The Un could not reach an agreement about military intervention.

Synonyms

  • ingreep
  • tussenkomst

Related terms

  • tussenbeide
