bedriegster (26787)


Etymology

From bedriegen (“to cheat”) + | -ster.

Noun

bedriegster m (plural bedriegsters, diminutive bedriegstertje n, masculine bedrieger)

  1. female deceiver, cheater, fraudster
  2. female crook, hustler, impostor

Synonyms

  • (deceiver) misleidster
  • (cheater) valsspeelster
  • (fraudster; crook) oplichtster

Related terms

  • bedrieglijk
  • bedrog
