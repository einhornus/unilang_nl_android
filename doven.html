doven (12940)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch dôven, from Old Dutch *dōven, from Proto-Germanic *daubijaną.

Verb

doven

  1. (ergative) to extinguish
Inflection

Etymology 2

See the etymology of the main entry.

Noun

doven

Werkwoord
