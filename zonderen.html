zonderen (50168)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch sonderen, from Old Dutch *sundron, from Proto-Germanic *sundrōną.

Verb

zonderen

  1. (transitive, obsolete) to separate, to set apart

Derived terms

  • afzonderen
  • uitzonderen
  • zonderling

Werkwoord

zonderen | 1 |
