chauffeur (2036)


Etymology

Borrowed from French chauffeur.

Noun

chauffeur m (plural chauffeurs, diminutive chauffeurtje n, feminine chauffeuse)

  1. driver (person who drives a motorized vehicle, such as a car or a bus)
