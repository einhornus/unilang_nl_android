harpij (116955)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch harpie, arpie, from Old French harpie, from Latin harpyia, from Ancient Greek ἅρπυια (hárpuia).

Noun

harpij f (plural harpijen)

  1. (Greek mythology) harpy (hybrid of a woman and a bird of prey)
  2. harpy eagle, Harpia harpyja
    Synonym: harpijarend
  3. (derogatory) harpy, harridan (malicious or unpleasant woman)

Derived terms

  • harpijarend
