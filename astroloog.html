astroloog (36343)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Astrologe, from Latin astrologus, from Ancient Greek ᾰ̓στρολόγος (astrológos). Equivalent to astro- + | -loog.

Noun

astroloog m (plural astrologen, diminutive astroloogje n)

  1. astrologer [from early 19th c.]

Synonyms

Related terms
