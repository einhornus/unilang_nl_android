zepen (62156)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch sepen. Equivalent to zeep + | -en.

Verb

zepen

  1. (transitive) to soap (to cover with soap or apply soup to)
    De wasvrouw zeepte haar was.
    The washerwoman applied soap to her laundry.
    Synonym: inzepen
