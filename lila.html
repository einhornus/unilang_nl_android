lila (7818)


Etymology 1

From French lilas, from lilacs, the plural of lilac.

Noun

lila n (plural lila's, diminutive lilaatje n)

  1. lilac (color)

Adjective

lila (invariable, comparative meer lila, superlative meest lila)

  1. lilac
Inflection

Unknown, possibly oriental
