damasten (132247)


Etymology

From French damas (“damask”), from Damas 'Damascus', the Syrian city damask is supposed to originate from

Noun

damasten

  1. Plural form of damast

Adjective

damasten (not comparable)

  1. made of damask

Related terms

  • damasceren (verb)
  • damassé (adjective)
  • damastachtig (adjective)
  • gedamast (adjective)
