ijzervreter (51643)


Etymology

From ijzer (“iron”) + | vreter (“devourer”).

Noun

ijzervreter m (plural ijzervreters, diminutive ijzervretertje n, feminine ijzervreetster)

  1. A stout-hearted warrior, a miles gloriosus.
  2. (by extension) A tough, ruthless or relentless combative person, such as a hawk or a hard-ass.

Zelfstandig naamwoord

ijzervreter m

  1. vuurvreter, gehard militair
  2. (schertsend) doorzetter.
Synoniemen
  • vechtersbaas
