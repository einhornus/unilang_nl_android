parkiet (26079)


Etymology

Borrowed from French perroquet, Spanish periquito or Portuguese periquito.

Noun

parkiet m (plural parkieten, diminutive parkietje n)

  1. A parakeet, a budgie; a member of various species of small parrots (not forming a monophyletic clade).

Zelfstandig naamwoord

parkiet m

  1. (vogels) kleine papegaaiachtige vogel
