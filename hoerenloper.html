hoerenloper (39440)


Etymology

Compound of hoer (“whore, prostitute”) + | -en- + | loper.

Noun

hoerenloper m (plural hoerenlopers, diminutive hoerenlopertje n)

  1. jockey, john (client of a prostitute)

Synonyms

  • hoerenhengst
  • venusjanker
