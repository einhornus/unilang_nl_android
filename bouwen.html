bouwen (1514)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch bouwen, from Old Dutch buwan, from Proto-Germanic *būaną.

Verb

bouwen

  1. (transitive) to build, to construct
  2. (transitive, dialectal) to cultivate, to till
    Synonym: verbouwen
