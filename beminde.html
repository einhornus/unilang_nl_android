beminde (17533)


Etymology 1

From bemind.

Noun

beminde m (plural beminden, diminutive bemindetje n)

  1. loved one
    Synonym: geliefde

Participle

beminde

  1. Inflected form of bemind

Etymology 2

See the etymology of the main entry.

Verb

beminde

  1. singular past indicative and subjunctive of beminnen

Werkwoord

beminde
