begunstigde (17245)


Etymology 1

From begunstigd.

Noun

begunstigde m or f (plural begunstigden)

  1. beneficiary

Etymology 2

See the etymology of the main entry.

Verb

begunstigde

  1. singular past indicative and subjunctive of begunstigen
