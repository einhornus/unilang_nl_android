hoornblazer (60667)


Etymology

Compound of hoorn (“horn”) + | blazer (“blower”).

Noun

hoornblazer m (plural hoornblazers, diminutive hoornblazertje n)

  1. (literally) A hornblower, who uses a signalhorn
  2. A musician who plays a horn

Synonyms

  • (musician) hoornist

Related terms

  • Engelse hoorn m
  • hoornspel n
  • blaasensemble n
  • houtblazer m
  • koperblazer m
