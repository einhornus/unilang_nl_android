lemming (103798)


Etymology

From Danish and Norwegian lemming, from Old Norse lómundr, læmingi, læmingr (“lemming”), perhaps from Sami luomek.

Noun

lemming m (plural lemmingen or lemmings, diminutive lemminkje n)

  1. lemming (rodent)
