regendruppel (59461)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch regendrup, Middle Dutch regendrop, from Old Dutch *regandropo, from Proto-Germanic *regnadrupô. Equivalent to regen (“rain”) + | druppel (“drop, droplet”).

Noun

regendruppel m (plural regendruppels, diminutive regendruppeltje n)

  1. A raindrop, drop(let) of rainwater

Related terms

  • regenwater
  • druppelen, druppen
  • regenen
  • waterdruppel

Zelfstandig naamwoord
