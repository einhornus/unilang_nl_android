snorhaar (62957)


Etymology

Compound of snor + | haar.

Noun

snorhaar n (plural snorharen, diminutive snorhaartje n)

  1. (countable) A whisker of certain non-human mammals, such as those of cats and rodents.
  2. (countable) A moustache hair; (uncountable) moustache hair.
