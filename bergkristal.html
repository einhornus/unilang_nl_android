bergkristal (109125)


Etymology

Compound of berg + | kristal.

Noun

bergkristal n (plural bergkristallen, diminutive bergkristalletje n)

  1. rock crystal (clear, colourless form of quartz)

Zelfstandig naamwoord

bergkristal o | 1 |
