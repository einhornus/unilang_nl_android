doorprikken (76332)


Etymology 1

From door + | prikken.

Verb

doorprikken

  1. (transitive) to puncture
  2. (transitive) to expose as false
Inflection

Etymology 2

From door- + | prikken.

Verb

doorprikken

  1. (transitive) to puncture through completely
  2. (transitive) to put numerous punctures into
Inflection
