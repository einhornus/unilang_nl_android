zelfzeker (55348)


Etymology

From zelf- (“self-”) + | zeker (“certain”), calque of German selbstsicher (“confident”).

Adjective

zelfzeker (comparative zelfzekerder, superlative zelfzekerst)

  1. (Belgium) confident

Inflection
