onbenul (16505)


Etymology

From on- + | benul.

Noun

onbenul n (uncountable)

  1. complete lack of understanding, insight or awareness

Noun

onbenul m or f (plural onbenullen)

  1. someome who shows a complete lack of understanding, insight or awareness

Zelfstandig naamwoord

onbenul | 2 |
