taalgevoel (48373)


Etymology

Compound of taal + | gevoel.

Noun

taalgevoel n (plural taalgevoelens)

  1. (uncountable) sense for language, Sprachgefühl (intuitive notion of grammaticality)
  2. (chiefly in the plural) sentiment with respect to language

Zelfstandig naamwoord

taalgevoel o
