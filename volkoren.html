volkoren (33687)


Etymology

Apocope of volkorenbrood and volkorenmeel, perhaps influenced by German Vollkorn.

Adjective

volkoren (not comparable)

  1. Wholemeal. [from ca. 1930s]

Inflection

Noun

volkoren n (uncountable)

  1. Wholemeal products. [from late 20th c.]

Bijvoeglijk naamwoord

volkoren
