drenkeling (38372)


Etymology

From drenken + | -ling.

Noun

drenkeling m (plural drenkelingen, diminutive drenkelingetje n)

  1. someone who has fallen into water, drowning person

Zelfstandig naamwoord

drenkeling m

  1. iemand die te water geraakt en dreigt te verdrinken
