stoeprand (25586)


Etymology

Compound of stoep (“pavement, sidewalk”) + | rand (“rim, edge”).

Noun

stoeprand m (plural stoepranden, diminutive stoeprandje n)

  1. A kerb, the edge of a pavement.

Zelfstandig naamwoord

stoeprand m
