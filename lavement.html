lavement (51426)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch lavament. Borrowed from Old French or Latin. Ultimately from Latin lavare.

Noun

lavement n (plural lavementen, diminutive lavementje n)

  1. washing, wash
  2. lavement, enema, clyster

Synonyms

  • klysma

Derived terms

  • lavementspuit

Zelfstandig naamwoord

lavement o | 3 |

Synoniemen
