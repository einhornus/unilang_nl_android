naast (844)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch naest, from Old Dutch *nāist, *nāst, from Proto-Germanic *nēhwist (“nearest, closest”).

Preposition

naast

  1. beside, next to
  2. in addition to
Inflection
Pronominal adverbs of naast

Etymology 2

See the etymology of the main entry.

Verb

naast

  1. first-, second- and third-person singular present indicative of naasten
  2. imperative of naasten
