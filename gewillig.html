gewillig (21352)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch gewillich, ghewillich, ghewillech, from gewille + -ich.

Adjective

gewillig (comparative gewilliger, superlative gewilligst)

  1. voluntary, showing readiness, willing
  2. compliant, yielding

Derived terms

  • gewilligheid

Adverb

gewillig

  1. compliantly, pliantly
  2. readily, willingly

Bijvoeglijk naamwoord

gewillig | 2 |

  1. bereidwillig, gehoorzaam
Synoniemen
  • goedwillig, goedgezind, welgezind, welwillend, volgzaam, gedwee
