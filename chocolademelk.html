chocolademelk (10197)


Etymology

Compound of chocolade + | melk.

Noun

chocolademelk m (uncountable)

  1. chocolate milk (milk flavored with chocolate and served cold or hot)
    Synonym: cacaomelk

Zelfstandig naamwoord

chocolademelk v/m

  1. (drinken) drank gemaakt van cacao, suiker en melk
Synoniemen
  • cacaomelk, chocomel
Verwante begrippen
