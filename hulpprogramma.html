hulpprogramma (105207)


Etymology

Compound of hulp + | programma.

Noun

hulpprogramma n (plural hulpprogramma's)

  1. (computing) A software utility, a program aiding in a specific task.
  2. An aid programme.
    De bijdragende landen gebruiken het hulpprogramma enkel om hun eigen banken te steunen.
    The contributing countries only used the aid programme to support their own banks.
