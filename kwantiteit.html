kwantiteit (29230)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch quantiteit, from Middle French quantité, from Old French quantite, from Latin quantitās.

Noun

kwantiteit f (uncountable)

  1. quantity, amount
    Synonyms: aantal, hoeveelheid

Related terms
