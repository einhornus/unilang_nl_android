aars (26817)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch āers, from Old Dutch *ars, from Proto-Germanic *arsaz, from Proto-Indo-European *h₃érsos.

Noun

aars m (plural aarzen, diminutive aarsje n)

  1. anus, arse(hole), rectum
    Synonym: anus
  2. (rare) butt
  3. (vulgar) shit
