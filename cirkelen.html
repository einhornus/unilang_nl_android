cirkelen (27741)


Etymology

From cirkel + | -en.

Verb

cirkelen

  1. (intransitive) to form a circle, turn/move/travel in a circle
  2. (transitive) to mark with a circle

Synonyms

(move in a circle):

  • circuleren
  • ronddraaien

(mark):

  • omcirkelen

Derived terms

  • omcirkelen

Related terms

  • circulair
