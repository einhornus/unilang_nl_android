vastbijten (86869)


Etymology

Compound of vast + | bijten.

Verb

vastbijten

  1. (reflexive, intransitive) to bite deep [+ in (into)]
  2. (reflexive) to dig in [+ in (inanimate)], to continue with resilience [+ in (object)], to carry on resiliently [+ in (with)]

Inflection

Werkwoord

vastbijten | 1 |

  1. vastberaden doorzetten
  2. wederkerend zich ~: zich door bijten vasthechten
