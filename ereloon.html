ereloon (93704)


Etymology

Of Germanic origin. eer (“honor”) + | loon (“wage”).

Noun

ereloon n (plural erelonen, diminutive ereloontje n)

  1. A honorarium, as customary for the services of doctors, notaries etc.

Synonyms

  • honorarium n

Related terms

  • eerbewijs n
  • ereteken n
  • loontrekker m
  • weekloon n
