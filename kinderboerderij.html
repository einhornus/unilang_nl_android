kinderboerderij (39344)


Etymology

From kind (“child”) + | -er- + | boerderij (“farm”).

Noun

kinderboerderij f (plural kinderboerderijen, diminutive kinderboerderijtje n)

  1. petting zoo (educational and recreational facility for familiarising children with (chiefly) farm animals)
