echtpaar (14153)


Etymology

Compound of echt (“marriage”) + | paar (“couple”).

Noun

echtpaar n (plural echtparen, diminutive echtpaartje n)

  1. A married couple. [from 17th c.]

Related terms

  • echtgenoot
  • echtgenote
