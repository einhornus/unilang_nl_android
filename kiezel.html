kiezel (82197)


Etymology

Cognate with Old English cisil.

Noun

kiezel m (plural kiezels, diminutive kiezeltje n)

  1. pebble, pebblestone

Noun

kiezel n (plural kiezels, diminutive kiezeltje n)

  1. flint, gravel [from 1631]
  2. silicon
    Synonym: silicium
