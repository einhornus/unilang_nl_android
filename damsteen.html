damsteen (132236)


Etymology 1

Compound of dammen + | steen.

Noun

damsteen m (plural damstenen, diminutive damsteentje n)

  1. draught, checker [from 18th c.]
    Synonyms: damschijf, damstuk

Etymology 2

Compound of dam + | steen.

Noun

damsteen m (plural damstenen, diminutive damsteentje n)

  1. (archaic) stone that closes a furnace or crucible, preventing the molten metal from flowing away [19th c.]
