doorgeefluik (76340)


Etymology

From doorgeven + | luik.

Noun

doorgeefluik n (plural doorgeefluiken, diminutive doorgeefluikje n)

  1. a hatch or a shutter in a wall, in particular between the dining room and the kitchen, that allows the cook to relay the food easily to the people in the dining room
    Synonym: dienluik
  2. (figurative) a medium, a broker
  3. (law) mere conduit

Synonyms

  • (medium): tussenpersoon
