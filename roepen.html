roepen (1831)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch roepen, from Old Dutch ruopan, from Proto-Germanic *hrōpaną.

Verb

roepen

  1. (transitive or intransitive) to cry, shout
  2. (transitive) to call, summon, invite etc.
Synonyms
  • (shout): brullen, bulderen, schreeuwen, tieren
