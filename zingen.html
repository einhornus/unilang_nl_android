zingen (1093)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch singen, from Old Dutch singan, from Proto-Germanic *singwaną, from Proto-Indo-European *sengʷʰ-.

Verb

zingen

  1. to sing
  2. (slang) to sing, to blab to the police, to confess under interrogation

Derived terms

  • bezingen

Related terms

  • zang
