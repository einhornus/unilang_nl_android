doodgraver (30607)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch dootgravere. Equivalent to dood + | graver.

Noun

doodgraver m (plural doodgravers, diminutive doodgravertje n)

  1. gravedigger
  2. sexton beetle, burying beetle; beetle of the genus Nicrophorus
  3. (derogatory, colloquial) funeral director
    Synonym: begrafenisondernemer

Zelfstandig naamwoord

doodgraver m
