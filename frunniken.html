frunniken (83468)


Etymology

Probably from punniken, with fr- as an expressive onset found also in frommelen, frutselen and fröbelen.

Verb

frunniken

  1. (intransitive) to fidget, fiddle

Inflection

Synonyms

  • peuteren
  • friemelen
