halfnaakt (18619)


Etymology

half- (“half”) + | nakt (“naked”).

Adjective

halfnaakt (comparative halfnaakter, superlative halfnaaktst)

  1. seminaked, largely nude, only partially dressed or otherwise covered

Inflection

Synonyms

  • halfbloot
  • schaarsgekleed

Related terms

  • helft
  • naaktloperij
