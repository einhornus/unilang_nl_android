meeuw (31082)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch mêwe, from Old Dutch *mēo, from Proto-West Germanic *maiw, from Proto-Germanic *maiwaz.

Noun

meeuw f (plural meeuwen, diminutive meeuwtje n)

  1. A seagull, seabird of the family Laridae.
