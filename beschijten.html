beschijten (109089)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch beschiten. Equivalent to be- + | schijten.

Verb

beschijten

  1. (transitive) to beshit, to shit on or in
  2. (transitive, archaic, dialectal) to deceive, to con

Inflection

Werkwoord
