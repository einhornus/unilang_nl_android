fluiter (83527)


Etymology

From the root of fluiten 'to flute, whistle' + -er

Noun

fluiter m (plural fluiters, diminutive fluitertje n)

  1. A fluter, flute player, flutist
  2. A whistler; whistleblower
  3. wood warbler (songbird species Phylloscopus sibilator)

Synonyms

  • (fluter) fluitist m, fluitspeler m

Derived terms

  • bergfluiter m
  • regenfluiter m
  • tuinfluiter m

Related terms

  • flierefluiter m
  • fluitketel m
  • gefluit n

Zelfstandig naamwoord

fluiter m | 1 |
