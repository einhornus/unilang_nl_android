stroming (10389)


Etymology

From stromen + | -ing

Noun

stroming f (plural stromingen, diminutive strominkje n)

  1. a stream
  2. a flow, current
    De stroming in de mantel veroorzaakt de bewegingen van de platen en vulkanisme aan het aardoppervlak. | 1 | | The flow in the mantle causes the movements of the plates and volcanism on the Earth's surface.
  3. a movement (e.g. cultural, social, or artistic)
  4. a school of thought

Related terms
