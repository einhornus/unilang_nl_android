veehouder (86850)


Etymology

From vee (“livestock”) + | houden (“to keep”) + | -er (denominative agent suffix).

Noun

veehouder m (plural veehouders, diminutive veehoudertje n, feminine veehouderes)

  1. (agriculture) someone who keeps livestock, a livestock farmer
