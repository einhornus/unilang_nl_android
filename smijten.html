smijten (14195)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch smiten, from Old Dutch *smītan, from Proto-Germanic *smītaną, from Proto-Indo-European *smeyd-. Compare Low German smieten, West Frisian smite, German schmeißen, English smite, Danish smide.

Verb

smijten

  1. to fling or hurl, to throw forcefully

Inflection

Werkwoord

smijten

  1. overgankelijk hard gooien of werpen
    • Hij smeet de bal ontzettend ver.
Afgeleide begrippen
  • gesmijt, smijter
