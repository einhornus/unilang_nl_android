dartel (42424)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch dertel, derten; further etymology unclear. Possibly from Proto-Indo-European *dʰerh₃-. This etymology is incomplete. You can help Wiktionary by elaborating on the origins of this term.

dartel (comparative darteler, superlative dartelst)

  1. frisky, frolic, playful, gamesome
