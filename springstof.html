springstof (19771)


Etymology

Compound of springen (“to explode”) + | stof (“matter, substance”), calque of German Sprengstoff.

Noun

springstof f (plural springstoffen, diminutive springstofje n)

  1. (countable, uncountable) explosive, explosive material

Zelfstandig naamwoord

springstof v/m | 3 |
