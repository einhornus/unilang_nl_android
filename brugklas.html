brugklas (27749)


Etymology

Compound of brug + | klas. First attested in the 1920s, although the current format became law in 1963 and went into force in 1968.

Noun

brugklas f (plural brugklassen, diminutive brugklasje n)

  1. (Netherlands) the first form or grade of secondary education
  2. (Netherlands) a school class of pupils at this level

Derived terms

  • brugger
  • brugklasser

Zelfstandig naamwoord
