reliek (88826)


Etymology

From Old French, from Latin reliquiae (“remains, relics”), from relinquō (“I leave behind, abandon, relinquish”), from re- + linquō (“I leave, quit, forsake, depart from”).

Noun

reliek f or n (plural relieken, diminutive reliekje n)

  1. relic

Synonyms

  • relikwie

Zelfstandig naamwoord

reliek v / o | 3 |

  1. relikwie
  2. overblijfsel, relict
