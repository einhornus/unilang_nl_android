kerks (104481)


Etymology

From kerk + | -s.

Adjective

kerks (comparative kerkser, superlative meest kerks or kerkst)

  1. (Christianity) churched, church-going
    Zij is kerkser dan haar vrouw.
    She is a more frequent churchgoer than her wife.

Derived terms

  • kerksheid
