kanjer (5520)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch keniart, canjaert, kangart, kenjard, from Old French cagnard.

Noun

kanjer m (plural kanjers, diminutive kanjertje n)

  1. Something or someone who is big or the biggest of their kind; a whopper
    Synonym: joekel
  2. One who is admirable; a hero, a star.
