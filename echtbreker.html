echtbreker (93825)


Etymology

From echt (“marriage”) + | breken (“to break”) + | -er.

Noun

echtbreker m (plural echtbrekers, diminutive echtbrekertje n, feminine echtbreekster)

  1. An adulterer, who breaks his (or her) marital bond
  2. A homewrecker

Synonyms

  • overspelige m or f

Related terms

  • echtbreuk
  • echtgenoot m
  • ijsbreker m
