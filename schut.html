schut (56051)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch schut. This etymology is incomplete. You can help Wiktionary by elaborating on the origins of this term.

Noun

schut n (plural schutten, diminutive schutje n)

  1. A dam or lock gate.
  2. A light movable partition or wall, such as a folding screen.

Zelfstandig naamwoord

schut o
