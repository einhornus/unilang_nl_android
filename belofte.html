belofte (2545)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch belofte. Equivalent to beloven + | -te.

Noun

belofte f (plural beloftes or beloften, diminutive beloftetje n)

  1. promise, vow, pledge

Synonyms

  • toezegging

Derived terms

  • belofte maakt schuld
  • belofteploeg
