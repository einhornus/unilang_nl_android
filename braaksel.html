braaksel (15197)


Etymology

From braken (“to vomit”) + | -sel (“suffix indicating product of an action”).

Noun

braaksel n (uncountable)

  1. vomit (that which one has vomited out)

Synonyms

  • kots

Zelfstandig naamwoord

braaksel | 1 |
