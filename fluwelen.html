fluwelen (18064)


Etymology 1

From fluweel + | -en.

Adjective

fluwelen (not comparable)

  1. (literally) velveteen, made of velvet
  2. (figuratively) velvety, soft and pleasant like velvet
Synonyms
  • (figurative): fluwelig
Related terms
  • fluweelzacht
