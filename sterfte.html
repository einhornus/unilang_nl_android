sterfte (53154)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch sterfte, steerfte. Equivalent to sterven + | -te.

Noun

sterfte f (plural sterftes or sterften)

  1. death, dying
  2. mortality
  3. (now chiefly in compounds) mass death

Derived terms

  • bijensterfte
  • kindersterfte
  • kraamsterfte
  • oversterfte
  • veesterfte
  • vissterfte
