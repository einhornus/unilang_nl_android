computerspel (47613)


Etymology

From computer + | spel.

Noun

computerspel n (plural computerspellen, diminutive computerspelletje n)

  1. A computer game, an electronic game designed to be run on a computer.

Synonyms

  • computergame

Related terms

  • boordcomputer
  • computerprogramma
  • spelconsole
