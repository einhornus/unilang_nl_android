abonnement (21155)


Etymology

Borrowed from French abonnement.

Noun

abonnement n (plural abonnementen, diminutive abonnementje n)

  1. subscription
  2. season ticket, especially for public transport (train, bus, etc...)

Related terms

  • abonneren
