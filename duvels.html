duvels (131546)


Etymology 1

Noun

duvels

  1. Plural form of duvel

Etymology 2

Variant of duivels, equivalent to duvel + | -s.

Adjective

duvels

  1. devilish
  2. wretched, damned
  3. angry

Adverb

duvels

  1. to a great extent, really
    Ik was toen echt even duvels kwaad.
    I was really angry at the time.

Interjection

duvels

  1. an exclamation of surprise
    Alle duvels!
