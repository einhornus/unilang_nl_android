vrijkorps (78252)


Etymology

First attested around 1783. From vrij + | korps, probably a calque of German Freikorps, attested a few years earlier.

Noun

vrijkorps n (plural vrijkorpsen, diminutive vrijkorpsje n)

  1. paramilitary group
