hela (27131)


Etymology 1

From Proto-Germanic *hanhilaz. Cognate with Middle Dutch hiele (Dutch hiel), Old Frisian hēla, Old Norse hæll (Danish hæl, Swedish häl).

Noun

hēla m (nominative plural hēlan)

  1. heel (of the foot)

Etymology 2

Variant of ēalā.

Interjection

hēlā

  1. Alternative form of ēalā
