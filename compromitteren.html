compromitteren (54794)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch compromitteren, from Latin comprōmittō.

Verb

compromitteren

  1. (transitive) to compromise
    Het zal de zaak niet compromitteren.
    It will not compromise the case.

Inflection

Related terms

  • compromise

Werkwoord

compromitteren | 3 |

  1. overgankelijk verdacht maken, blameren, in opspraak brengen
