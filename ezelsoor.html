ezelsoor (106962)


Etymology

From ezel (“donkey, ass”) + | -s- (genitive interfix) + | oor (“ear; handle”). The sense “dog-ear” is a calque of German Eselsohr.

Noun

ezelsoor n (plural ezelsoren, diminutive ezelsoortje n)

  1. donkey's ear
  2. dog-ear (on a page)
