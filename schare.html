schare (63114)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch schare, from Old Dutch skara, from Proto-Germanic *skar≈ç. Cognates include German Schar, English share.

Noun

schare f (plural scharen, diminutive scharetje n)

  1. (formal, poetic) crowd

Verb

schare
