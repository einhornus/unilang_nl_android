fuifnummer (70058)


Etymology

Compound of fuif (“party”) + | nummer (“act; track, song; number, character”).

Noun

fuifnummer n (plural fuifnummers, diminutive fuifnummertje n)

  1. party animal

Synonyms

  • fuifbeest
