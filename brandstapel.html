brandstapel (14267)


Etymology

From branden + | stapel.

Noun

brandstapel m (plural brandstapels, diminutive brandstapeltje n)

  1. pile used for burning at the stake
    Synonym: mutsaard
  2. (metonymically) burning at the stake as a method of execution

Zelfstandig naamwoord
