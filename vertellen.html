vertellen (248)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch vertellen. Equivalent to ver- + | tellen.

Verb

vertellen

  1. (transitive) to tell
    Ik heb het aan hem verteld. / Ik vertelde hem.
    I have told him. / I told him.
