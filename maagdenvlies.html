maagdenvlies (34951)


Etymology

Dutch Wikipedia has an article on:
maagdenvlies
Wikipedia nl

Compound of maagd (“virgin”) + | -en- + | vlies (“fleece, membrane”).

Noun

maagdenvlies n (plural maagdenvliezen, diminutive maagdenvliesje n)

  1. The hymen, a membrane which occludes the vagina and is in traditional thinking supposed to remain intact only in virgin females.

Related terms

  • maagdelijkheid
  • ontmaagden
