scheen (10854)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch schene, from Old Dutch *skina, from Proto-Germanic *skin≈ç. Cognate with German Schiene, English shin.

Noun

scheen f (plural schenen, diminutive scheentje n)

  1. (chiefly plural) shin
  2. strip of wood or metal
