rikketik (50838)


Etymology

Onomatopoeic.

Interjection

rikketik

  1. Onomatopoeia for a ticking sound.

Noun

rikketik m (plural rikketikken)

  1. (informal or childish, Netherlands) heart
    Synonym: hart
