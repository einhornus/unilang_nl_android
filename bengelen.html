bengelen (58065)


Etymology

From bengel + | -en.

Verb

bengelen

  1. (intransitive) to dangle, swing around loosely
  2. (transitive, obsolete) to bother, to tease
  3. (transitive, obsolete) to ring (a bell)

Inflection

Werkwoord
