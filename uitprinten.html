uitprinten (42900)


Etymology

Compound of uit + | printen.

Verb

uitprinten

  1. (transitive, Netherlands) to print (out)

Usage notes

Sometimes considered incorrect when believed to be a contamination of printen and uitdraaien. Not officially proscribed, however.
