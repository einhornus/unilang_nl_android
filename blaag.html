blaag (54947)


Etymology

Compare western German colloquial or dialectal Blag (“kid”).

Noun

blaag m (plural blagen, diminutive blaagje n)

  1. child, kid, only said of minors, in this sense usually in the plural
    Synonyms: kind, telg
  2. rascal, brat
    Synonyms: deugniet, kwajongen, rakker

Derived terms

  • blagerig
