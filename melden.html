melden (2220)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch melden, from Old Dutch *meldon (attested in maltho (“I declare”)), from Proto-Germanic *meldōną.

Verb

melden

  1. (transitive) to report, communicate; let know

Synonyms

  • meedelen

Derived terms

  • melding
  • aanmelden
  • afmelden
  • vermelden

Werkwoord
