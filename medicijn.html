medicijn (3479)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch medicine, from Latin medicÄ«na.

Noun

medicijn n (plural medicijnen, diminutive medicijntje n)

  1. medicine, cure, medication (substance)
    Er is nog geen goed medicijn tegen die ziekte.
    At the moment there is no good medicine yet against that disease.
    Ik ben vergeten mijn medicijn in te nemen.
    I have forgotten to take my medication.
