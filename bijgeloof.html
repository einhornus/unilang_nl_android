bijgeloof (9691)


Etymology

From bij + | geloof. Compare German Aberglauben.

Noun

bijgeloof n (plural bijgeloven, diminutive bijgeloofje n)

  1. superstition (a set of unscientific beliefs that future events may be influenced by one's behaviour in some magical or mystical way)
