brengen (399)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch brengen, from Old Dutch bringan, from Proto-Germanic *bringaną, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰrenk-.

Verb

brengen

  1. (transitive) to bring, to carry
  2. (transitive) to deliver, to supply
