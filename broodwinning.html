broodwinning (31334)


Etymology

From brood (“bread”) + | winning (“acquisition, gain”). Compare English breadwinner (“household provider”).

Noun

broodwinning f (plural broodwinningen, diminutive broodwinninkje n)

  1. livelihood [from early 18th c.]

Related terms

  • broodwinner
