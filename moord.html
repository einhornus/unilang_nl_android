moord (603)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch mort, from Old Dutch morth, from Proto-Germanic *murþą, from Proto-Indo-European *mer- (“to die”).

Noun

moord m (plural moorden, diminutive moordje n)

  1. murder
Related terms
  • vermoorden
  • moorden
  • moordenaar
  • moordenares
