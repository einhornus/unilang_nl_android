ontwerp (5143)


Etymology 1

From ontwerpen. Compare German Entwurf.

Noun

ontwerp n (plural ontwerpen, diminutive ontwerpje n)

  1. design
    Synonym: design

Etymology 2

See the etymology of the main entry.

Verb

ontwerp

  1. first-person singular present indicative of ontwerpen
  2. imperative of ontwerpen

Zelfstandig naamwoord
