dikzak (9915)


Etymology

Equivalent to dik (“thick, fat”) + | zak (“sack, bag”).

Noun

dikzak m (plural dikzakken, diminutive dikzakje n)

  1. (derogatory) fatass

Synonyms

  • bolle
  • dikkerd
  • pafzak
  • papzak
  • vetzak

Zelfstandig naamwoord
