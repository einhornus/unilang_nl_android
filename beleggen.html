beleggen (21414)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch beleggen. Equivalent to be- + | leggen.

Verb

beleggen

  1. (transitive) to cover, smear, butter, spread over (e.g. a piece of bread with butter and jam)
  2. (transitive, finance) to invest money
  3. (transitive) to convoke, to call a meeting
  4. (transitive) to belay, to fasten (a rope), to fasten (with rope)
  5. (transitive) to cover with
  6. (transitive, obsolete) to plan, to devise

Derived terms

  • beleg
  • belegger
  • belegging
