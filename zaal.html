zaal (3427)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch sale, from Old Dutch sala, from Proto-Germanic *saliz.

Noun

zaal f (plural zalen, diminutive zaaltje n)

  1. room, hall
  2. ward in a hospital or similar medical or caregiving institute
  3. (historical) Germanic single-room home
Synonyms
  • ruimte
  • kamer
Related terms
  • salet
  • salon

zaal n (plural zaals)
