slokdarm (28487)


Etymology

From slokken (“to swallow”) + | darm (“gut, instestine”)

Noun

slokdarm m (plural slokdarmen, diminutive slokdarmpje n)

  1. gullet, oesophagus

Synonyms

  • oesophagus

Derived terms

  • slokdarmspraak m or f

Related terms

  • dikke darm
  • endeldarm
