heikneuter (69677)


Etymology

Compound of hei + | kneuter. The sense “bumpkin” may be due to association with unrelated keuter, keuterboer.

Noun

heikneuter m (plural heikneuters)

  1. common linnet, Linaria cannabina
    Synonyms: kneu, kneuter
  2. (derogatory) bumpkin, yokel, hick (unsophisticated rural person)
    Synonym: boer

Zelfstandig naamwoord

heikneuter m

  1. (scheldwoord) lomperd
  2. (vogels) Carduelis cannabina , een vinkachtige zangvogel, kneu
