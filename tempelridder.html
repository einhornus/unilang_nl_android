tempelridder (98909)


Etymology

Compound of tempel (“temple”) + | ridder (“knight”). Tempel here refers to the Temple Mount in Jerusalem.

Noun

tempelridder m (plural tempelridders, diminutive tempelriddertje n)

  1. A Knight Templar, knightly member of the Templar order

Synonyms

  • tempelheer
  • tempelier
