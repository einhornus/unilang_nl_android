gedachtenis (54471)


Etymology

From gedacht + | -e- + | -nis. Cognate with German Gedächtnis.

Noun

gedachtenis f (plural gedachtenissen, diminutive gedachtenisje n)

  1. commemoration, remembrance
  2. (obsolete) keepsake
  3. (obsolete) commemorative monument

Synonyms

  • (commemoration): nagedachtenis
  • (keepsake): souvenir
