paragnost (48720)


Etymology

From Ancient Greek παρά- (pará-, “beside; next to, near, from; against, contrary to”) + | γνῶσις (gnôsis, “inquiry, investigation, knowing”)

Noun

paragnost m (plural paragnosten)

  1. clairvoyant

Synonyms

  • helderziende

Zelfstandig naamwoord

paragnost m

  1. (beroep) helderziende
Verwante begrippen
