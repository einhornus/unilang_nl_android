spriet (72761)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch spriet.

Noun

spriet m (plural sprieten, diminutive sprietje n)

  1. (botany) long, narrow shoot from a plant; blade (of grass or corn), sprig
  2. (nautical) sprit
  3. antenna
