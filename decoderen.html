decoderen (23875)


Etymology

Borrowed from English decode.

Verb

decoderen

  1. (transitive) to decode, transpose a coded message in (intel)legible form
  2. (transitive) to apply technological reversal of encryption, e.g. convert a compressed signal into the final (and original) form

Inflection

Synonyms

  • ontcijferen

Related terms

  • decoder
  • decodeur

Werkwoord

decoderen

  1. overgankelijk uit code overbrengen in gewone taal
Synoniemen
  • ontcijferen
Verwante begrippen
  • decoder, decodering
