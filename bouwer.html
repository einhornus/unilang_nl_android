bouwer (37352)


Etymology

From bouwen + | -er

Noun

bouwer m (plural bouwers, diminutive bouwertje n, feminine bouwster)

  1. builder
  2. (dialectal) Alternative form of boer.

Derived terms

  • landbouwer
  • vioolbouwer
