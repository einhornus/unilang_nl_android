kippevel (60418)


Etymology

kip + | -e- + | vel

Noun

kippevel n (plural kippevellen, diminutive kippevelletje n)

  1. Superseded spelling of kippenvel.

Usage notes

  • The spelling kippevel was deprecated in 1996 in the new Groene Boekje (“Little Green Book”) spelling reform.

Zelfstandig naamwoord

kippevel

  1. verouderde spelling of vorm van kippenvel van vóór 1996
