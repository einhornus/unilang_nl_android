schoolslag (88365)


Etymology

school + | slag (“stroke”)

Noun

schoolslag m (plural schoolslagen, diminutive schoolslagje n)

  1. The swimming style breaststroke, the basic one (first) taught at European schools

Related terms

  • crawl m
  • rugslag m
  • vlinderslag m
  • vrije slag m
  • wisselslag m
