wangedrocht (66646)


Etymology

No later than the 17th century. From wan- + | gedrocht.

Noun

wangedrocht n (plural wangedrochten, diminutive wangedrochtje n)

  1. monstrosity, monster, something or someone very ugly
    Synonyms: monster, gedrocht
