intimidatie (10457)


Etymology

From intimideren + | -atie, from French intimidation, from Medieval Latin *intimidatio, from intimidare (“to intimidate”). See intimidate.

Noun

intimidatie f (plural intimidaties, diminutive intimidatietje n)

  1. intimidation

Related terms
