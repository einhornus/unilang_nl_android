stoephoer (67297)


Etymology

Compound of stoep (“pavement, sidewalk”) + | hoer (“whore”).

Noun

stoephoer f (plural stoephoeren, diminutive stoephoertje n)

  1. (vulgar, derogatory, offensive) A highly pejorative term for a woman.
  2. (vulgar, uncommon) street whore (a prostitute who operates in public areas)
    Synonym: straathoer

stoephoer v

  2. (scheldwoord) (pejoratief) ordinaire del
