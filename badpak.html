badpak (12406)


Etymology

From bad (“bath”) + | pak (“suit”).

Noun

badpak n (plural badpakken, diminutive badpakje n)

  1. bathing suit, swimsuit
Synonyms
  • zwempak

Zelfstandig naamwoord

badpak o
