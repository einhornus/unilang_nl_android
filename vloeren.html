vloeren (11705)


Noun

vloeren

  1. Plural form of vloer

Verb

vloeren

  1. (transitive) To deck, (throw against the) floor
  2. (figuratively, transitive) To floor, defeat totally, eliminate
  3. (transitive) To (lay a) floor (in ...), cover with flooring
