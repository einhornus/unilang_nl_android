korf (39324)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch corf, from Old Dutch *korf, from Proto-Germanic *kurbaz, of disputed origin.

Noun

korf m (plural korven, diminutive korfje n)

  1. basket
Synonyms
  • mand

See the etymology of the main entry.

korf
