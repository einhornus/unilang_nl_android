oefening (3630)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch oefeninge. Equivalent to oefenen + | -ing. Compare German Übung (“exercise, practice”).

Noun

oefening f (plural oefeningen, diminutive oefeninkje n)

  1. practice
  2. exercise (activity or problem to train a skill, e.g. exercises in educational material)

Derived terms

  • gevechtsoefening
  • godsdienstoefening
  • ochtendoefening
  • oefening baart kunst
  • taaloefening
  • wapenoefening
