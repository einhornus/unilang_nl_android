loopy (64159)


Etymology

loop + | -y

Adjective

loopy (comparative loopier, superlative loopiest)

  1. Having loops.
    A loopy rollercoaster.
  2. (slang) Idiotic, crazy or drunk.

Synonyms

  • (having loops): looped
  • (idiotic): See Thesaurus:stupid
  • (crazy): See Thesaurus:insane
  • (drunk): See Thesaurus:drunk
