olm (114809)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch olm, from Old Dutch (only attested in toponyms), from Proto-Germanic *elmaz.

Noun

olm m (plural olmen, diminutive olmpje n)

  1. An elm, tree of the genus Ulmus.

Synonyms

  • iep
