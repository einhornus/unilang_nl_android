dingen (229)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch dingen (“to convene, to plead”), from Old Dutch *thingon, from Proto-Germanic *þingōną. The verb was originally weak, but became strong by analogy with other verbs.

Verb

dingen

  1. (transitive) to solicit

Derived terms

  • bedingen
  • meedingen
