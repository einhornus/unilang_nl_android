flikflooien (39583)


Etymology

From flikken (“to hit, to flatter, to fix”) + | vlooien (“to flatter”).

Verb

flikflooien

  1. (transitive) to butter up, flatter hoping for profit in return
  2. (intransitive) to practice flattery, sycophancy
  3. (intransitive) to resort to dubious methods or ploys

Derived terms

  • flikflooi
  • flikflooier
  • flikflooierij
  • flikflooiing
  • flikflooieren
