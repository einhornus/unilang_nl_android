verjongen (111602)


Etymology

From ver- + | jong + | -en.

Verb

verjongen

  1. (intransitive) to become young, to rejuvenate
  2. (transitive) to make young, to rejuvenate

Derived terms

  • verjonging

Werkwoord

verjongen

  1. jonger maken
