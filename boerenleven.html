boerenleven (70835)


Etymology

Compound of boer (“farmer”) + | -en- (“life, living”) + | leven.

Noun

boerenleven n (plural boerenlevens, diminutive boerenleventje n)

  1. The country life, notably the way of life as/with a farmer or agricultural employee

  • boerenjongen m
