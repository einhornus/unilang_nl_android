thans (25568)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch t(e)hande, t(e)hant, from te (“at”) + | hand (“hand”). Compare Middle Low German tohant, Middle High German zehant.

Adverb

thans

  1. now (at the present moment)

Synonyms

  • (now): nu

Derived terms

  • althans

Bijwoord

thans

  1. op het huidige tijdstip
Synoniemen
