wijlen (9829)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch wilen, from Old Dutch wīlon, dative plural of wīla (“while”). Cognate with English whilom.

Adjective

wijlen (not comparable)

  1. late, deceased
Inflection

Etymology 2

Noun

wijlen

  1. Plural form of wijl (“brief period of time”)

Noun

wijlen

  1. Plural form of wijl (“veil”)
  2. Plural form of wijle (“veil”)
