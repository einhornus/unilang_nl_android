ervaren (3926)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch ervaren, from Middle High German ervarn. Equivalent to er- + | varen.

Verb

ervaren

  1. (transitive) to experience, to undergo
Inflection

Etymology 2

Past participle of the verb ervaren.

Adjective

ervaren (comparative ervarener, superlative ervarenst)

  1. experienced, seasoned
Inflection
