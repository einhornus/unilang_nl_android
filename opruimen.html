opruimen (2903)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch oprumen. Equivalent to op + | ruimen.

Verb

opruimen

  1. (transitive) To clean (something) up,
  2. (intransitive) To tidy up, get in order
  3. (transitive) To clear away, eliminate, get rid of

  • opgeruimd staat netjes
