oversnijden (51015)


Etymology 1

From over + | snijden.

Verb

oversnijden

  1. (transitive) to cut again
  2. (transitive) to cut over, to cut across
Inflection

Etymology 2

From over- + | snijden.

Verb

oversnijden

  1. (transitive, Southern) to cut through
  2. (transitive) to cut excessively, to cut more than necessary
Inflection
