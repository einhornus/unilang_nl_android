verloederen (86703)


Etymology

Probably borrowed from German verludern. Equivalent to ver- + | loeder + | -en.

Verb

verloederen

  1. (intransitive) to become decadent, to decay (morally), to degenerate
  2. (intransitive) to deteriorate, to worsen (e.g. conditions)

Derived terms

  • verloedering

Werkwoord

verloederen

  1. ergatief te gronde gaan
    • Die stad was helemaal verloederd.
