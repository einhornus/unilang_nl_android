dienstmaagd (54700)


Etymology

Compound of dienst + | maagd.

Noun

dienstmaagd f (plural dienstmaagden, diminutive dienstmaagdje n)

  1. (formal) maidservant, maid
    Synonyms: bode, dienstbode, dienstmeid, meid
