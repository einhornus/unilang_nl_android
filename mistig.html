mistig (20045)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch mistigh, equivalent to mist + | -ig.

Adjective

mistig (comparative mistiger, superlative mistigst)

  1. (weather) misty
  2. (figuratively) vague, unclear

Inflection

Related terms

  • misten

mistig
