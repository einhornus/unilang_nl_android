alsem (61843)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch alsene. This etymology is incomplete. You can help Wiktionary by elaborating on the origins of this term.

Noun

alsem m (plural alsems)

  1. (chiefly uncountable) artemisia, any herb of the genus Artemisia.
  2. (uncountable) the buds and flowers of the artemisia plants, used as a herb
