afwassen (13873)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch afwasscen. Equivalent to af + | wassen.

Verb

afwassen

  1. (transitive) to wash off
  2. (intransitive) to wash up, to do the dishes
Derived terms
  • afwas
  • afwasser
