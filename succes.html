succes (764)


Etymology

From French succès (“success”), from Latin successus, past participle of succedere, from sub and cēdere (“withdraw, yield”).

Noun

succes n (plural successen, diminutive succesje n)

  1. success

Synonyms

  • welgang

Derived terms
