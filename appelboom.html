appelboom (39841)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch appelbôom. Equivalent to appel (“apple”) + | boom (“tree”).

Noun

appelboom m (plural appelbomen, diminutive appelboompje n)

  1. apple tree (any cultivar of the Malus domestica tree species)

Synonyms

  • appelaar

Zelfstandig naamwoord

appelboom m

  1. boom waaraan appels groeien
