parlement (8168)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch parlement, from Old French parlement.

Noun

parlement n (plural parlementen, diminutive parlementje n)

  1. parliament
  2. (obsolete) conversation, talking, discussion, talk
  3. (obsolete) meeting, assembly
