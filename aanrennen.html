aanrennen (55218)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch anerennen (“to attack”). Equivalent to aan + | rennen.

Verb

aanrennen

  1. (intransitive) to run near, to run close(r)
  2. (intransitive, obsolete) to attack [+ op (object)], to charge [+ op (against)]

Usage notes

This verb is often used in the infinitive or perfect participle in conjunction with komen.
