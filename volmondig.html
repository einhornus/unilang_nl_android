volmondig (71939)


Etymology

From vol- (“fully”) + | mond (“mouth”) + | -ig (“-y”).

Adjective

volmondig (comparative volmondiger, superlative volmondigst)

  1. frank

Adverb

volmondig

  1. frankly
  2. whole-heartedly, unconditionally
