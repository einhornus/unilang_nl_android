gestaag (51847)


Etymology

From earlier gestadig through syncope, from Middle Dutch gestadich, gestedich. Prefixed from ge- + | stadig, although the unprefixed form is now rare.

Adjective

gestaag (comparative gestager, superlative gestaagst)

  1. steady, continuous
  2. gradual
