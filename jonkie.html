jonkie (26189)


Etymology

Diminutive of jong (“young”).

Noun

jonkie n (plural jonkies)

  1. (Netherlands) youngling, little one ((relatively) young person)

Zelfstandig naamwoord

jonkie o

  1. jongen, knaap, jongdier, jong iemand.
