gedenken (17671)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch gedenken, from Old Dutch *gethenken. Equivalent to ge- + | denken.

Verb

gedenken

  1. (transitive) to remember, to commemorate

Inflection

Synonyms

  • herdenken

Werkwoord
