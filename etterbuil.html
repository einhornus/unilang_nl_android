etterbuil (61125)


Etymology

From etter (“pus”) + | buil (“hump, bump”).

Noun

etterbuil m (plural etterbuilen, diminutive etterbuiltje n)

  1. a localized accumulation of pus in the skin; a boil

Zelfstandig naamwoord

etterbuil v / m | 1 |

  1. (medisch) zwelling die etter bevat
  2. (scheldwoord) zeer onaangenaam persoon
Verwante begrippen
