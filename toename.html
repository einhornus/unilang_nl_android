toename (18900)


Etymology 1

Derived from toenemen on the model of German Zunahme. The same is true of other nouns in -name.

Noun

toename f or m (plural toenamen or toenames)

  1. increase
Synonyms
  • groei

Etymology 2

Verb

toename

  1. (archaic) singular past subjunctive of toenemen (when using a subclause)

Zelfstandig naamwoord

toename v/m

  1. het groter worden in aantal of maat
Synoniemen
  • toeneming
