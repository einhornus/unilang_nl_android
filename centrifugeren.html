centrifugeren (70664)


Etymology

Borrowed from French centrifuger.

Verb

centrifugeren

  1. (transitive) to centrifuge; to rotate quickly in a centrifuge in order to separate or dry its constituents

Inflection

Related terms

  • centrifugaal
  • centrifuge

Werkwoord

centrifugeren

  1. overgankelijk met een centrifuge scheiden, zuiveren of drogen
