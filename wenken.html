wenken (32146)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch wenken, from Old Dutch *wenken, from Proto-Germanic *wankijaną.

Verb

wenken

  1. to beckon, wave
  2. to warn by hinting with body movements

Etymology 2

See the etymology of the main entry.

Noun

wenken

  1. Plural form of wenk
