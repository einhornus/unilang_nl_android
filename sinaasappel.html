sinaasappel (16191)


Etymology

Modification of earlier Chinas-appel (“China's apple”).

Noun

sinaasappel m (plural sinaasappelen or sinaasappels, diminutive sinaasappeltje n)

  1. orange (fruit)
    Synonym: appelsien

Derived terms

  • sinaasappelboom
  • sinaasappelhuid
  • sinaasappelsap

Related terms

  • appelsien
  • sinas
