intelligentie (5489)


Etymology

From Old French intelligence, from Latin intelligentia.

Noun

intelligentie f (plural intelligenties, diminutive intelligentietje n)

  1. intelligence (capacity of mind)

Related terms

  • intelligent
  • intelligentiequotient, IQ
  • intelligentietest

Zelfstandig naamwoord

intelligentie v
