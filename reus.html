reus (5868)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch ruese, rose, rese, from Old Dutch *riso, *risi, from Proto-Germanic *risiz (“giant”). Cognate to German Riese (“giant”) and Old Norse risi (“giant”).

Noun

reus m (plural reuzen, diminutive reusje n, feminine reuzin)

  1. giant
