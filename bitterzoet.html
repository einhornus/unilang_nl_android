bitterzoet (54950)


Etymology

Compound of bitter + | zoet.

Adjective

bitterzoet (comparative bitterzoeter, superlative bitterzoetst)

  1. bittersweet, simultaneously sweet and bitter
  2. bittersweet, evoking or involving both sad and pleasant emotions
