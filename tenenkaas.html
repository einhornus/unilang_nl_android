tenenkaas (79070)


Etymology

From the similarity with kaas, cheese; tenengt;toes

Noun

tenenkaas f (uncountable)

  1. sticky, smegma-like substance that accumulates between one's toes in case of insufficient hygiene; toe cheese; toe jam.
    "Een vrijer met tenenkaas, plezant!"
    An unhygienic lover, yuk!
