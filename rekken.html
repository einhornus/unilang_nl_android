rekken (8783)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch recken, from Old Dutch *rekken, from Proto-West Germanic *rakkjan, from Proto-Germanic *rakjaną (“to stretch, to straighten”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₃roǵéyeti.

Verb

rekken

  1. to stretch
  2. to prolong (in duration)
    Synonym: verlengen
