knapzak (60400)


Etymology

Compound of knappen or knap + | zak. 16th century.

Noun

knapzak m (plural knapzakken, diminutive knapzakje n)

  1. bindle or other food bag (traveler's food bag, often a piece cloth tied to a stick)
