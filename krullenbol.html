krullenbol (42046)


Etymology

From krullen (“curls”) + | bol (“head”).

Noun

krullenbol m (plural krullenbollen, diminutive krullenbolletje n)

  1. a head with lots of curls
  2. a curly-headed person, a curly

Synonyms

  • (head) krullenkop
  • (person) krullenkop
