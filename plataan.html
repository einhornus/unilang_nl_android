plataan (114006)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch platanus, platane, from Latin platanus but subsequently influenced by Middle French platane, from Ancient Greek πλᾰ́τᾰνος (plátanos), which see.

Noun

plataan m (plural platanen, diminutive plataantje n)

  1. A plane tree, a sycamore; a tree of the genus Platanus.

Zelfstandig naamwoord

plataan m
