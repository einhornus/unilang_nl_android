kruidenier (16237)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch crudenare, with later change of suffix to -ier. Equivalent to kruid + | -en- + | -ier.

Noun

kruidenier m (plural kruideniers, diminutive kruideniertje n, feminine kruidenierster)

  1. grocer
