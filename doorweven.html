doorweven (93994)


Etymology 1

From door- + | weven.

Verb

doorweven

  1. (transitive) to weave through
  2. (transitive) to interlace, to embroider, to inweave
  3. (transitive, figurative) to be connected, to be intertwined

Etymology 2

From door + | weven.

Verb

doorweven

  1. (transitive) to weave through
  2. (intransitive) to continue weaving, to resume weaving

Werkwoord

dóórweven

  1. inergatief voortgaan met weven
    • Er werd de hele avond doorgeweven en doorgebreid.

Werkwoord

doorwéven

  1. overgankelijk door weven door iets heen doen
    • Het kleed was kunstig doorweven met gouden en zilveren draden.
