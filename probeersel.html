probeersel (100986)


Etymology

From proberen + | -sel.

Noun

probeersel n (plural probeersels or probeerselen, diminutive probeerseltje n)

  1. Something that has been or is being tried out or the result thereof; a test, an attempt, a practice.

Zelfstandig naamwoord

probeersel o | 1 |
