voorstad (27875)


Etymology

From voor (“before, in front of”) + | stad (“city”).

Noun

voorstad m or f (plural voorsteden, diminutive voorstadje n)

  1. (literally) A suburb:
    1. suburban municipality, adjacent and subordinate to a (major) city
    2. (originally) a quarter outside the city walls
    3. a suburban quarter
  2. (figuratively) A nearby hinterland
