overwerken (12280)


Etymology 1

Compound of over + | werken.

Verb

overwerken

  1. (intransitive) to work overtime
  2. (transitive) to rework, to work on (something) that has already been done
Inflection
Related terms
  • overwerk

Etymology 2

From over- + | werken.

Verb

overwerken

  1. (reflexive) to overwork, to get overworked
Inflection
