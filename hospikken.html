hospikken (42172)


Etymology

From hospik, a blend of hospitaal 'hospital' (from Latin) + pik 'dick, pecker, penis' (as crude pars pro toto for '[army] boy, man')

Noun

hospikken

  1. Plural form of hospik

Verb

hospikken

  1. To serve as military (hospital) orderly or as medic

Zelfstandig naamwoord

hospikken mv

  1. meervoud van het zelfstandig naamwoord hospik
