klooi (56994)


Etymology 1

Lenited form of kloot (“bollock”). The verb klooien may be (un)related.

Noun

klooi m (plural klooien, diminutive klooitje n)

  1. A dork, jerk, nobody
  2. A bumbler, loser
Synonyms
  • (dork) sul m, clown m, nul(liteit), oelewapper m, oen n, onnozelaar m
  • (bumbler) knoeier m, stuntel m, stuntelaar m, mislukkeling m
Related terms
  • geklooi n
  • kloten

Etymology 2

See the etymology of the main entry.

Verb

klooi

  1. first-person singular present indicative of klooien
  2. imperative of klooien

Zelfstandig naamwoord

  2. (scheldwoord) stuntel

klooi
