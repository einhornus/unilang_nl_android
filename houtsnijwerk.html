houtsnijwerk (49324)


Etymology

Compound of hout + | snijwerk.

Noun

houtsnijwerk n (plural houtsnijwerken, diminutive houtsnijwerkje n)

  1. (countable, uncountable, collective) A wood carving or several wood carvings; something carved from wood.
  2. (uncountable) The art of wood carving.
