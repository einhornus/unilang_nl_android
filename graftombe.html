graftombe (28186)


Etymology

Compound of graf + | tombe.

Noun

graftombe f (plural graftomben or graftombes, diminutive graftombetje n)

  1. tomb, crypt; sometimes used for any large funerary monument
    Synonym: tombe

Usage notes

Sometimes considered pleonastic (or more rarely tautological).
