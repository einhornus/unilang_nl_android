bolleke (119100)


Noun

bolleke n (plural bollekes) (Belgium)

  1. Diminutive of bol
  2. A kind of rounded glass in which regular De Koninck beer is served
  3. (by extension) A bolleke full of De Koninck.
    (Brabantian)
    Garçon, twee bollekes astenblief!
    Waiter, two De Koninck beers please!
