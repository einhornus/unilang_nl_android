bakerpraatje (109427)


Etymology

From bakerpraat + | -je.

Noun

bakerpraatje n (plural bakerpraatjes)

  1. (countable) an urban myth about childbirth and childrearing
  2. (by extension, countable) any urban myth or unfounded, unreliable story
