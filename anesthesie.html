anesthesie (28342)


Etymology

Borrowed from Latin anaesthēsia, from Ancient Greek ἀναισθησία (anaisthēsía), from ἀν- (an-, “not”) with αἴσθησις (aísthēsis, “sensation”). Equivalent to an- + | esthesie.

Noun

anesthesie f (plural anesthesieën)

  1. anesthesia

Synonyms

  • verdoving

Related terms

  • anestheseren
  • anesthesiologie

Zelfstandig naamwoord

anesthesie ; v

  1. (medisch): verdoving of narcose
  2. specialisme dat zich bezighoudt met narcose
