slagbal (99800)


Etymology

Compound of slag + | bal.

Noun

slagbal n (uncountable)

  1. A sport similar to baseball and softball that is played on a smaller, according to the official rules hexagonal, field with either a flat bat or a small cylindrical bat and in which runners receive an out when the ball is put on the home plate.

Derived terms

  • slagballen
