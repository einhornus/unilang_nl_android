richten (2710)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch richten, from Old Dutch rihten, from Proto-Germanic *rihtijaną (“to right”), derived from *rehtaz (“right, straight”) (modern recht).

Verb

richten

  1. (transitive) to direct, aim

Derived terms

  • richting
  • aanrichten
  • africhten
  • berichten
  • gelijkrichten
  • inrichten
  • oprichten
  • uitrichten
  • verrichten
