kletskous (25746)


Etymology

the root of kletsen 'to chatter' + kous 'stocking' (the second component is popular etymology for a variant on kouten 'to babble')

Noun

kletskous m or f (plural kletskousen, diminutive kletskousje n)

  1. chatterbox

Zelfstandig naamwoord

kletskous v / m | 3 |

  1. kletser
Synoniemen
  • kwebbel, kwebbelkont, ouwehoer
