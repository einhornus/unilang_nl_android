rugzaktoerist (113342)


Etymology

rugzak (“backpack”) + | toerist (“tourist”)

Noun

rugzaktoerist m (plural rugzaktoeristen, diminutive rugzaktoeristje n)

  1. A backpacker, tourist who carries all his luggage in a backpack

Related terms

  • rugtas f or m
  • reiszak m
  • toerisme n
  • toeristenklasse f
