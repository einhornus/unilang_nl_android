bloeddorst (44047)


Etymology

Backformed from bloeddorstig. Equivalent to bloed + | dorst.

Noun

bloeddorst m (uncountable)

  1. bloodthirst, bloodthirstiness [from 17th c.]
    Synonym: bloeddorstigheid

Related terms
