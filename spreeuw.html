spreeuw (35606)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch spreeuwe. This etymology is incomplete. You can help Wiktionary by elaborating on the origins of this term.

Noun

spreeuw m or f (plural spreeuwen, diminutive spreeuwtje n)

  1. The common starling, Sturnus vulgaris, individually or specifically.
  2. A starling, passerine bird of the family Sturnidae.
