hazenpad (82882)


Etymology

Compound of haas + | -en- + | pad.

Noun

hazenpad n (plural hazenpaden, diminutive hazenpaadje n)

  1. (uncommon) a hare's trail
    Synonym: hazenspoor

Usage notes

Predominantly used in the expression het hazenpad kiezen.

Zelfstandig naamwoord

hazenpad o

  1. door hazen gemaakt spoor
