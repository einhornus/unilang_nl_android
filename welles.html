welles (11776)


Interjection

welles

  1. (affirmation, informal, childish) Used to contradict a negative assertion.
    "Jij bent helemaal niet in Den Haag geboren." "Welles!"
    "You weren't born in The Hague at all." "I was, too!"
    "Nietes!" "Welles!"
    "Did not!" "Did too!"
    "It is not!" "It is, too!"
