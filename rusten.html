rusten (1581)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch rusten. Equivalent to rust + | -en.

Verb

rusten

  1. (intransitive) to rest
  2. (intransitive) to lie, be supported (by something)
Derived terms
  • berusten
  • uitrusten
