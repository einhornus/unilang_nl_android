dominatie (107531)


Etymology

From Middle English domynacion, from Old French dominaciun, from Latin dominātiō (“rule, dominion”), from dominor (“domineer; rule”); see dominate; from domineren + | -atie

Noun

dominatie f (plural dominaties)

  1. domination

Synonyms

  • overheersing
