kontlikker (39328)


Etymology

From kont (“butt, arse”) + | likker (“licker”). Parallel to French lèche-cul.

Noun

kontlikker m (plural kontlikkers, diminutive kontlikkertje n)

  1. (vulgar) sycophant, arse-kisser, shameless or even slavish flatterer

Synonyms

  • gatlikker

Zelfstandig naamwoord

kontlikker m

  1. (informeel) slijmerd, slijmbal, vleier
