perestroika (101397)


Etymology

Borrowed from Russian перестро́йка (perestrójka, “restructuring”).

Derived terms

  • antiperestroika
  • Perestroikan
  • Salinastroika

Noun

perestroika (plural perestroikas)

  1. (singularity theory) A situation where a small variation of parameters leads to a sudden change in properties.
