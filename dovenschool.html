dovenschool (107426)


Etymology

From dove (“deaf person”) + | -n- + | school.

Noun

dovenschool f (plural dovenscholen, diminutive dovenschooltje n)

  1. school for the deaf, where sign language is "spoken"

Zelfstandig naamwoord

dovenschool v / m

  1. (onderwijs) school voor dove en zwaar slechthorende kinderen
