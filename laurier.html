laurier (81908)


Etymology

Borrowed from Middle French laurier, from Old French lorier.

Noun

laurier m (plural laurieren, diminutive lauriertje n)

  1. laurel, Laurus nobilis
    Synonym: lauwer

Related terms

  • lauweren

Zelfstandig naamwoord

laurier m
