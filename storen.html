storen (2601)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch stôren, from Old Dutch *stōren, from Proto-Germanic *staurijaną.

Verb

storen

  1. to disturb, to bother
  2. (radio) to interfere with, to cause interference

Inflection

Werkwoord
