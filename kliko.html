kliko (116078)


Etymology

From Kliko, acronym of proper names Klinkenberg and Koster, a company that historically sold wheelie bins.

Noun

kliko f (plural kliko's)

  1. (Netherlands) wheelie bin (outdoor tall plastic garbage container with two wheels to one side)

Synonyms

  • minicontainer
  • sulo (regional)
  • milko (regional)
  • otto (regional)
  • engels (regional)
