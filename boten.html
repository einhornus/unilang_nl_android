boten (4113)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch boten, from Old Dutch *bōtan, from Proto-Germanic *bautaną (“to strike”).

Verb

boten

  1. (transitive, Belgium) to hit
  2. (transitive, agriculture, chiefly Belgium) to knock the seeds out of a flax plant
