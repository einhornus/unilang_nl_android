koosjer (21305)


Etymology

Borrowed from Yiddish כּשר | (kosher), from Hebrew כָּשֵׁר | (kashér).

Adjective

koosjer (not comparable)

  1. (of food) kosher
  2. (figuratively, by extension) proper, in order, meet

Inflection

Related terms

  • kasjroet
