wreed (3475)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch wreet, from Old Dutch *wrēth, from Proto-Germanic *wraiþaz.

Adjective

wreed (comparative wreder, superlative wreedst)

  1. cruel, callous
  2. (slang) wicked, cool, awesome

Inflection
