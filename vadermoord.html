vadermoord (78667)


Etymology

From vader + | moord.

Noun

vadermoord m (plural vadermoorden, diminutive vadermoordje n)

  1. patricide, parricide of one's father
  2. (figurative) defamation of a fatherlike authority figure (a person or an organization such as a business)

Related terms

  • parricidium, moedermoord
