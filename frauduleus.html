frauduleus (131005)


Etymology

Borrowed from French frauduleux, from Latin fraudulosus, from fraus (“fraud”).

Adjective

frauduleus (comparative frauduleuzer, superlative meest frauduleus or frauduleust)

  1. fraudulent, constituting fraud

Inflection

Synonyms

  • bedrieglijk

Related terms

  • frauderen
  • fraudeur m
