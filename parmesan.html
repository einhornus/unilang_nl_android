parmesan (73648)


Etymology

Borrowed from French parmesan, from Italian parmigiano.

Noun

parmesan (usually uncountable, plural parmesans)

  1. A hard, full-fat Italian cheese from Parma.
  2. (by extension) A similar cheese produced elsewhere.
  3. (slang) Money.
