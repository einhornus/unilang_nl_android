legionair (42020)


Etymology

Borrowed from French légionnaire, from Latin legiōnārius.

Noun

legionair m (plural legionairs, diminutive legionairtje n)

  1. A legionnaire, a legionary.

Adjective

legionair (not comparable)

  1. (uncommon) legionary, pertaining to a legion

Inflection

Related terms

  • legioen
