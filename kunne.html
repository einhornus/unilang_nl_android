kunne (35928)


Etymology 1

From Old Dutch *kunni, from Proto-Germanic *kunją, from Proto-Indo-European *ǵenh₁- (“to produce”).

Noun

kunne f (uncountable)

  1. gender, sex

Etymology 2

Verb

kunne
