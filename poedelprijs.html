poedelprijs (89266)


Etymology

From poedel (“miss, failure”) + | prijs (“prize”).

Noun

poedelprijs m (plural poedelprijzen, diminutive poedelprijsje n)

  1. (Netherlands) booby prize, a prize awarded to the loser.
