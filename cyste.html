cyste (31989)


Etymology

Ultimately from Latin cystis, from Ancient Greek κύστις (kústis).

Noun

cyste f (plural cysten or cystes, diminutive cystetje n)

  1. cyst (sac which develops in the natural cavities of an organ)

Zelfstandig naamwoord

cyste v/m | 3 |
