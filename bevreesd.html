bevreesd (26328)


Etymology

From bevrezen.

Adjective

bevreesd (comparative bevreesder, superlative meest bevreesd or bevreesdst)

  1. afraid
    Wees niet bevreesd. | Don't be afraid.

Inflection

Synonyms

  • vervaard

bevreesd
