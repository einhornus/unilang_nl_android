duister (6481)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch duuster, from Old Dutch *thiustri, from Proto-Germanic *þiustrijaz.

Adjective

duister (comparative duisterder, superlative duisterst)

  1. dark
    Synonym: donker
  2. bleak; gloomy
  3. sinister
  4. obscure

Inflection
