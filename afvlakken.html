afvlakken (134086)


Etymology

From af + | vlak + | -en.

Verb

afvlakken

  1. (transitive, intransitive) to smoothen (to make or become smooth)
    Synonyms: egaliseren, nivelleren, schaven

Inflection

Werkwoord
