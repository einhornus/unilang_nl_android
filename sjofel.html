sjofel (40176)


Etymology

Either borrowed from German schofel from Yiddish or a direct borrowing from Yiddish שפֿל | (shofl, “shabby”), from Hebrew שָׁפָל | (šāfāl, “low, humble”).

Adjective

sjofel (comparative sjofeler, superlative sjofelst)

  1. shabby, poor

Inflection
