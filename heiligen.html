heiligen (8964)


Etymology

From Old Dutch *hēlagon, *heiligon, from Proto-Germanic *hailagōną.

Verb

heiligen

  1. to hallow, to sanctify

Inflection

Noun

heiligen

  1. Plural form of heilige

Related terms

  • heilig
  • heiligenbeeld

Werkwoord
