hartfalen (60756)


Etymology

Compound of hart + | falen.

Noun

hartfalen n (uncountable)

  1. (medicine) heart failure ((chronic) inability of the heart to circulate a sufficient amount of blood)
  2. (colloquial, media) heart attack

Usage notes

The sense “heart attack”, while ubiquitous in the media and in common parlance, is generally considered medically incorrect.

Zelfstandig naamwoord

hartfalen o
