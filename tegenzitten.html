tegenzitten (112400)


Etymology

Compound of tegen (“against”) + | zitten (“to sit”)

Verb

tegenzitten

  1. (intransitive) to go wrong
  2. (intransitive) to be harder or less pleasant than expected

Inflection

Werkwoord

tegenzitten
