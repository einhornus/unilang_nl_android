grasland (42222)


Etymology

Compound of gras + | land.

Noun

grasland n (plural graslanden, diminutive graslandje n)

  1. grassland; may also refer to a pasture or lawn

Zelfstandig naamwoord

grasland o | 1 |

  1. (landbouw) grond waarop overwegend gras groeit.
Verwante begrippen
  • wei, weide
