tjaptjoi (72428)


Etymology

Borrowed from Cantonese 雜菜／杂菜 (zaap6 coi3), from 雜／杂 (zaap6, “mixed”) + 菜 (coi3, “vegetables”).

Noun

tjaptjoi m (uncountable)

  1. chop suey (Chinese dish with meat and mixed vegetables)

Zelfstandig naamwoord

tjaptjoi v/m
