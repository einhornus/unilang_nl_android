weg (95)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch wech, from Old Dutch weh, weg, from Proto-Germanic *wegaz, from Proto-Indo-European *weǵʰ-.

Noun

weg m (plural wegen, diminutive wegje n or weggetje n)
