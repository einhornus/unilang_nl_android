volmaakt (6278)


Etymology 1

Verb

volmaakt

  1. second- and third-person singular present indicative of volmaken (“to make full”) (when using a subclause)

Etymology 2

Adjective

volmaakt (not comparable)

  1. perfect

Verb

volmaakt

  1. second- and third-person singular present indicative of volmaken
  2. (archaic) plural imperative of volmaken

Participle

volmaakt

  1. past participle of volmaken
