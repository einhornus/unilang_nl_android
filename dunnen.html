dunnen (49649)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch dunnen. Equivalent to dun + | -en.

Verb

dunnen

  1. (ergative) to thin, to become/make slender
  2. (ergative) to become/make sparse

Inflection

Werkwoord

dunnen | 1 |

  1. onovergankelijk dun of dunner worden
  2. overgankelijk dun of dunner maken
