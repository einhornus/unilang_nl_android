immer (18992)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch immer (“always”), from Old Dutch iomer (“always”). From ie (“always”) + | meer (“more”). Related to ieder, iemand, iets, ooit. Cognate with German immer.

Adverb

immer

  1. (formal) always

Synonyms

  • altijd
  • altoos

Bijwoord

immer

  1. op ieder moment
Synoniemen
  • altijd, steeds
