populistisch (113895)


Etymology

From populist + | -isch. First attested in the late nineteenth century.

Adjective

populistisch (not comparable)

  1. populist, pertaining to populism [from late 20th c.]
  2. populist, pertaining to American populism or the Populist Party [from late 19th c.]

Inflection

Related terms
