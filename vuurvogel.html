vuurvogel (97215)


Etymology

Compound of vuur (“fire”) + | vogel (“bird”).

Noun

vuurvogel m (plural vuurvogels, diminutive vuurvogeltje n)

  1. A fire bird, e.g. a phoenix, Bennu or other mythological bird associated with fire.
