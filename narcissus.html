narcissus (45012)


Etymology

Borrowed from Latin narcissus, from Ancient Greek νάρκισσος (nárkissos), ultimately either from Pre-Greek or related to νάρκη (nárkē).

Noun

narcissus (plural narcissuses or narcissi)
