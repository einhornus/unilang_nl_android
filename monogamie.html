monogamie (27539)


Etymology

Probably borrowed from French monogamie.

Noun

monogamie f (uncountable)

  1. monogamy, marriage with only one partner at a time [from late 18th c.]
  2. (zoology) monogamy, the practice of having only one partner at a time

Related terms

  • monogaam
  • monogamist
