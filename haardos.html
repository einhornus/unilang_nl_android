haardos (57322)


Etymology

From haar + | dos.

Noun

haardos m (plural haardossen, diminutive haardosje n)

  1. hair, headhair (all of a person's head hair)
    Synonyms: dos, haarbos, hoofdhaar
  2. hairdo, hairstyle, haircut (particular way of styling one's hair)
    Die haardos staat je goed!
    That hairstyle looks good on you!
    Synonym: kapsel
