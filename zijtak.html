zijtak (96554)


Etymology

From zij + | tak.

Noun

zijtak m (plural zijtakken, diminutive zijtakje n)

  1. a branch originating from a larger part of a tree
  2. an offshoot of a river
  3. (figurative) ramification, offshoot, spur (something that derives from another)
