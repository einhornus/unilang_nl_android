fiks (43799)


Etymology 1

Borrowed from Middle French fixe, from Latin fixus.

Adjective

fiks (comparative fikser, superlative meest fiks or fikst)

  1. strong
  2. large, sizable
  3. (obsolete) solid, fixed
Inflection

Etymology 2

See the etymology of the main entry.

Verb

fiks

  1. first-person singular present indicative of fiksen
  2. imperative of fiksen
