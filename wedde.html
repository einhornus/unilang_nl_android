Noun

wedde f (plural wedden or weddes, diminutive weddetje n)

  1. (chiefly Belgium) wage, wages
  2. (Netherlands, dated, military) wage of a soldier

Etymology 2

Verb

wedde

  1. singular past indicative and subjunctive of wedden
  2. (archaic) singular present subjunctive of wedden

Zelfstandig naamwoord
