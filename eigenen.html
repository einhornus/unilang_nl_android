eigenen (93774)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch eigenen. Equivalent to eigen + | -en.

Verb

eigenen

  1. (transitive, obsolete) to give in ownership, to grant [+ aan (object) = to]
  2. (reflexive, obsolete) to claim ownership of, to take

Inflection

Werkwoord
