schans (67613)


Etymology

From German Schanze; cognate with English sconce

Noun

schans f (plural schansen, diminutive schansje n)

  1. A sconce or a similar type of fortification.
  2. A ramp.
    1. A platform or ramp used for ski jumpers (or any similar sport) to gain elevation during their jumps.

Synonyms

  • binnenschans
  • schansspringen
  • skischans
  • springschans

Zelfstandig naamwoord

  2. wal om een stad (met name om Amsterdam)
