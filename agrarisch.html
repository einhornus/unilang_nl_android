agrarisch (85248)


Etymology

Borrowed from Latin agrārius. The general meaning “agrarian, agricultural” derives from German agrarisch.

Adjective

agrarisch (not comparable)

  1. agrarian, agricultural
  2. (historical, archaic) pertaining to the Agrarian laws

Inflection

Related terms

  • agrariër

Bijvoeglijk naamwoord
