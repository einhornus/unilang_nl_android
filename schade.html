schade (1941)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch schāde, from Old Dutch skatho, from Proto-Germanic *skaþô.

Noun

schade f (uncountable)

  1. damage
    voorkom alcoholschade bij uw opgroeiende kind ― prevent damage from alcohol in your maturing child
