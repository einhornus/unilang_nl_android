bakkes (47820)


Etymology

From bakhuis, compound of bak (“chin, cheek”) + | huis (“house, encasing”).

Noun

bakkes n or m (plural bakkesen, diminutive bakkesje n)

  1. (informal, vulgar) face

Synonyms

  • gelaat
  • gezicht

Zelfstandig naamwoord

bakkes o | 3 |

  1. (informeel) gezicht
  2. (informeel) mond
Synoniemen
