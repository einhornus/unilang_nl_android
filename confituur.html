confituur (94470)


Etymology

Borrowed from French confiture.

Noun

confituur f (plural confituren)

  1. (chiefly Belgium) jam, marmelade (congealed sweet mixture of conserved fruits)
  2. (uncountable, uncommon) sugar-preserved confitted fruits

Synonyms

(jam):

  • jam
  • marmelade

Related terms

  • konfijt
