godvruchtig (64922)


Etymology

god (“god”) + | vrucht (“fear”) + | -ig (“-y”)

Adjective

godvruchtig (comparative godvruchtiger, superlative godvruchtigst)

  1. God-fearing, devout, pious

Synonyms

Related terms
