symboliek (29031)


Etymology

Borrowed from French symbolique. The sense “theological study of symbolism” comes from German Symbolik. Equivalent to symbool + | -iek.

Noun

symboliek f (plural symbolieken)

  1. symbolism
  2. (dated) the theological study of symbolism

Adjective

symboliek (comparative symbolieker, superlative symboliekst)

  1. (uncommon) symbolic
    Synonym: symbolisch
