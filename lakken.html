lakken (31770)


Etymology 1

From lak (“varnish, lacquer”).

Verb

lakken

  1. (transitive, intransitive) to lacquer, to varnish
Inflection

Etymology 2

From Middle Dutch lacken.

Verb

lakken

  1. (chiefly Southern) Alternative form of likken.
Inflection

Etymology 3

See the etymology of the main entry.

Noun

lakken

  1. Plural form of lak

Zelfstandig naamwoord

lakken mv
