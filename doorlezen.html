doorlezen (25396)


Etymology 1

Compound of door + | lezen.

Verb

doorlezen

  1. (transitive) to read completely, to peruse
  2. (intransitive) to continue reading, to resume reading
Inflection

Etymology 2

Compound of door- + | lezen.

Verb

doorlezen

  1. (transitive) to spend time reading
  2. (transitive, dated) to read completely
Inflection
