radja (37831)


Etymology

Via an Indonesian language from Hindi राजा (rājā), from Sanskrit राज (rāja), cognate with Latin rex, etc.

Noun

radja m (plural radja's, diminutive radjaatje n)

  1. The Hindu princely title rajah, in various meanings, yet often rendered as king

Related terms

  • maharadja m
  • rana m
  • rawal m
  • rawat m
