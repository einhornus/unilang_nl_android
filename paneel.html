paneel (14809)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch paneel, from Old French panel, from Medieval Latin pannellus, later variant of pannulus, diminutive of pannus.

Noun

paneel n (plural panelen, diminutive paneeltje n)

  1. panel (flat, rectangular, often wooden, surface)
  2. control panel
    Synonym: bedieningspaneel
