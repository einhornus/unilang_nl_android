zogenaamd (6891)


Etymology

Univerbation of zo + | genaamd.

Adjective

zogenaamd (not comparable)

  1. so-called (having such a name, being called such)
  2. so-called, supposedly

Synonyms

  • zogeheten
  • zg. (abbreviation)
  • zgn. (abbreviation)
  • z.g. (abbreviation)
