muggenzifter (102691)


Etymology

17th century or earlier. From muggenziften + | -er.

Noun

muggenzifter m (plural muggenzifters, diminutive muggenziftertje n)

  1. (derogatory) nitpicker

Synonyms

  • haarklover
  • mierenneuker
  • vitter

Zelfstandig naamwoord

muggenzifter m

  1. kleingeestige vitter
Synoniemen
  • mierenneuker, kommaneuker
