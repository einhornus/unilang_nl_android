poedel (15132)


Etymology 1

Borrowed from German Pudel. Cognate with English poodle.

Noun

poedel m (plural poedels, diminutive poedeltje n)

  1. poodle (dog breed)
    Poedels zijn van oorsprong jachthonden.
    Poodles were originally used as hunting dogs.
Derived terms
  • labradoedel
  • poedelnaakt (literally, 'naked as a poodle', starkers, from the fashion of shaving most of a poodle's fur)

Noun

poedel m (plural poedels, diminutive poedeltje n)

  1. (archaic, chiefly ball games) A miss; a throw or shot that misses the goal or target.
