pest (2694)


Noun

pest f (uncountable)

  1. A plague, pest, pestilence.
  2. A specific bovine plague
  3. An obnoxious person

Verb

pest

  1. first-, second- and third-person singular present indicative of pesten
  2. imperative of pesten
