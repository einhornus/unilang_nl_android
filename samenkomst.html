samenkomst (18923)


Etymology

Compound of samen + | komst, from samenkomen.

Noun

samenkomst f (plural samenkomsten, diminutive samenkomstje n)

  1. meeting, gathering, meetup
  2. junction, intersection (place where two or more things come together, esp. lines and (water)ways)

Zelfstandig naamwoord

samenkomst v

  1. bijeengekomen mensen
Verwante begrippen
  • vergadering, bijeenkomst, ontmoeting
