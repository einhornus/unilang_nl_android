rits (8715)


Etymology 1

Onomatopoeic. As a noun, a shortening of ritssluiting.

Interjection

rits

  1. Sound of tearing or closing a zip fastener.

Noun

rits f or m (plural ritsen, diminutive ritsje n)

  1. zipper (Us), zip fastener (Uk)
Synonyms
  • ritssluiting
  • treksluiting
