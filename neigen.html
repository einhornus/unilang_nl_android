neigen (31060)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch neigen, from Old Dutch neigen, from Proto-Germanic *hnaigijaną, the causative of nijgen.

Verb

neigen

  1. to bend over, lean (towards)
  2. to tend, incline

Derived terms

  • neiging
  • geneigd zijn

Werkwoord

neigen
