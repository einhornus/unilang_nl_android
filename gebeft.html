gebeft (40801)


Adjective

gebeft (comparative gebefter, superlative gebeftst)

  1. fitted with a bef (senses collar etc.), said of wearer or (especially formal) dress, notably in the expression gemanteld en gebeft, i.e. wearing a protestant minister's liturgic outfit
