waterdier (120983)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch waterdier. Equivalent to water (“water”) + | dier (“animal”)

Noun

waterdier n (plural waterdieren, diminutive waterdiertje n)

  1. (zoology) An aquatic animal (animal which lives (primarily) in water).

Zelfstandig naamwoord

waterdier o

  1. dier dat in het water leeft
