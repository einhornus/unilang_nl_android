ondier (73911)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch ondier. Equivalent to on- + | dier.

Noun

ondier n (plural ondieren, diminutive ondiertje n)

  1. A vermin, a nasty or unwanted beast; a monster.
    Synonyms: gedrocht, monster, wangedrocht

Related terms

  • ongedierte
