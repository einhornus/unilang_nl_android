gedrocht (13394)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch gedrochte, ghedrochte (“apparition, vision”).

Noun

gedrocht n (plural gedrochten, diminutive gedrochtje n)

  1. monstrosity, monster, something or someone very ugly
    Dat gebouw is een gedrocht. ― That building is a monstrosity.
    Synonyms: monster, wangedrocht
  2. (archaic) apparition, chimera

Related terms

  • wandrocht

Zelfstandig naamwoord
