Verb

pijpen

  1. (transitive, intransitive) to whistle; to hum; to blow (as in to play a wind instrument)
    Synonym: fluiten
  2. (transitive, intransitive, colloquial) to fellate, to blow, to suck (the object usually indicates the receiving person, but sometimes the member or item undergoing the action)
    Pijp m'n stijve
    Suck my stiffy
    Synonyms: fellatio doen, likken, fluiten
  3. (transitive, intransitive, dated) to smoke (a pipe)
    Synonym: pijpeneren
