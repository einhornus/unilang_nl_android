courtisane (40943)


Etymology

Borrowed from Middle French courtisane, from Italian cortigiana.

Noun

courtisane f (plural courtisanes)

  1. A courtesan, a high-status prostitute or mistress.
    Synonyms: hofhoer, hofpop

Zelfstandig naamwoord

courtisane v | 3 |
