bunkeren (94796)


Etymology

From bunker + | -en.

Verb

bunkeren

  1. (transitive, nautical) to create a stock of (mostly of fuel)
  2. (transitive, intransitive) to devour, to feast, to eat voraciously in large quantities

Inflection

Werkwoord

bunkeren | 2 |

  1. onovergankelijk de bunkers (brandstofreservoirs) vullen, brandstof innemen
  2. onovergankelijk (informeel) schransen
