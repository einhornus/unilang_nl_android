zelfvoldaan (28383)


Etymology

Back-formation from zelfvoldaanheid. Equivalent to zelf (“self”) + | voldaan (“satisfied”).

Adjective

zelfvoldaan (comparative zelfvoldaaner, superlative zelfvoldaanst)

  1. self-satisfied, smug [from early 19th c.]

Inflection
