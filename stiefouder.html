stiefouder (112674)


Etymology

From stief- (“step-”) + | ouder (“parent”) (from the comparative degree of oud (“old, senior”)); cognate with German Stiefelter.

Noun

stiefouder m (plural stiefouders, diminutive stiefoudertje n)

  1. A stepparent.

  • stiefgezin
  • stiefgrootouder

  • stiefmoeder
