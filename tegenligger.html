tegenligger (98953)


Etymology

From tegen + | liggen + | -er. Originally a nautical term.

Noun

tegenligger m (plural tegenliggers, diminutive tegenliggertje n)

  1. oncoming traffic, oncoming traffic user [from 20th c.]
  2. (nautical) oncoming vessel [from 19th c.]

Zelfstandig naamwoord

tegenligger m

  1. tegemoetkomend voertuig
