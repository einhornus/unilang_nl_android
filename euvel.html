euvel (76115)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch evel, ovel, oevel, from Old Dutch uvil, from Proto-Germanic *ubilaz. Cognate with English evil, West Frisian evel, and German Übel.

Adjective

euvel (comparative euveler, superlative euvelst)

  1. evil

Inflection

Synonyms

  • (evil): slecht, boosaardig, kwaadaardig

Noun

euvel n (plural euvels, diminutive euveltje n)

  1. evil
  2. problem

Synonyms

  • (problem): probleem
