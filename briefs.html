briefs (132805)


Noun

briefs

  1. plural of brief
  2. (usually in the plural) A short, close-fitting type of underpants.

Usage notes

Despite being a single item of clothing, briefs—like pants, trousers, underpants, amp;c.—are considered to come in "pairs" and are counted using the phrase "pair(s) of".
