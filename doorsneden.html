doorsneden (107459)


Etymology 1

Noun

doorsneden

  1. Plural form of doorsnede

Etymology 2

Adjective

doorsneden (comparative doorsnedener, superlative doorsnedenst)

  1. (heraldry) divided into two equal parts horizontally, per fess

Verb

doorsneden

  1. plural past indicative and subjunctive of doorsnijden (“to keep cutting, to cut in two”) (when using a subclause)

Etymology 3

Verb

doorsneden

  1. plural past indicative and subjunctive of doorsnijden (“to intersect”)

Participle

doorsneden

  1. past participle of doorsnijden (“to intersect”)
