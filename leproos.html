leproos (69056)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch leproes (“leprous”), from Latin leprōsus. Equivalent to lepra + | -oos.

Noun

leproos m (plural leprozen, diminutive leproosje n)

  1. leper

Synonyms

  • melaatse

Adjective

leproos (not comparable)

  1. (obsolete) leprous
    Synonym: lepreus
