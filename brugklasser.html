brugklasser (94846)


Etymology

From brugklas + | -er. First attested in the early 1950s.

Noun

brugklasser m (plural brugklassers, diminutive brugklassertje n)

  1. (Netherlands) a pupil in the first form or grade of secondary education, usually at the age of eleven or twelve
    Synonyms: brugger, brugpieper

Zelfstandig naamwoord

brugklasser m

  1. (onderwijs) iemand die in de brugklas zit
