stijl (2384)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch stijl, from Old French estile, from Latin stilus.

Noun

stijl m (plural stijlen, diminutive stijltje n)

  1. style, fashion
  2. (botany) style of a pistil
  3. a bar on a metal fence
  4. the vertical part of the metal or concrete support frame that surrounds a window or a door
