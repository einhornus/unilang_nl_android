citroen (7677)


Etymology

Borrowed from Middle French citron, from Latin citrus.

Noun

Citroenen
Lemons

citroen f or m (plural citroenen, diminutive citroentje n)

  1. lemon (fruit)
  2. lemon tree, Citrus limon

Related terms

  • citronella
