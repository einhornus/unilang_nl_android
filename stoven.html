stoven (59101)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch stoven. Equivalent to stoof + | -en.

Verb

stoven

  1. to stew on a fire
  2. (figuratively) to prepare
  3. to stove
  4. to simmer for a long time
Inflection

Noun

stoven

  1. Plural form of stoof

Etymology 2

Verb

stoven
