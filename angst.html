angst (1052)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch anxt, from Old Dutch *angust, from Proto-Germanic *angustiz.

Noun

angst m (plural angsten, diminutive angstje n)

  1. fear, fright, anxiety
    Synonyms: schrik, vrees, vrucht

Derived terms

  • angstaanjagend
  • angsthaas
  • angstig
  • angstpsychose
  • angststoornis
  • faalangst
  • vliegangst

Related terms
