grijpen (3233)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch gripen, from Old Dutch grīpan, from Proto-Germanic *grīpaną, from Proto-Indo-European *gʰreyb- (“to grasp, grab”).

Verb

grijpen

  1. (transitive) to grab

Inflection
