gezellin (49455)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch gesellinne. Equivalent to gezel (“companion”) + | -in (“suffix indicating femininity”).

Noun

gezellin f (plural gezellinnen, diminutive gezellinnetje n)

  1. female companion
  2. wife
  3. girlfriend, female partner in a relationship
