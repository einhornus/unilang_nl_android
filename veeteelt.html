veeteelt (66922)


Etymology

Compound of vee (“livestock, cattle”) + | teelt (“cultivation, breeding”).

Noun

veeteelt f (uncountable)

  1. husbandry, the raising of livestock

Related terms

  • veeboer
  • veestapel
  • pluimveeteelt
  • runderteelt

Zelfstandig naamwoord
