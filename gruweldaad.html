gruweldaad (31200)


Etymology

Compound of gruwel (“abomination, horror”) + | daad (“action, deed”).

Noun

gruweldaad f (plural gruweldaden, diminutive gruweldaadje n)

  1. horrific action, abominable deed

Zelfstandig naamwoord
