goeiedag (8608)


Interjection

goeiedag

  1. Alternative spelling of goedendag.

Usage notes

Although commonly used in everyday writing, this spelling is considered incorrect by some, who favour goedendag instead. Regardless of spelling, the pronunciation of both is identical.
