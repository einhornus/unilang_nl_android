zelfverminking (77880)


Etymology

Compound of zelf + | verminking.

Noun

zelfverminking f (plural zelfverminkingen)

  1. (chiefly uncountable) self-mutilation, self-harm
    Synonym: zelfmutilatie
  2. (rare, zoology) autotomy
    Synonym: autotomie

Zelfstandig naamwoord

zelfverminking v
