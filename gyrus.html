gyrus (82969)


Etymology

From Latin gȳrus (“circle”), from Ancient Greek γῦρος (gûros). Doublet of gyro and gyre.

Noun

gyrus (plural gyri or gyruses)

  1. (anatomy) A ridge or fold on the cerebral cortex.
