brandweerauto (32751)


Etymology

Compound of brandweer (“fire department”) + | auto (“car, motorized vehicle”).

Noun

brandweerauto m (plural brandweerauto's, diminutive brandweerautootje n)

  1. fire engine, fire truck

Synonyms

  • brandweerwagen

Zelfstandig naamwoord

brandweerauto m
