druppen (83812)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch droppen, from Proto-Germanic *druppōn-, from Proto-Indo-European *dʰrbʰ-néh₂-, from *dʰrbʰ-. Also compare *drupô (“a drop”).

Verb

druppen

  1. (intransitive) to drip
