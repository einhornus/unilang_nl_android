infecteren (27105)


Etymology

From Latin inficere, modelled on French infecter

Verb

infecteren

  1. (transitive) To infect, bring into contact with a contagious germ causing illness
  2. (figuratively) To contaminate, pass on (to ...) some undesirable idea, habit etc.
