stoelen (4355)


Etymology

stoel 'chair, seat' (cognate with English stool, German Stuhl) + -en

Verb

stoelen

  1. (used with the preposition op 'on' and an indirect object) To rest, be based/ founded, on something (such as an argumentation)
  2. (botany, intransitive) To form into a stool (stump)

Inflection

Related terms

  • stoelrogge
  • uitstoelen

stoelen
