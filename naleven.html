naleven (23745)


Etymology 1

From na (“after, according to, subsequent to”) + | leven (“to live”).

Verb

naleven

  1. (transitive) To observe, live by, respect a rule etc.
    Synonyms: nakomen, respecteren, volgen
  2. (intransitive) To live on, have an afterlife, be continued after one's own time
    Oudmodische kunststromingen leven vaak lang na in maniërisme.
    Old-fashioned artistic styles often live on long in mannerism.
    Synonym: doorleven
Inflection
Related terms
  • nakomen
  • uitleven

Etymology 2

From na (“after, subsequent to”) + | leven (“life”), calque of German Nachleben.

Noun

naleven n (plural nalevens)

  1. A continued existence in a reduced form, a state of living on in a lesser form.
    Synonym: nachleben

Werkwoord

naleven

  1. overgankelijk zich houden aan een verplichting
    • We moeten de reglementen naleven.
Synoniemen
  • nakomen
