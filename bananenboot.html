bananenboot (95553)


Etymology

Compound of banaan (“banana”) + | -en- + | boot (“boat”).

Noun

bananenboot m (plural bananenboten, diminutive bananenbootje n)

  1. A banana boat, a boat that transports bananas.
  2. A banana boat, a yellow inflatable boat.
  3. A dessert consisting of one or two bananas, ice cream, whipped cream and often including chocolate, vaguely shaped like a boat.
