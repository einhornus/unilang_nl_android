maandelijks (31740)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch maendelijx. Equivalent to maand (“month”) + | -e- + | -lijks (“-ly”).

Adverb

maandelijks (not comparable)

  1. monthly, related to a month, occurring during a month or every month
