inval (7128)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch inval (“inspiration, sudden idea”).

Noun

inval m (plural invallen, diminutive invalletje n)

  1. inspiration, brain wave
  2. bust, raid
  3. incursion, invasion
  4. incidence

Etymology 2

See the etymology of the main entry.

Verb

inval

  1. first-person singular present indicative of invallen (when using a subclause)

Zelfstandig naamwoord

inval m

  2. plotseling opkomende gedachte
