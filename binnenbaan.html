binnenbaan (108864)


Etymology

Compound of binnen (“inside, inner, indoor”) + | baan (“court, lane, orbit”).

Noun

binnenbaan f (plural binnenbanen, diminutive binnenbaantje n)

  1. (sports) indoor court, indoor playing field
  2. (sports) inner lane
