kiezen (1180)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch kiesen, from Old Dutch *kiesan, from Proto-Germanic *keusaną, from Proto-Indo-European *ǵews-.

Verb

kiezen

  1. (transitive, intransitive) to choose
