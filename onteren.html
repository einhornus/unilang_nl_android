onteren (25231)


Etymology

From ont- (“dis-”) + | eer (“honor”) + | -en.

Verb

onteren

  1. (transitive) to dishonor
    Je kunt dat niet doen zonder zijn nagedachtenis te onteren.
    You cannot do that without dishonoring his memory.
  2. (transitive) to rape, to violate sexually

Inflection

Werkwoord

onteren
