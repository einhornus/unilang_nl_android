overspuiten (80590)


Etymology 1

From over + | spuiten.

Verb

overspuiten

  1. (transitive) to spray again
  2. (transitive) to spray over, to spray across
Inflection

Etymology 2

From over- + | spuiten.

Verb

overspuiten

  1. (transitive) to overspray
  2. (transitive) to spray over, to spray across
Inflection
