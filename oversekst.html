oversekst (44899)


Etymology

From over- + | seks + | -t.

Adjective

oversekst (not comparable)

  1. (informal, derogatory) hypersexual, having a mind in the gutter (considered excessively preoccupied with sex)

Derived terms

  • oversekstheid

Bijvoeglijk naamwoord

oversekst
