blindedarmontsteking (52317)


Etymology

Compound of blindedarm (“cecum”) + | ontsteking (“inflammation”).

Noun

blindedarmontsteking f (plural blindedarmontstekingen, diminutive blindedarmontstekinkje n)

  1. (pathology) appendicitis, inflammation of the vermiform appendix

Related terms

Zelfstandig naamwoord

blindedarmontsteking v
