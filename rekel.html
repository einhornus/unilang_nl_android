rekel (67818)


Etymology

From Middle Low German rekel. This etymology is incomplete. You can help Wiktionary by elaborating on the origins of this term.

Noun

rekel m (plural rekels, diminutive rekeltje n)

  1. male of a canine species, notably dog, fox or wolf
  2. insolent person

Synonyms

  • (male canine) reu
  • (person) hondsvot, deugniet, rakker, vlegel m

Zelfstandig naamwoord

rekel m

  1. (scheldwoord) deugniet
  2. mannetje van de hond, de vos, de wolf en de das
Verwante begrippen
  • [1] bengel, blaag, snotaap, vlegel
  • [2] mannetjesvos, rekelvos, reu
