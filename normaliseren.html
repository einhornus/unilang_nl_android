normaliseren (63782)


Etymology

Borrowed from French normaliser.

Verb

normaliseren

  1. (transitive) to normalize, to make normal
  2. (transitive, statistics, mathematics) to normalize, to correct for variables or to adjust values to a given norm
  3. (transitive) to normalize, to standardize
