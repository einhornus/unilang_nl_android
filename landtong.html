landtong (91363)


Etymology

Compound of land + | tong. Cf. German Landzunge, which also dates from the eighteenth century.

Noun

landtong f (plural landtongen, diminutive landtongetje n)

  1. spit of land, headland [from 18th c.]

Synonyms

  • nes
  • uitham

Zelfstandig naamwoord

landtong v/m

  1. smalle strook land die zich in zee of meer uitstrekt
