volbouwen (121337)


Etymology 1

From vol- + | bouwen.

Verb

volbouwen

  1. (transitive) to fully build, to build till completion

Etymology 2

From vol + | bouwen.

Verb

volbouwen

  1. (transitive) to build in specific area till it's filled up.
