uitslag (4142)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch uteslach.

Noun

uitslag m (plural uitslagen, diminutive uitslagje n)

  1. result (e.g. sporting or exam)
  2. outcome (of a decision)
  3. (medicine) rash
  4. the act of bringing into circulation
  5. land that is not protected by dikes
  6. (obsolete) the act of driving livestock into pastures
