tokkelen (46290)


Etymology

A frequentive verb derived from tokken + | -elen (to pull, tap, tick).

Verb

tokkelen

  1. (intransitive) to pluck strings
  2. (intransitive) to strum instruments
  3. (intransitive) to tick, tap staccato
  4. (transitive, obsolete) to tap, touch reeatedly, in various ways meanings
