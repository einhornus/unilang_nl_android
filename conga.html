conga (32732)


Etymology

Likely borrowed from English conga, from Spanish conga, from Congo.

Noun

conga f (plural conga's)

  1. (music) A conga (small Cuban drum used in pairs).
  2. (music, uncountable) Conga (Cuban march music and dance style).
