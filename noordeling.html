noordeling (114870)


Etymology

From noorden (“north”) + | -ling.

Noun

noordeling m (plural noordelingen, diminutive noordelingetje n)

  1. northerner
  2. especially someone from the North of the Netherlands
  3. name of a potato variety

Synonyms

  • noorderling m

Related terms

  • noordelijk
  • Noords
