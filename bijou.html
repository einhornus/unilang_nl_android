bijou (25445)


Etymology

Borrowed from French bijou, from Breton bizoù.

Noun

bijou m (plural bijoux or bijous, diminutive bijoutje n)

  1. a piece of jewelry, often specifically with fake gems

Derived terms

  • bijouterie

Zelfstandig naamwoord

bijou o | 3 |
