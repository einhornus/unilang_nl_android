bemoeien (4122)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch bemoeien. Equivalent to be- + | moeien.

Verb

bemoeien

  1. (reflexive) to interfere, to meddle [+ met (object) = with]

Derived terms

  • bemoeial
  • bemoeienis
  • bemoeizucht
