fregatvogel (93407)


Etymology

fregat 'frigate' + vogel 'bird'

Noun

fregatvogel m (plural fregatvogels, diminutive fregatvogeltje n)

  1. (zoology) A frigate bird, the species Fregata aquila or another rapacious seabird of the Fregatidae family

Related terms

  • fregatkapitein m
  • zeevogel m
