indiaan (5642)


Etymology

Ultimately from Latin indiānus. Equivalent to Indië (“East or West Indies”) or India (“India”) + | -aan.

Noun

indiaan m (plural indianen, diminutive indiaantje n, feminine indiaanse)

  1. Native American, Indian, First Nations person
