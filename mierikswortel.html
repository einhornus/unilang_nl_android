From mierik + | wortel.

Noun

mierikswortel m (plural mierikswortels, diminutive mieriksworteltje n)

  1. The root of the horseradish, harvested as a pungent condiment and formerly a remedy for scorbut
  2. horseradish, Armoracia rusticana.

Synonyms

  • (root): peperwortel

Derived terms
