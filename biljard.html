biljard (77023)


Etymology 1

Borrowed from German Billiarde.

Noun

biljard n (plural biljarden, diminutive biljardje n)

  1. quadrillion (1015)

Etymology 2

Noun

biljard n or m (plural biljarden or biljards, diminutive biljardje n)

  1. Obsolete spelling of biljart.
