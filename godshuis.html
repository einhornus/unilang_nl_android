godshuis (92888)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch godshuus. Equivalent to god (“god”) + | -s- (“house”, genitival interfix) + | huis.

Noun

godshuis n (plural godshuizen, diminutive godshuisje n)

  1. A house of worship (building for religious services).
  2. (now Belgium) A hospice, a charitable institution providing housing to the elderly, the infirm, etc.
    Synonym: gasthuis
