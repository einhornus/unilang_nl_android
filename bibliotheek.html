bibliotheek (2838)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch biblioteke, from Middle French bibliotheque, from Latin bibliothēca, from Ancient Greek βιβλιοθήκη (bibliothḗkē).

Noun

bibliotheek f (plural bibliotheken, diminutive bibliotheekje n)

  1. library

Related terms

  • bibliothecaris

Zelfstandig naamwoord

bibliotheek v
