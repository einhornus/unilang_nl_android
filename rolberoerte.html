rolberoerte (59400)


Etymology

Compound of rollen + | beroerte.

Noun

rolberoerte f (plural rolberoertes or rolberoerten)

  1. A rolling fit; a seizure causing one to roll over the ground (commonly used in hyperbole, typically in relation to fright, surprise or laughing).
