karwei (12087)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch corweide, perhaps via Old French corvee, from Medieval Latin corveda, from corrogata in corrogata opera.

Noun

karwei n (plural karweien, diminutive karweitje n)

  1. job, task
  2. work

Related terms

  • corvee
