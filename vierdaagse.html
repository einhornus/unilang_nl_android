vierdaagse (72021)


Etymology

From vierdaags.

Noun

vierdaagse m or f (plural vierdaagsen or vierdaagses)

  1. A four-day episodal race, contest or sports event.
  2. A four-day trip or other event.

Adjective

vierdaagse

  1. Inflected form of vierdaags
