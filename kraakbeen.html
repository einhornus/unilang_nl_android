kraakbeen (30447)


Etymology

From kraken (“to crack”) + | been (“bone”).

Noun

kraakbeen n (plural kraakbeenderen or kraakbenen, diminutive kraakbeentje n)

  1. (uncountable) cartilage
  2. (countable) a piece of gristle
