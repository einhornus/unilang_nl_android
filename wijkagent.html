wijkagent (35435)


Etymology

Compound of wijk (“neighbourhood, quarter”) + | agent (“police officer”).

Noun

wijkagent m (plural wijkagenten, diminutive wijkagentje n)

  1. community police officer

Zelfstandig naamwoord

wijkagent m
