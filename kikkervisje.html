kikkervisje (57019)


Etymology

From kikker (“frog”) + | visje (“fish”).

Noun

kikkervisje n (plural kikkervisjes)

  1. tadpole, the young stage of a frog after hatching, before it reaches its adult form

Synonyms

  • dikkop
