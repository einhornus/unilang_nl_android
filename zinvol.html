zinvol (17917)


Etymology

From zin + | -vol, calque of German sinnvoll.

Adjective

zinvol (comparative zinvoller, superlative zinvolst)

  1. meaningful, useful
  2. sensible, making sense

Inflection

Bijvoeglijk naamwoord

zinvol
