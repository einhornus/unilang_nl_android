vernietigingskamp (86686)


Etymology

From vernietiging + | kamp.

Noun

vernietigingskamp n (plural vernietigingskampen)

  1. extermination camp, death camp (camp intended for the extermination of certain groups)

Usage notes

  • The word has strong associations with the Second World War and with the Holocaust in particular.
