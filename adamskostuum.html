adamskostuum (110001)


Etymology

Compound of Adam (“Adam”) + | -s- + | kostuum (“suit, costume”). Referring to nudity in the Garden of Eden.

Noun

adamskostuum n (plural adamskostuums)

  1. A man's birthday suit, more generally any person's birthday suit; the nude state.

Synonyms

  • blootje
  • bloterik
  • flikker
  • nakie

Related terms

  • adamsappel
