verdoemen (40020)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch verdoemen, from Old Dutch [Term?], from Proto-Germanic *fradōmijaną, equivalent to ver- + | doem + | -en.

Verb

verdoemen

  1. to damn
  2. to curse
  3. (literally) to bring doom upon

Derived terms

  • verdoemenis

Related terms

  • doemen
  • doem
