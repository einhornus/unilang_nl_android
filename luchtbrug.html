luchtbrug (27558)


Etymology

Compound of lucht (“air, sky”) + | brug (“bridge”).

Noun

luchtbrug f (plural luchtbruggen, diminutive luchtbruggetje n)

  1. An airlift.
  2. A skybridge, a bridge connecting buildings or various part of a building.

Zelfstandig naamwoord
