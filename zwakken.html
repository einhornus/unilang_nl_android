zwakken (15363)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch swacken, swaken. Equivalent to zwak + | -en.

Verb

zwakken

  1. (ergative, obsolete) to weaken, to become/make strengthless

Derived terms

  • afzwakken
  • verzwakken
