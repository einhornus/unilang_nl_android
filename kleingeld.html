kleingeld (6709)


Etymology

Compound of klein (“small”) + | geld (“money”): cognate to German Kleingeld.

Noun

kleingeld n (uncountable)

  1. small change

Related terms

  • geld
  • bankbiljet
  • munt

Zelfstandig naamwoord

kleingeld o

  1. geld in kleine coupures, vooral in munten
