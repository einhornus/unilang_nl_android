alternatief (6186)


Etymology

From Middle French alternatif, from Medieval Latin alternativus, from the participle stem of Latin alternāre (“to do by turns”).

Adjective

alternatief (not comparable)

  1. alternative
  2. alternative, deviant
  3. (obsolete) alternating

Inflection

Noun

alternatief n (plural alternatieven, diminutive alternatiefje n)

  1. alternative
