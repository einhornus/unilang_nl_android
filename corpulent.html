corpulent (107975)


Etymology

Borrowed from Middle French corpulent, from Old French corpulent, from Latin corpulentus.

Adjective

corpulent (comparative corpulenter, superlative corpulentst)

  1. overweight, corpulent
    Synonyms: gezet, lijvig

Inflection
