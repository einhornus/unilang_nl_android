dorpsgenoot (131730)


Etymology

Compound of dorp + | -s- + | genoot.

Noun

dorpsgenoot m (plural dorpsgenoten, diminutive dorpsgenootje n, feminine dorpsgenote)

  1. Someone from the same village, fellow villager.
