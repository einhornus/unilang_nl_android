stokoud (33789)


Etymology

From stok (“cane, walking stick”) + | oud (“old”).

Adjective

stokoud (comparative stokouder, superlative stokoudst)

  1. very old, ancient

Inflection

Related terms

  • stokdoof
  • stokstil
