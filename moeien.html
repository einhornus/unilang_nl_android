moeien (31071)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch moeyen, from Old Dutch *muoien, from Proto-Germanic *mōwijaną.

Verb

moeien

  1. (transitive) to annoy, to bother
  2. (transitive) to involve
  3. (reflexive) to involve oneself

Inflection

Werkwoord

  1. lastig vallen, mengen
