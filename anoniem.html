anoniem (7202)


Etymology

Borrowed from French anonyme, from Latin anōnymus, from Ancient Greek ἀνώνυμος (anṓnumos).

Adjective

anoniem (comparative anoniemer, superlative anoniemst)

  1. anonymous, without an (acknowledged) name

Synonyms

  • naamloos

Derived terms

  • anonimiteit
