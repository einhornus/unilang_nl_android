bericht (1176)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch berecht. Equivalent to a verbal neutrum from berichten.

Noun

bericht n (plural berichten, diminutive berichtje n)

  1. message
  2. news
  3. communication
Derived terms
  • berichtgeven
  • spoedbericht
  • tegenbericht
  • weerbericht

See the etymology of the main entry.

Verb

bericht

  2. imperative of berichten
