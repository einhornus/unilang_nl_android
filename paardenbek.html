paardenbek (63523)


Etymology

From paard (“horse”) + | -en- + | bek (“animal's mouth”).

Noun

paardenbek m (plural paardenbekken, diminutive paardenbekje n)

  1. a horse's mouth
  2. (derogatory, by extension) a mouth that bears resemblance to that of a horse, a broad mouth with a set of protruding teeth
