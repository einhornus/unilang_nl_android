vermist (1955)


Etymology

From vermissen.

Adjective

vermist (not comparable)

  1. missing, lost

Inflection

Verb

vermist

  1. second- and third-person singular present indicative of vermissen
  2. (archaic) plural imperative of vermissen

Participle

vermist

  1. past participle of vermissen
