waagschaal (36468)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch waechschale.

Noun

waagschaal f (plural waagschalen, diminutive waagschaaltje n)

  1. (figuratively) risk, risky situation or condition(s)
  2. Archaic form of weegschaal.

Derived terms

  • in de waagschaal stellen

Zelfstandig naamwoord

waagschaal v/m | 2 |
