kennis (1236)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch kennesse. Equivalent to kennen + | -nis.

Noun

kennis f (uncountable)

  1. knowledge

Noun

kennis m or f (plural kennissen, diminutive kennisje n)

  1. acquaintance, friend

Related terms
