professie (100961)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch professie (“profession, declaration of faith”), from Latin professiō (“declaration, avowal”).

Noun

professie f (plural professies)

  1. profession, occupation (line of work)
  2. profession, avowal (declaration of belief)
