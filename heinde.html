heinde (35091)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch gehende, equivalent to ge- + hand, meaning "at hand".

Adverb

heinde

  1. closeby
    Synonyms: na, nabij, dichtbij

Usage notes

Rarely used outside the expression van heinde en verre (“lit. "from closeby and far", from everywhere”).

Related terms

  • behendig
  • bijdehand
  • handig
