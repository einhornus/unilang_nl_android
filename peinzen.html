peinzen (50985)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch pensen, peinsen, from Old French penser, from Latin pens≈ç.

Verb

peinzen

  1. (intransitive) to ponder, meditate
  2. (intransitive, Belgium) to think

Inflection

Werkwoord
