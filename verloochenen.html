verloochenen (25122)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch verlogenen, verloochenen, verloghenen. Equivalent to ver- + | loochenen. Template:etylstub

Verb

verloochenen

  1. (transitive) to disavow
  2. (transitive) to deny, to block out (e.g. the possibility of something occurring)

Inflection

Werkwoord
