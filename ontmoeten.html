ontmoeten (649)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch ontmoeten, untmuten, entmuten, corresponding to ont- + | moeten. The latter verb is from Old Dutch *muoten, from Proto-Germanic *mōtijaną.

The unprefixed form is cognate Low German möten, West Frisian moetsje, English meet, Danish møde.

Verb

ontmoeten

  1. to meet
  2. to encounter, come across
