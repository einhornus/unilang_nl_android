prent (23046)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch prente. This etymology is incomplete. You can help Wiktionary by elaborating on the origins of this term.

Noun

prent f or m (plural prenten, diminutive prentje n)

  1. A print, a printed picture.
  2. (colloquial) A fine.
    Synonyms: bekeuring, boete, bon
  3. film, movie
