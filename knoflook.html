knoflook (8690)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch knofloick, knuuflooch, a dissimilated variant of earlier Middle Dutch cloffloc (literally “clove-leek”), equivalent to klieven + | look. Compare German Knoblauch, West Frisian knyflok and English clove.
