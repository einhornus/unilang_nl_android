dieperik (94138)


Etymology

From diep + | -erik.

Noun

dieperik m (plural dieperiken, diminutive dieperikje n)

  1. depth, any deep place, or in particular, drop
  2. (uncommon, Netherlands, dated, art) individual, usually an art critic, considered to be overly concerned with finding deep meanings
