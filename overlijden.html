overlijden (5979)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch overliden, from Old Dutch overlīthan. Equivalent to over- + | lijden (“to suffer, to undergo”).

Verb

overlijden

  1. (intransitive) to die
    Synonym: sterven

Inflection

Noun

overlijden n (uncountable)

  1. death, passing away
