discus (107603)


Etymology

Borrowed from Latin discus, from Ancient Greek δίσκος (dískos). First attested in the eighteenth century.

Noun

discus m (plural discussen, diminutive discusje n)

  1. discus
    Synonym: werpschijf

  • diskette
