regenwater (28522)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch regenwater. Equivalent to regen + | water.

Noun

regenwater n (plural regenwateren or regenwaters)

  1. (chiefly uncountable) rainwater (water falling as rain; such water being stored up)

Zelfstandig naamwoord

regenwater o

  1. water dat in de vorm van regen uit de hemel komt.
