onecht (27515)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch onechte. Equivalent to on- + | echt (“legal, legally valid, marital”).

Adjective

onecht (not comparable)

  1. extramarital (chiefly of children and extramarital relations)
Inflection

Etymology 2

From on- + | echt (“real”).

Adjective

onecht (not comparable)

  1. inauthentic, fraudulent, false (chiefly in relation to literary forgeries)
Inflection

Bijvoeglijk naamwoord

onecht | 1 |
