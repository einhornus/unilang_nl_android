hoogachtend (13380)


Etymology

Compound of hoog + | achtend.

Adjective

hoogachtend (comparative hoogachtender, superlative hoogachtendst)

  1. having high regard (with an indirect object optionally indicating the object of high regard)

Inflection

Interjection

hoogachtend

  1. (formal) yours sincerely, yours faithfully (ending of a formal letter)

Werkwoord

hoogachtend

  1. onvoltooid deelwoord van hoogachten
