hechten (10616)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch hechten, from Old Dutch heften, from Proto-Germanic *haftijanÄ….

Verb

hechten

  1. (transitive) to attach, to bind
  2. (transitive) to suture, to stitch

Inflection
