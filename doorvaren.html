doorvaren (43885)


Etymology 1

From door- + | varen.

Verb

doorvaren

  1. (transitive) to sail through completely
  2. (transitive, figuratively) to experience a sensation through the entire body
Inflection

Etymology 2

From door + | varen.

Verb

doorvaren

  1. (transitive) to sail through
  2. (intransitive) to continue sailing, to resume sailing
Inflection
