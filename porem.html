porem (89190)


Etymology

Borrowed from Yiddish פּנים | (ponem, “face”) from Hebrew פָּנִים | (pāním, “face”). See ponem.

Noun

porem n (plural porems, diminutive porempje n)

  1. (informal) face
    Dat is toch geen porem.
    What an ugly sight.

Zelfstandig naamwoord

porem o

  1. (Jiddisch-Hebreeuws) gezicht, smoel
