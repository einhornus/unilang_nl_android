masochist (40522)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Masochist, from Leopold von Sacher-Masoch + | -ist.

Noun

masochist m (plural masochisten, diminutive masochistje n)

  1. A masochist.
  2. (informal) A glutton for punishment.

Derived terms

  • masochistisch
  • sadomasochist

Related terms

  • masochisme
