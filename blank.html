blank (3207)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch blanc, from Old Dutch *blank, from Proto-Germanic *blankaz.

Adjective

blank (comparative blanker, superlative blankst)

  1. white, pale
  2. white (having a light skin tone)

Derived terms

  • blank staan
  • blankvoorn
