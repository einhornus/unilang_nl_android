dommelen (54677)


Etymology 1

Unknown. May be the same as dommelen (“to buzz”), with influence from dom (“dumb”).

Verb

dommelen

  1. (intransitive) to doze, to snooze
Derived terms
  • indommelen
  • wegdommelen

Etymology 2

From Middle Dutch dommelen, from dommen.

Verb

dommelen

  1. (intransitive) to buzz
  2. (intransitive) to thump (sound)

Werkwoord

dommelen

  1. inergatief half in slaap zijn
    • Na het eten zaten opa en oma wat te dommelen onder de luifel.
