nonchalant (23079)


Etymology

From French nonchalant.

Adjective

nonchalant (comparative nonchalanter, superlative nonchalantst)

  1. careless, showing no interest or effort

Inflection

Related terms

  • nonchalance

Bijvoeglijk naamwoord

nonchalant
