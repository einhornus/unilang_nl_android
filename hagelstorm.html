hagelstorm (57318)


Etymology

Equivalent to hagel (“hail”) + | storm (“storm”).

Noun

hagelstorm m (plural hagelstormen, diminutive hagelstormpje n)

  1. hailstorm (storm accompanied by precipitation in the form of hail)
