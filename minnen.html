minnen (56669)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch minnen, from Old Dutch minnon, from Proto-Germanic *minjōną. Equivalent to min + | -en.

Verb

minnen

  1. (transitive) to love
  2. (transitive) to neck, cuddle
  3. (transitive) to have an affinity for
Inflection

minnen
