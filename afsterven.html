afsterven (42637)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch afsterven. Equivalent to af + | sterven.

Verb

afsterven

  1. (intransitive) to die off
  2. (intransitive) to die out, to die away
  3. (intransitive) to decompose

Derived terms

  • afsterving
