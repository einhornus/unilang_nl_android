deerniswekkend (107758)


Etymology

deernis + | wekkend

Adjective

deerniswekkend (comparative deerniswekkender, superlative deerniswekkendst)

  1. meagre (UK), meager (US), paltry
  2. pitiful, pathetic

Inflection

Synonyms

  • (pitiful): meelijwekkend
