schoenmaker (16475)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch schoemaker, variants with -n- already existed in later Middle Dutch. Equivalent to schoen (“shoe”) + | maker.

Noun

schoenmaker m (plural schoenmakers, diminutive schoenmakertje n)

  1. shoemaker (artisan who produces and repairs shoes)

Zelfstandig naamwoord

schoenmaker m

  1. (beroep) iemand die als vak schoenen repareert
