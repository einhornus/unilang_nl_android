ketjap (104450)


Etymology

Borrowed from Indonesian kecap.

Noun

ketjap m (plural ketjaps, diminutive ketjapje n)

  1. kecap, Indonesian soy sauce
    Hypernym: sojasaus

Related terms

  • ketchup
