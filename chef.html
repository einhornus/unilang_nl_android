chef (1972)


Etymology

Borrowed from French chef.

Noun

chef m (plural chefs, diminutive chefje n, feminine cheffin)

  1. A boss, chief, head, leader.
    Synonym: baas
  2. A culinary chef, a head cook.
    Synonym: chefkok
  3. Short for a title including chef.
