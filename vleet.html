vleet (36482)


Etymology

From earlier vlete, from Middle Dutch vlote, ablaut from vlieten (“to flow”).

Noun

vleet f (plural vleten)

  1. (historical) A series of drift nets used for catching herring. [from 16th c.]

Zelfstandig naamwoord
