perzik (13630)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch perseke, ultimately from Late Latin persicum, persica. This etymology is incomplete. You can help Wiktionary by elaborating on the origins of this term.

Noun

Perzik
Peach

perzik f or m (plural perziken, diminutive perzikje n)

  1. A peach tree, Prunus persica.
  2. A peach, a fruit of the peach tree, in particular one with a fuzzy skin.

Zelfstandig naamwoord
