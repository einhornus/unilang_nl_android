keukenprinses (69313)


Etymology

Compound of keuken (“kitchen”) + | prinses (“princess”).

Noun

keukenprinses f (plural keukenprinsessen, diminutive keukenprinsesje n, masculine keukenprins)

  1. A woman who is good at and enjoys cooking non-professionally. [from ca. 1830]
