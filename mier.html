mier (12686)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch miere, from Old Dutch *miuri, from Proto-Germanic *miurij«≠.

Noun

mier f (plural mieren, diminutive miertje n)

  1. (zoology) ant
    Synonym: emt
  2. (figuratively) insignificant individual
