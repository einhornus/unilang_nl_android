pioen (125458)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch pionie, from Old French [Term?], from Latin paeōnia, from Ancient Greek παιωνία (paiōnía).

Noun

pioen f (plural pioenen)

  1. A peony, a plant of the genus Paeonia.
    Synonym: pioenroos
