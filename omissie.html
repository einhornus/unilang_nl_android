omissie (80956)


Etymology

Borrowed from Middle French omission, from Late Latin omittere (“to send”). Post-classical Latin introduced the term from the past participial stem of omittere and the suffix -ie.

Noun

omissie f (plural omissies, diminutive omissietje n)

  1. omission
  2. negligence

Related terms

  • omitteren
