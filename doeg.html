doeg (19895)


Etymology

Derived from the Dutch dialect Zaans, where doeg means dag (“day”).

Interjection

doeg

  1. (Netherlands) bye

Usage notes

This word is mostly used in the Netherlands, it is less common in Belgium.

Synonyms

  • doei
  • dag
  • toedeloe
  • houdoe
Synoniemen
  • doei, toedeloe
