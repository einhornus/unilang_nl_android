sonderen (99616)


Etymology

Borrowed from Middle French sonder. Equivalent to sonde + | -eren.

Verb

sonderen

  1. (transitive) to probe, to measure or examine with a probe
  2. (transitive, figurative) to probe, to question or investigate

Inflection

Werkwoord

sonderen | 2 |
