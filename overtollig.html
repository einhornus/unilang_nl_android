overtollig (53566)


Etymology

Arose as a variant of overtallig. Equivalent to over- + | tal + | -ig, but can be reanalyzed as over- + | tol + | -ig

Adjective

overtollig (comparative overtolliger, superlative overtolligst)

  1. waste, superfluous
    overtollig water - waste water

Inflection

Bijvoeglijk naamwoord

overtollig

  1. wat in te ruime mate aanwezig is
    • De overtollig goederen zullen worden gedumpt.
Synoniemen
  • overbodig, overcompleet, overmatig, overtallig
Verwante begrippen
  • overmaat, overschot, overvloed, restant, teveel
