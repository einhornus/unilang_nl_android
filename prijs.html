prijs (895)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch prijs, prise, from Old French pris, preis, from Latin pretium (“worth, price, money spent, wages, reward”).

Noun

prijs m (plural prijzen, diminutive prijsje n)

  1. price, fare, charge
  2. price, price tag, price ticket
  3. prize, award, trophy
  4. reward, prize
  5. praise, act of praising

Etymology 2

See prijzen.

Verb

prijs

  1. first-person singular present indicative of prijzen
  2. imperative of prijzen
