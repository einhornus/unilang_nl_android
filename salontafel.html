salontafel (34717)


Etymology

Compound of salon + | tafel.

Noun

salontafel f (plural salontafels, diminutive salontafeltje n)

  1. coffee table, tea table (low, relatively small table used in living rooms, parlours, etc.)

Zelfstandig naamwoord

salontafel v/m
