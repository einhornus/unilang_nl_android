voegen (5760)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch voegen, from Old Dutch fuogen, from Proto-Germanic *fōgijaną.

Verb

voegen

  1. (transitive) to place, to put
  2. (transitive) to add
  3. (transitive) to grout
  4. (transitive) to fit, to suit
    Dat voegt je niet.
    That doesn't suit you.
