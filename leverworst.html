leverworst (38079)


Etymology

Compound of lever (“liver”) + | worst (“sausage”) (cognate with German Leberwurst).

Noun

leverworst f or m (plural leverworsten, diminutive leverworstje n)

  1. liverwurst, a liver sausage [from 16th c.]
    Synonym: leverbeuling

Zelfstandig naamwoord

leverworst v / m | 1 |

  1. (voeding) worst die flink wat lever bevat
Verwante begrippen
  • vleeswaren, leverbeuling, beuling
