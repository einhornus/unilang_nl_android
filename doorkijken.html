doorkijken (57696)


Etymology 1

Compound of door + | kijken.

Verb

doorkijken

  1. (intransitive) to continue watching
  2. (transitive) to skim, to briefly or hastily look at
  3. (intransitive) to watch through, to see through

doorkijken
