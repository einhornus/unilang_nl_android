beuren (22309)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch bueren, from Old Dutch *burien, from Proto-Germanic *burjanÄ….

Verb

beuren

  1. (transitive) to lift, to raise
  2. (transitive) to collect (debt, owed money)
  3. (transitive, poetic) to receive
  4. (intransitive) to happen, to occur
  5. (transitive) to befall

Derived terms

  • gebeuren
  • opbeuren
  • verbeuren
