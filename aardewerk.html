aardewerk (30744)


Etymology

From aarde + | werk.

Noun

aardewerk n (plural aardewerken)

  1. (chiefly uncountable, collective) earthenware

Adjective

aardewerk (not comparable)

  1. (uncommon) earthenware
    Synonym: aardewerken
