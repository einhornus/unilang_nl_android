tegenoffensief (87503)


Etymology

Compouund of tegen + | offensief. The entered common usage during the First World War.

Noun

tegenoffensief n (plural tegenoffensieven, diminutive tegenoffensiefje n)

  1. (military) A counter-offensive. [from late 1890s]
