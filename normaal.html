normaal (672)


Etymology

Ultimately from Latin nōrmālis. This etymology is incomplete. You can help Wiktionary by elaborating on the origins of this term.

Adjective

normaal (comparative normaler, superlative normaalst)

  1. normal, usual, in accordance with what is common
  2. (mathematics) normal, perpendicular

Inflection

Adverb

normaal

  1. normally, usually
    Doe normaal!
    Calm down!
    Get a grip!
    Chill out!

Noun

normaal f (plural normalen)

  1. (mathematics) normal (line or vector perpendicular to another element)
