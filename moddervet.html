moddervet (46908)


Etymology

Compound of modder (“mud”) + | vet (“fat, cool”).

Adjective

moddervet (not comparable)

  1. very fat, extremely obese
  2. extremely fat (containing a lot of lipids)
  3. very cool, awesome

Bijvoeglijk naamwoord

moddervet

  1. (intensief) heel erg dik
Synoniemen
  • adipeus, corpulent
