sap (6089)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch sap, from Old Dutch *sap, from Proto-Germanic *sapÄ…. Cognate to English sap and German Saft (from Old High German saf). | 1 |

Noun

sap n (plural sappen, diminutive sapje n)

  1. sap (fluid in plants)
  2. juice
    Hyponyms: aalbessensap, appelsap, citroensap, druivensap, sinaasappelsap, vruchtensap
