knekelhuis (104248)


Etymology

Compound of knekel (“bone”) + | huis (“house”).

Noun

knekelhuis n (plural knekelhuizen, diminutive knekelhuisje n)

  1. building where disinterred (human) bones are stored, usually at a cemetery; bonehouse, charnel house
    Synonym: beenderhuis
