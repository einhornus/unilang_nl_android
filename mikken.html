mikken (12152)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch micken, from Old Dutch *mikken, from Proto-Germanic *mikjaną.

Verb

mikken

  1. (transitive, intransitive) to aim, to target
  2. (transitive, figuratively) to intend

Inflection

Synonyms

  • munten

mikken
