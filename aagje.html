aagje (41174)


Etymology

Name derived from Agatha, from a farce dating back to 1653 " 't Nieuwsgierig Aagje van Enkhuizen.", Curious Agatha from Enkhuizen'

Noun

aagje n (uncountable)

  1. a very curious person, "Paulina Pry".
