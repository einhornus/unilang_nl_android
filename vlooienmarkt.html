vlooienmarkt (26403)


Etymology

From vlo (“flea”) + | markt (“market”).

Noun

vlooienmarkt f (plural vlooienmarkten, diminutive vlooienmarktje n)

  1. flea market, an outdoor market for trading inexpensive antiques, curios

Synonyms

  • rommelmarkt
