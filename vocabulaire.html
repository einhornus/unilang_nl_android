vocabulaire (38717)


Etymology

Borrowed from French vocabulaire, from Late Latin vocābulārium, from Latin vocabulum.

Noun

vocabulaire n or m (plural vocabulaires)

  1. vocabulary
    Synonym: woordenschat
  2. vocabulary list, word list
    Synonym: woordenlijst
