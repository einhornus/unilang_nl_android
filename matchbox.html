matchbox (51310)


Etymology 1

From match + | box.

Noun

matchbox (plural matchboxes)

  1. A small cardboard box in which matches are kept.

Etymology 2

From the die-cast toy car brand Matchbox owned by Mattel, so-called due to the size of the toys to fix in matchboxes.

Noun

matchbox (plural matchboxes)

  1. Any small die-cast toy car, usually collectible.
