achterkamer (16423)


Etymology

Compound of achter + | kamer.

Noun

achterkamer f (plural achterkamers, diminutive achterkamertje n)

  1. backroom (with similar connotations of secrecy, especially in the diminutive)

Zelfstandig naamwoord
