rekels (113580)


Etymology

genitive of rekel 'male canine, scamp'

Adjective

rekels (comparative rekelser, superlative meest rekels or rekelst)

  1. (literally, rare) relating to a rekel (male of certain species, notably canine such as fox)
  2. (commonly; metaphorically) scampish, impish
  3. (by extension) damned, blasted
    Vader is het rekels wangedrag van zoonlief zat.
    Father has had it with sonnyboy's impish misbehavior.

Synonyms

  • rekelachtig

Noun

rekels

  1. Plural form of rekel

Zelfstandig naamwoord

rekels mv

  1. meervoud van het zelfstandig naamwoord rekel
