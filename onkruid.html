onkruid (10862)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch oncruut, from on- (“un-, not”) + cruut (“herb”). Equivalent to modern on- + | kruid.

Noun

onkruid n (plural onkruiden, diminutive onkruidje n)

  1. (uncountable) weeds; (countable) weed, unwanted plant

Synonyms

  • (unwanted plant): wied

Zelfstandig naamwoord

onkruid o
