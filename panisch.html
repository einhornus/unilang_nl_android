panisch (48728)


Etymology

Borrowed German panisch, from Middle French panice, from Ancient Greek πανικός (panikós).

Adjective

panisch (comparative panischer, superlative meest panisch)

  1. panic [from 18th c.]
    Synonym: paniek

Inflection

Related terms

  • paniek
