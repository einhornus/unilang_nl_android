atomair (133867)


Etymology

From atoom + | -air. Cf. German atomar, which may have been an influence on the Dutch term.

Adjective

atomair (not comparable)

  1. atomic (of or relating to atoms)
  2. atomic (of or relating to nuclear weapons)

Inflection

Bijvoeglijk naamwoord

atomair

  1. betrekking hebbend op atomen
  2. betrekking hebbend op atoomsplitsing
  3. ondeelbaar
