aardedonker (66201)


Etymology

Compound of aarde + | donker.

Adjective

aardedonker (not comparable)

  1. dark as night, pitch-black
    De kelder was aardedonker.
    The cellar was pitch-black.
    Synonyms: pikdonker, stikdonker
