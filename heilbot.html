heilbot (37108)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch heilbot. Equivalent to heil + | bot.

Noun

heilbot m (plural heilbotten, diminutive heilbotje n)

  1. halibut, the largest flatfish species of marine genus Hippoglossus

Zelfstandig naamwoord

heilbot m

  1. (vissen) Hippoglossus hippoglossus , grote schol, reus onder de platvissen, consumptievis
