toeter (14191)


Etymology

From toet + | -er.

Noun

toeter m (plural toeters, diminutive toetertje n)

  1. horn, claxon

Synonyms

  • claxon
  • hoorn
  • (on trains): tyfoon

Adjective

toeter (comparative toeterder, superlative toeterst)

  1. (colloquial) drunk

Inflection

Verb

toeter

  1. first-person singular present indicative of toeteren
  2. imperative of toeteren
