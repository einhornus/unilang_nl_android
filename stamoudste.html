stamoudste (99395)


Etymology

Equivalent to stam (“tribe”) + | oudste (“elder (from the superlative of oud (“old, senior”)”)

Noun

stamoudste m (plural stamoudsten)

  1. A tribal elder, senior tribesman by age and/or rank, typically member of a tribal council which may counsel or even supervise the tribal chief(s)

Related terms

  • dorpsoudste
  • stamvorst
