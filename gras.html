gras (2942)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch gras, from Old Dutch *gras, from Proto-Germanic *grasą, from Proto-Indo-European *gʰreh₁- (“to grow”).

Noun

gras n (plural grassen, diminutive grasje n)

  1. grass
