spitsroede (123645)


Etymology

spits 'point(ed)' + roede 'rod'

Noun

spitsroede f (plural spitsroeden, diminutive spitsroedetje n)

  1. switch, thin rod, used for punishment

Related terms

  • spitsroeden lopen

Zelfstandig naamwoord

spitsroede v/m | 3 |
