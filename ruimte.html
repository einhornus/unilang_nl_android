ruimte (977)


Etymology

From late Middle Dutch rumede (“space”). Equivalent to ruim + | -te.

Noun

ruimte f (plural ruimtes or ruimten)

  1. space, room for a physical object to occupy
  2. room in a building
    Synonyms: kamer, vertrek
  3. (normally with the definite article) space, outer space
