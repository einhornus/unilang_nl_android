handwerk (24531)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch hantwerc, from Old Dutch *handwerk, from Proto-Germanic *handuwerką; equivalent to hand + | werk.

Noun

handwerk n (plural handwerken, diminutive handwerkje n)

  1. handiwork

Related terms

  • handwerker
