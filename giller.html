giller (13664)


Etymology

From gillen + | -er.

Noun

giller m (plural gillers, diminutive gillertje n, feminine gilster)

  1. (informal) Something hilarious; (also) something risible, something ridiculous.
  2. (uncommon) A screamer, a yeller (one who shouts).
