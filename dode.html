dode (1172)


Etymology 1

From dood + | -e.

Noun

dode m or f (plural doden)

  1. A deceased person
    Synonym: overledene
    Synonyms: levende, ondode
  2. A casualty, victim of a fatal killing, illness, accident etc.
Derived terms
  • dodenmis
  • van de doden geen kwaad
