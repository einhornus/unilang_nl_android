afnemer (39859)


Etymology

From afnemen + | -er.

Noun

afnemer m (plural afnemers)

  1. (organisation) customer, client, buyer (of goods or services)
  2. (profession) buyer, purchaser, one who is busy with procurement professionally

Synonyms

  • (organization): cliënt, klant, opdrachtgever
  • (profession): inkoper, koper

Zelfstandig naamwoord
