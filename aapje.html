aapje (8775)


Etymology

The carriage was named after the A.R.M. drivers' uniforms, which were compared to those of the uniformed monkeys of street and circus artists.

Noun

aapje n (plural aapjes)

  1. Diminutive of aap
  2. (historical, slang) A roofed carriage that could be hired for rides.
