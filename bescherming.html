bescherming (2185)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch bescherminge. Equivalent to beschermen + | -ing.

Noun

bescherming f (plural beschermingen, diminutive bescherminkje n)

  1. protection (state or quality of being protected)
  2. protection, shelter (something that protects)
