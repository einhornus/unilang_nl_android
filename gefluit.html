gefluit (60984)


Etymology

From ge- + | fluiten (“to flute, whistle, warble”)

Noun

gefluit n (uncountable)

  1. A whistling
  2. A warbling

Synonyms

  • fluiten n (=verb)

Related terms

  • gedruis n
