begenadigen (133625)


Etymology

From be- + | genadig + | -en.

Verb

begenadigen

  1. (transitive) to grace [+ met (with)], to gracefully give [+ met (object)] (giving something that is considered a blessing)
  2. (transitive) to pardon

Derived terms

  • begenadiging

Werkwoord

begenadigen | 1 |

  1. iemand zegenen
  2. iemand geen straf geven die het wel zou verdienen
Synoniemen
  • [1] begiftigen, gratificeren, begunstigen, favoriseren
  • [2] kwijtschelden, vergeven, matsen
