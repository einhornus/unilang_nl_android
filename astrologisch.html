astrologisch (77318)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Astrologe, from Latin astrologus, from Ancient Greek ᾰ̓στρολόγος (astrológos). Equivalent to astro- + | -loog.

Adjective

astrologisch (not comparable)

  1. astrological, pertaining to astrology [from 18th c.]

Related terms
