Adjective

statisch (comparative statischer, superlative meest statisch or statischt)

  1. static, stationary, concerning a motionless state or object in equilibrium
  2. concerning statics, statical
  3. invariable, constant, immobile

Inflection

Synonyms

  • onveranderlijk

Related terms
