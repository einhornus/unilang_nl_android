kozak (29845)


Etymology

From Middle French cosaque, from Russian каза́к (kazák) and Ukrainian коза́к (kozák), from a Turkic word meaning “free man, wanderer”. Cognate with Kazakh.

Noun

kozak m (plural kozakken, diminutive kozakje n)

  1. Cossack
