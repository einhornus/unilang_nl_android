mazelen (20050)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch masel (“sore, spot on the skin”), from Old Dutch *masala, from Proto-Germanic *masalō.

Noun

mazelen pl (plural only)

  1. The acute, highly contagious viral disease measles

Related terms

  • gepokt en gemazeld
  • mazelhout n

Zelfstandig naamwoord

mazelen mv
