stase (67336)


Etymology 1

From Ancient Greek στάσις (stásis).

Noun

stase f (uncountable)

  1. stasis, inactive state, as of a bodily liquid arrested in its flow
Related terms
  • statisch

Etymology 2

From station.

Noun

stase f (plural stases, diminutive staseke n)

  1. (Brabant) train station
Synonyms
  • treinstation
