wende (46084)


Etymology 1

From wenden.

Noun

wende f (plural wenden or wendes)

  1. turnaround

Etymology 2

Verb

wende

  1. (archaic) singular present subjunctive of wenden
  2. singular past indicative and subjunctive of wennen

Zelfstandig naamwoord

wende

wende
