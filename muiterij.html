muiterij (12837)


Etymology

From the stem of muiten (“to mutiny”) + | -erij (“-ery”). Or alternatively, from muiter (“mutineer”) + | -ij (“-y”). See also the usage notes at -erij.

Noun

muiterij f (plural muiterijen, diminutive muiterijtje n)

  1. mutiny

Related terms

  • muiter

Zelfstandig naamwoord

muiterij v | 3 |
