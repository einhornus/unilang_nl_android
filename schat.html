schat (387)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch schat, from Old Dutch *skat, from Proto-Germanic *skattaz.

Noun

schat m (plural schatten, diminutive schatje n)

  1. treasure
  2. honey, darling, sweet person

schat v/m
