zonneklaar (58431)


Etymology

Compound of zon (“Sun”) + | -e- + | klaar (“clear”).

Adjective

zonneklaar (not comparable)

  1. evident, abundantly clear, clear as day

Bijvoeglijk naamwoord

zonneklaar
