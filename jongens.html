jongens (287)


Etymology

Germanic, cognate with young, youngster

Noun

jongens

  1. Plural form of jongen
  2. (colloquial) guys, a form of address for a group of male persons or a group of mixed male and female persons.
    Hoi jongens! - Hey guys!

Interjection

jongens

  1. boy!

Synonyms

  • deksels!

Zelfstandig naamwoord

jongens mv

  1. meervoud van het zelfstandig naamwoord jongen
