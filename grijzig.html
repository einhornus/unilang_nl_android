grijzig (105899)


Etymology

From grijs (“grey”) + | -ig (“-y, -ish”).

Adjective

grijzig (comparative grijziger, superlative grijzigst)

  1. greyish, of a colour or shade which resembles or hinges on grey and/or contains some grey

Inflection

Synonyms

  • (resembling grey): grijsachtig
