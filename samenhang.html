samenhang (26041)


Etymology

From samenhangen (verb)

Noun

samenhang m (plural samenhangen, diminutive samenhangetje n)

  1. (topology) connectedness
  2. relation, relationship

Synonyms

  • samenhangendheid

Verb

samenhang

  1. first-person singular present indicative of samenhangen (when using a subclause)

Werkwoord
