heftig (5302)


Etymology

16th c., borrowed from German heftig, from Middle High German heftec, heiftec, from Old High German heiftig. Can be analyzed as heft (“control”) + | -ig (“-y”).

Adjective

heftig (comparative heftiger, superlative heftigst)

  1. fierce, severe
  2. violent
  3. passionate
  4. impetuous, impulsive

Related terms

  • heftigheid

Bijvoeglijk naamwoord

heftig

  1. extreem in mate
    • De overstroming was des te heftiger omdat de stortbui kwam na een lange droogte.
Synoniemen
  • erg, rabiaat, fel, hevig

Bijwoord

heftig
