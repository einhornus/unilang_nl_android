rolstoel (5410)


Etymology

From rollen (“to roll”) + | stoel (“chair”). Attested since the nineteenth century, but already mentioned in several dictionaries during the eighteenth century.

Noun

rolstoel m (plural rolstoelen, diminutive rolstoeltje n)

  1. wheelchair [from 18th or 19th c.]

Zelfstandig naamwoord

rolstoel m

  1. (medisch) vervoermiddel voor mensen die slecht ter been zijn
Synoniemen
  • rijstoel
