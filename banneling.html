banneling (49961)


Etymology

From bannen + | -e- + | -ling (see also balling).

Noun

banneling m (plural bannelingen, diminutive bannelingetje n)

  1. exile: an exiled person

Zelfstandig naamwoord

banneling m

  1. iemand die verbannen is
    • De Dalai Lama verblijft als banneling in India.
