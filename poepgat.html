poepgat (89261)


Etymology

Compound of poepen (“to shit, to poo”) + | gat (“hole”).

Noun

poepgat n (plural poepgaten, diminutive poepgaatje n)

  1. (Netherlands) anus, butthole
    Synonyms: aars, anus
