oogst (5469)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch ogest, from Latin augustus, named after the month in which the harvest normally took place. Compare herfst and English harvest for a similar development.

Noun

oogst f or m (plural oogsten, diminutive oogstje n)

  1. harvest

oogst
