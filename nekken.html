nekken (24851)


Etymology 1

From nek + | -en.

Verb

nekken

  1. (transitive) to neck, to kill by hitting the neck or strangulation
  2. (transitive) to eliminate (e.g. a rival), to hurt very badly
Inflection
Synonyms
  • afmaken
