wijl (58539)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch wile (“while”), Old Dutch *wīla, from Proto-Germanic *hwīlō.

Cognate with English while, Low German Wiel, German Weile.

Noun

wijl f (plural wijlen, diminutive wijltje n)

  1. a short period; a while

Conjunction

wijl

  1. when
  2. while (time aspect)
  3. while (contradiction)
  4. because
Synonyms
  • (when): wanneer, wen, toen
  • (while (time aspect)): terwijl
  • (while (contradiction)): hoewel
  • (because): omdat, dewijl
Related terms
  • bij tijd en wijle
  • dewijl
  • langwijlig
  • terwijl
  • verwijlen
  • wijlen

Etymology 2

From Middle Dutch wile, wijle.

Noun

wijl f (plural wijlen, diminutive wijltje n)

  1. veil for women
  2. (figurative) something that veils
Synonyms
  • (veil): sluier, floers
Related terms
  • wijlen

Zelfstandig naamwoord

wijl v/m
