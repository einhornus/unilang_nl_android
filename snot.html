snot (18534)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch snotte, from Old Dutch *snotto, from Proto-Germanic *snuttuz.

Noun

snot n (uncountable)

  1. snot, nasal mucus

Derived terms

  • snotaap
  • snotneus
