gedachte (1916)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch gedachte, from Old Dutch githāht, from Proto-Germanic *þanhtaz.

Noun

gedachte f (plural gedachten or gedachtes, diminutive gedachtetje n)

  1. thought, idea
