volmacht (14878)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch volmacht, possibly influenced by Middle High German. Equivalent to vol + | macht. Compare German Vollmacht.

Noun

volmacht f (plural volmachten)

  1. plenipotence, full authority or power
  2. power of attorney

Derived terms

  • volmachtigen
