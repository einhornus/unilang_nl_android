hartig (37115)


Etymology

From hart (“heart”) + | -ig (“-y”).

Adjective

hartig (comparative hartiger, superlative hartigst)

  1. (rare) savoury and spicy, heavily seasoned
  2. having a salty taste, such as cheese, meat etc. (i.e. not sweet)

Inflection

Bijvoeglijk naamwoord
