gokschuld (69820)


Etymology

From gokken (“to gamble”) + | schuld (“debt”)

Noun

gokschuld m or f (plural gokschulden, diminutive gokschuldje n)

  1. (mostly in the plural: gokschulden) A gambling debt

Synonyms

  • speelschuld m or f

Related terms

  • goktent
  • ereschuld m or f
