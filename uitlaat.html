Noun

uitlaat m (plural uitlaten, diminutive uitlaatje n)

  1. exhaust, system through which burned gases, steam etc. are vented or otherwise discharged; especially used for a car exhaust
  2. any outlet, to relieve pressure, literally and figuratively
