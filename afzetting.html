afzetting (28348)


Etymology

From afzetten + | -ing.

Noun

afzetting f (plural afzettingen, diminutive afzettinkje n)

  1. cordon, fencing
  2. (geology) deposit
  3. (chemistry) precipitate
  4. (medicine) amputation
  5. deposition, removal from office
