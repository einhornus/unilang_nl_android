verzorgen (4489)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch versorgen. Equivalent to ver- + | zorgen.

Verb

verzorgen

  1. (transitive) to take care of, to look after, to nurse
  2. (transitive) to provide (a service), to supply

Synonyms

  • behartigen

Derived terms

  • verzorgd
  • verzorgpony
