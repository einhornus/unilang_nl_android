inhaleren (19851)


Etymology

Borrowed from Middle French inhaler or French inhaler, from Latin inhālō.

Verb

inhaleren

  1. (intransitive) to inhale
    Synonym: inademen
  2. (transitive) to inhale (air, medicine or drugs), to consume by inhaling

Related terms

  • inhalatie
