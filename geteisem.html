geteisem (57401)


Etymology

Borrowed from Yiddish חטאתים | (khateysem), plural of חטאת | (khates, “sin offering”), from Hebrew חַטָּאת | (khatát, “sin offering”), from חָטָא | (khatá, “to sin”).

Noun

geteisem n (uncountable)

  1. (informal) riffraff

Zelfstandig naamwoord

geteisem o | 4 |

  1. (Jiddisch-Hebreeuws) (scheldwoord) geboefte, gespuis, uitvaagsel
