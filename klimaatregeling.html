klimaatregeling (82136)


Etymology 1

From klimaat + | regeling, calque of English climate control.

Noun

klimaatregeling f (plural klimaatregelingen, diminutive klimaatregelinkje n)

  1. air conditioning, climate control
    Synonyms: airconditioning, airco

Etymology 2

From klimaat + | regeling.

Noun

klimaatregeling f (plural klimaatregelingen)

  1. A measure aimed at targeting climate change.
