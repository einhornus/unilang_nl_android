buste (37335)


Etymology 1

Borrowed from Middle French buste, from Italian busto, from Latin bustum.

Noun

buste f (plural busten or bustes, diminutive bustetje n)

  1. bust (a sculptural portrayal of a person's head and shoulders)
  2. breast, of a woman
Synonyms
  • borstbeeld

Etymology 2

See the etymology of the main entry.

Verb

buste

  1. singular past indicative and subjunctive of bussen

Zelfstandig naamwoord

buste v / m | 3 |

  1. vrouwenborst, boezem, vrouwenbovenlijf
  2. borstbeeld
  3. paspop
