schijthuis (25608)


Etymology

From schijten (“to shit”) + | huis (“house”).

Noun

schijthuis n (plural schijthuizen, diminutive schijthuisje n)

  1. (vulgar) shithouse, outhouse
    Synonyms: kakhuis, privaat, secreet
  2. (vulgar, derogatory) term of abuse for a coward

Synonyms

  • (outhouse): secreet
  • (coward; vulgar terms): broekschijter, schijtlaars, schijtlijster, platbroek
