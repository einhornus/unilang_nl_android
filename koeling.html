koeling (31131)


Etymology

From koelen + | -ing.

Noun

koeling f (plural koelingen)

  1. (uncountable) The action or process of refrigerating.
  2. (countable) A refrigerated room or section.
    1. A cold room.
      Synonym: koelcel
    2. A refrigerated section or shelve in a grocery store or supermarket.
