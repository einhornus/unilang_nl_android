lasten (18590)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch lasten. Equivalent to last + | -en.

Verb

lasten

  1. (transitive, archaic) to emburden with a load
  2. (transitive, archaic) to charge with a responsibility
Derived terms
  • belasten
  • gelasten
