nazeggen (74111)


Etymology

From na + | zeggen.

Verb

nazeggen

  1. to repeat after, to say the same as

Inflection

The irregular past tense is used in the Netherlands, while the weak past forms are used primarily in Flanders.

Synonyms

  • napraten
