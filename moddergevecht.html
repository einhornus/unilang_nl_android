moddergevecht (102884)


Etymology

modder (“mud”) + | gevecht (“a fight”) (from vechten 'to fight')

Noun

moddergevecht n (plural moddergevechten, diminutive moddergevechttje n)

  1. (literally) A mud fight, where opponents wrestle in mud or other dirt
  2. (figurative) A dirty duel, involving verbal mudslinging
