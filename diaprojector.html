diaprojector (107674)


Etymology

Compound of dia + | projector.

Noun

diaprojector m (plural diaprojectoren or diaprojectors, diminutive diaprojectortje n)

  1. A slide projector.
    Synonyms: diaprojectieapparaat, diascoop
