kwark (64316)


Etymology

Borrowed from German Quark (medieval forms twarc, zwarc, quarc), from Proto-Slavic *tvarog—ä.

Noun

kwark m (uncountable, diminutive kwarkje n)

  1. (Netherlands) the soft creamy cheese type quark, made from unripe milk
  2. (Netherlands) a bowl of quark (usually in the diminutive form)

Synonyms

  • (Belgium) plattekaas, platte kaas
