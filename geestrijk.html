geestrijk (117704)


Etymology

From geest (“ghost, spirit; mind”) + | rijk (“rich; full of”). Cognate to German geistreich.

Adjective

geestrijk (comparative geestrijker, superlative geestrijkst)

  1. smart, witty
  2. alcoholic

Synonyms

  • vernuftig
  • alcoholisch
