hommel (35081)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch hommel. This etymology is incomplete. You can help Wiktionary by elaborating on the origins of this term.

Noun

hommel f (plural hommels, diminutive hommeltje n)

  1. bumblebee (bee of the genus Bombus)
    Synonym: hommelbij
  2. (archaic) drone (male bee)
    Synonyms: dar, hommelbij

Derived terms

  • hommelbij
  • steenhommel
