wegslepen (20178)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch wechslepen. Equivalent to weg + | slepen.

Verb

wegslepen

  1. (transitive) to tow away, to draw away, to haul away

Derived terms

  • wegsleepregeling

Werkwoord
