afgelopen (816)


Etymology

Past participle of aflopen (“to end”).

Adjective

afgelopen (not comparable)

  1. last, past
    afgelopen maand
    last month
  2. finished, ended
    De film is afgelopen.
    The movie has ended.

Participle

afgelopen

  1. past participle of aflopen
