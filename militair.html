militair (4258)


Etymology

From Middle French militaire, from Latin mīlitāris (“of soldiers or war”), from mīles (“soldier”).

Noun

militair m (plural militairen, diminutive militairtje n)

  1. soldier

Adjective

militair (not comparable)

  1. military
