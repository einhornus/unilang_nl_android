vogelvrij (20649)


Etymology 1

From vogel (“bird”) + | vrij (“free”), calque of German vogelfrei.

Adjective

vogelvrij (not comparable)

  1. outlawed (declared outside of the law and unprotected by the law)
Inflection

Etymology 2

From vogel (“bird”) + | vrij (“free”).

Adjective

vogelvrij (not comparable)

  1. free as a bird (completely free)
Inflection
