exerceren (70176)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch exerceren, from Middle French exercer.

Verb

exerceren

  1. (intransitive, military) to practise, to exercise

Inflection

Related terms

  • exercitie

Werkwoord

exerceren | 4 |
