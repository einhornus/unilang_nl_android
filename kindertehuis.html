kindertehuis (39343)


Etymology

Compound of kinder + | tehuis.

Noun

kindertehuis n (plural kindertehuizen, diminutive kindertehuisje n)

  1. children's home, orphanage (institution where children, e.g. orphans or abandoned children, are raised)
