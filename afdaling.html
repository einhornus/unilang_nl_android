afdaling (19719)


Etymology

From afdalen + | -ing.

Noun

afdaling f (plural afdalingen, diminutive afdalinkje n)

  1. descent
  2. (sports) downhill (alpine skiing discipline)

Zelfstandig naamwoord

afdaling v
