tierelantijn (98764)


Etymology

Expansion of tieretein (“fabric made of wool and cotton”).

Noun

tierelantijn m (plural tierelantijnen, diminutive tierelantijntje n)

  1. (chiefly plural, often diminutive) A flourish, something ornamental and not functional.

Related terms

  • tartan
