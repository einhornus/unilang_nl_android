smiecht (55981)


Etymology

Possibly related to dialectal smuigen (“to act insidiously”), from Proto-Germanic *smeuganą. This etymology is incomplete. You can help Wiktionary by elaborating on the origins of this term.

Noun

smiecht m (plural smiechten, diminutive smiechtje n)

  1. (derogatory, dated) a rat, sneak, bastard

Zelfstandig naamwoord
