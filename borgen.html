borgen (84616)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch borgen. Equivalent to borg + | -en.

Verb

borgen

  1. (transitive) to secure, to keep safe
  2. (transitive) to guarantee
  3. (transitive, now dialectal) to lend (especially of credit)
    Synonym: lenen
  4. (transitive, dated, dialectal) to borrow
    Synonym: lenen
Derived terms
  • afborgen
Related terms
  • bergen
