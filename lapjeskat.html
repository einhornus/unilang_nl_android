lapjeskat (81920)


Etymology

Compound of lapje + | -s- + | kat.

Noun

lapjeskat m (plural lapjeskatten, diminutive lapjeskatje n)

  1. A calico cat; a (usually female) cat with a multi-coloured fur (often white, black and orange-brown) with various blots and spots, giving its fur the appearance of a patchwork.
    Synonym: lapjespoes

Zelfstandig naamwoord

lapjeskat v

  1. (zoogdieren) kat waarvan de pels oogt als een lappendeken
