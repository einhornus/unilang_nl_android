manen (12005)


Etymology 1

From Old Dutch *manon, from Proto-Germanic *manōną.

Verb

manen

  1. to urge, admonish
  2. to demand, dun (especially a payment)
Derived terms
  • maning
  • aanmanen

Etymology 2

See the etymology of the main entry.

Noun

manen

  1. Plural form of maan (“moon”)

Noun

manen

  1. Plural form of maan (“mane”)
