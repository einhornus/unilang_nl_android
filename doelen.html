doelen (5088)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch doelen (“to target-practice”), equivalent to doel + | -en.

Verb

doelen

  1. (intransitive) to aim [+ op (object) = for, at]
  2. (intransitive) to allude, hint [+ op (object) = to, at]
