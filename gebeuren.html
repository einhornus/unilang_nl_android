gebeuren (477)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch geburen, from Old Dutch geburien (“to happen, occur”), from Proto-Germanic *gaburjaną. Equivalent to ge- + | beuren. Cognate with German Low German geböhren (“to happen”), West Frisian barre (“to happen”), Old English ġebyrian (“to happen”).

Verb

gebeuren

  1. (intransitive) to happen
Inflection
Related terms
  • beurt
  • beuren

Etymology 2

Gerund of the verb gebeuren.

Noun

gebeuren n (uncountable)

  1. Something which is happening or which happened; event

Werkwoord

gebeuren

  1. ergatief plaatshebben, werkelijkheid worden

Synoniemen
  • geschieden, gevallen, spelen
