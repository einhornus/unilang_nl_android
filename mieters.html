mieters (23095)


Etymology

From sodemieters.

Interjection

mieters

  1. (dated) wow, fantastic, golly
  2. (archaic) blast, damn, tarnation

Adjective

mieters (comparative mieterser, superlative meest mieters or mieterst)

  1. (dated) great, fantastic
  2. (archaic) blasted, damned, darned

Usage notes

Use of mieters (in a positive sense) is often considered old-fashioned and commonly used to indicate that someone is less than fashionable. Negative use is rarer still.
