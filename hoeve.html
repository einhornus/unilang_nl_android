hoeve (28718)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch hoeve, from Old Dutch *huova, from Proto-Germanic *hōbō (“plot of land”).

Noun

hoeve f (plural hoeven or hoeves, diminutive hoevetje n)

  1. farmstead
