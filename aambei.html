aambei (61975)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch aenbey. Equivalent to aan (“on; to”) + | bei (“berry”). Assimilation of -n- to -m- before -b, like in aambeeld from aanbeeld.

Noun

aambei f (plural aambeien, diminutive aambeitje n)

  1. piles, haemorrhoids, hemorrhoids
    Synonym: speen

Usage notes

Commonly used in the plural.

Zelfstandig naamwoord
