azijn (16722)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch aisine, aysijn, aysine, from Old French aisin, from Latin acetulum, from Latin acetum. Doublet of edik.

Noun

azijn m (plural azijnen, diminutive azijntje n)

  1. vinegar (condiment)
