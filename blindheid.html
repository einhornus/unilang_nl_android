blindheid (17882)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch blintheit. Equivalent to blind (“blind”) + | -heid (“-ness”).

Noun

blindheid f (plural blindheden)

  1. blindness, (medical) inability to see
  2. (figuratively) unwillingness to take notice

Related terms

  • verblinding

Zelfstandig naamwoord

blindheid v
