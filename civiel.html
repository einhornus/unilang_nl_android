civiel (47633)


Etymology

From Old French civil, from Latin cīvīlis (“relating to a citizen”), from cīvis (“citizen”).

Adjective

civiel (comparative civieler, superlative civielst)

  1. civil (all senses)
  2. civilian

Derived terms

  • civielbouw

Noun

civiel n (uncountable)

  1. plain clothes

Bijvoeglijk naamwoord

civiel
