fysicus (33399)


Etymology

Borrowed from Latin physicus, ultimately from Ancient Greek φυσικός (phusikós).

Noun

fysicus m (plural fysici, diminutive fysicusje n)

  1. physicist, scientist or amateur student of physics

Synonyms

  • natuurkundige m
