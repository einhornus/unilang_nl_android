tegenvaller (26005)


Etymology

root of tegenvallen 'to go adversely' (from tegen- 'against' + vallen 'to fall') + -er

Noun

tegenvaller m (plural tegenvallers, diminutive tegenvallertje n)

  1. A disappointing result, adversity, bummer

Synonyms

  • tegenslag
  • teleurstelling

Zelfstandig naamwoord

tegenvaller m
