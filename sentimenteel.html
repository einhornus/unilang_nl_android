sentimenteel (7485)


Etymology

Borrowed from English sentimental. Equivalent to sentiment + | -eel.

Adjective

sentimenteel (comparative sentimenteler, superlative sentimenteelst)

  1. sentimental

Derived terms

  • sentimentalisme
  • sentimentaliteit

Bijvoeglijk naamwoord

sentimenteel
