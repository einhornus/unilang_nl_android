lulkoek (9418)


Etymology

From lullen (“to talk nonsense”) + | koek (“cake”).

Noun

lulkoek m (uncountable)

  1. (vulgar) bullshit, poppycock, nonsense

Synonyms

  • gelul
  • kletskoek
  • lariekoek

Related terms

  • lulhannes
  • lullebroer
  • lulmeier
