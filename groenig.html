groenig (129956)


Etymology

groen (“green”) + | -ig (adjectival suffix).

Adjective

groenig (comparative groeniger, superlative groenigst)

  1. greenish, of a colour or shade which resembles or hinges on green and/or contains some green

Inflection

Synonyms

  • (greenish): groenachtig
