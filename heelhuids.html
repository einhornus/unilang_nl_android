heelhuids (11037)


Etymology

Compound of heel (“whole, entire”) + | huid (“skin, hide”) + | -s.

Adverb

heelhuids

  1. unharmed, safe and sound, unscathed

Adjective

heelhuids (not comparable)

  1. (rare) unscathed, unharmed
    Synonym: ongedeerd

Inflection

Bijwoord
