vogelspin (58664)


Etymology

Compound of vogel (“bird”) + | spin (“spider”).

Noun

vogelspin m (plural vogelspinnen, diminutive vogelspinnetje n)

  1. (zoology) true tarantula, spider of the family Theraphosidae
  2. (informal) any large, hairy spider resembling a tarantula
