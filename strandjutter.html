strandjutter (99218)


Etymology

Compound of strand + | jutter, influenced by strandjut.

Noun

strandjutter m (plural strandjutters)

  1. beachcomber (one who collects or steals items of salvage on a beach)
    Synonyms: jutter, strandjut

Related terms
