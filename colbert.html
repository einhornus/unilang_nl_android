colbert (18277)


Etymology

Named after Jean Baptiste Colbert.

Noun

colbert m or n (plural colberts, diminutive colbertje n)

  1. A formal jacket without tails. [from 1870s]

Derived terms

  • colbertjas
  • colbertkostuum

Zelfstandig naamwoord

colbert m/o
