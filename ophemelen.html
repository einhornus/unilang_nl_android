ophemelen (46730)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch ophemelen (“to hide”). Equivalent to op + | hemelen.

Verb

ophemelen

  1. to extol, to laud, to praise into the heavens

Inflection

Synonyms

  • de hemel in prijzen

Werkwoord

ophemelen
