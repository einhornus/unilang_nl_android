sodomiet (99645)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch sodomite (“Sodomite, inhabitant of Sodom”), from Latin sodomīta, from Ancient Greek σοδομίτης (sodomítēs). Equivalent to Sodom + | -iet.

Noun

sodomiet m (plural sodomieten, diminutive sodomietje n)

  1. (derogatory, offensive) A sodomite, a man who penetrates another man anally or (more generally) any male homosexual.
