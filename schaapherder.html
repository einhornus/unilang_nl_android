schaapherder (32325)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch schaepherder. Equivalent to schaap + | herder.

Noun

schaapherder m (plural schaapherders, diminutive schaapherdertje n)

  1. Alternative form of schaapsherder.

Zelfstandig naamwoord

schaapherder m | 1 |

  1. (beroep) herder van schapen
Synoniemen
  • schapenhoeder, scheper, schaapsherder
