wezel (14577)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch wēsel, from Old Dutch *wisula, *wisala, from Proto-Germanic *wisulǭ.

Noun

wezel f (plural wezels, diminutive wezeltje n)

  1. least weasel, Mustela nivalis
  2. any weasel

Zelfstandig naamwoord

wezel v/m

  1. (dierkunde) Mustela nivales , klein schuw marterachtig roofdiertje
