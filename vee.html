vee (3757)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch vêe, from Old Dutch fē, from Proto-Germanic *fehu, from Proto-Indo-European *peḱu- (“livestock”).

Noun

vee n (uncountable)

  1. (collective) livestock, cattle

Zelfstandig naamwoord

vee o

  1. (veeteelt) door de mens om economische redenen gehouden dieren, m.n runderen, varkens en kippen
