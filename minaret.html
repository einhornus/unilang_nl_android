minaret (127180)


Etymology

Borrowed from French minaret, from Ottoman Turkish مناره‏ | (Turkish minare), from Arabic مَنَارَة | (manāra, “lighthouse”).

Noun

minaret f or m (plural minaretten, diminutive minaretje n)
