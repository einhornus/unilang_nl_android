zeeleeuw (36428)


Etymology

Compound of zee (“sea”) + | leeuw (“lion”) (as the male appears maned).

Noun

zeeleeuw m (plural zeeleeuwen, diminutive zeeleeuwtje n)

  1. The sea lion, a seal species, member of the Otariidae family
