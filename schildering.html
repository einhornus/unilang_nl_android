schildering (67590)


Etymology

From schilderen + | -ing

Noun

schildering f (plural schilderingen, diminutive schilderingetje n)

  1. mural, painting (usually meant on a wall as opposed to schilderij)

Synonyms

  • schilderij

Zelfstandig naamwoord

schildering v

  1. geschilderde voorstelling
  2. beschrijving
Verwante begrippen
  • [1] doek, schilderij, schilderstuk, tafereel
