niezen (15274)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch niesen, from Old Dutch *niesan, from Proto-Germanic *hneusaną. Because of the onomatopoeic character of the word, variant forms with different initial consonants also exist(ed), such as fniezen (from Proto-Germanic *fneusaną) and English sneeze.

Verb

niezen

  1. (intransitive) To sneeze, exhale explosively
