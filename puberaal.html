puberaal (48638)


Etymology

Probably borrowed, cf. English puberal, Italian puberale. Equivalent to puber + | -aal. This etymology is incomplete. You can help Wiktionary by elaborating on the origins of this term.

Adjective

puberaal (not comparable)

  1. Pertaining to puberty; pubescent, pubertal.
  2. (derogatory) Resembling teenage behaviour; immature.

Inflection

Bijvoeglijk naamwoord

puberaal

puberaal
