helemaal (217)


Etymology

From earlier heeltemael, gheheel te mael. Compare Dutch allemaal, English suffix -meal.

Adverb

helemaal

  1. completely
  2. (colloquial) anyway
    Wie ben jij nu helemaal? | Who are you anyway?
