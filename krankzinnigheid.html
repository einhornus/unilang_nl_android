krankzinnigheid (15156)


Etymology

krankzinnig + | -heid

Noun

krankzinnigheid f (plural krankzinnigheden)

  1. insanity, madness, serious psychiatric condition
  2. (figuratively) grave foolishness, idiocy, a mad stunt
