besmetting (10939)


Etymology

From besmetten + | -ing.

Noun

besmetting f (plural besmettingen, diminutive besmettinkje n)

  1. contagion, contamination
    Er is een groot risico op besmetting met hepatitis.
    There is a large risk of being infected with hepatitis.

Derived terms

  • besmettingsgevaar

Related terms

  • besmetten
  • besmettelijk
