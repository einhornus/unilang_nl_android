doorlopen (2567)


Etymology 1

From door- + | lopen.

Verb

doorlopen

  1. (transitive) to go through, to go from start to end

Etymology 2

From door + | lopen.

Verb

doorlopen

  1. (intransitive) to keep going, to move on, to carry on, to continue

Etymology 3

See the etymology of the main entry.

doorlopen
