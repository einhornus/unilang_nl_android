paraaf (44880)


Etymology

Borrowed from French paraphe, from Latin paraphus. Compare English paraph.

Noun

paraaf m (plural parafen, diminutive paraafje n)

  1. One's initials used as alternative for an autograph when signing.

Related terms

  • paragraaf

Zelfstandig naamwoord

paraaf m

  1. handtekening die alleen bestaat uit initialen
