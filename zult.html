zult (441)


Etymology 1

Verb

zult

  1. second-person singular present indicative of zullen

Etymology 2

From Middle Dutch sulte (“brine, salted food”), from Old Dutch sulta (“salt water”), from Proto-Germanic *sultijō (“salt water, brine”). Cognate with Old Saxon sultia, Old High German sulza (German Sülze).

Noun

zult m (uncountable)

  1. brawn, head cheese
Synonyms
  • hoofdkaas
Derived terms
  • zultkop

Zelfstandig naamwoord

zult m

    • Ik hou niet van zure zult.
