ploert (20246)


Etymology

Unknown.

Noun

ploert m (plural ploerten, diminutive ploertje n)

  1. scoundrel, bounder
  2. git, jerk
  3. (historical, obsolete, student slang) Someone who doesn't study or hasn't studied at a university.
  4. (historical, obsolete, student slang) The landlady or landlord of a student's housing.
