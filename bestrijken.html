Etymology

From Middle Dutch bestriken. Equivalent to be- + | strijken.

Verb

bestrijken

  2. (transitive) to cover, to be spread across

  3. (transitive, military, of ranged weapon, especially artillery) to reach, to cover (a location or distance)
