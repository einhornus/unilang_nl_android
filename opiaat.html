opiaat (126009)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch *opiaet (only attested as opiaten), from Latin opiātum, from opiātus. Equivalent to opium + | -aat.

Noun

opiaat n (plural opiaten, diminutive opiaatje n)

  1. opiate (drug, hormone or other substance derived from or related to opium)
    Synonym: opiumderivaat

Zelfstandig naamwoord

opiaat o

  1. (medisch) opiumbevattend geneesmiddel
