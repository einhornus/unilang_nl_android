mispunt (45075)


Etymology

19th century. Compound of missen (“to miss”) + | punt (“point”).

Noun

mispunt n (plural mispunten, diminutive mispuntje n)

  1. (billiards, obsolete) foul, miss [19th c.]
  2. (by extension) jerk, prick, nasty [from 19th c.]
