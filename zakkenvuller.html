zakkenvuller (96674)


Etymology

Compound of zak + | -en- + | vullen + | -er.

Noun

zakkenvuller m (plural zakkenvullers, diminutive zakkenvullertje n)

  1. A profiteer, moneygrubber; someone who is enriched at the expense of others; (by extension) corrupt person; a financial parasite.

Zelfstandig naamwoord

zakkenvuller m
