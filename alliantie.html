alliantie (9660)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch alliancie, from Middle French alliance, from Old French aliance (French alliance).

Noun

alliantie f (plural allianties, diminutive alliantietje n)

  1. alliance

Synonyms

Zelfstandig naamwoord

alliantie v
