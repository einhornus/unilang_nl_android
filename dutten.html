dutten (45581)


Etymology

Unclear. May be from Middle Dutch dutten (“to rage”), through the semantic development "rage" | "be out of one's mind" | "be unconscious" | "doze".

Verb

dutten

  1. (transitive) to doze, to nap, to sleep lightly

Inflection
