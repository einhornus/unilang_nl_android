prullaria (80223)


Etymology

No later than the late eighteenth century. From prul + | -aria. It seems to have originally meant “literary dross”.

Noun

prullaria f (uncountable)

  1. (collective) trinkets, trifles

prullaria

  1. Plural form of prullarium
