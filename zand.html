zand (2797)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch sant, from Old Dutch *sant, from Proto-Germanic *samdaz, from Proto-Indo-European *sámh₂dʰos. Compare Low German Sand, West Frisian sand, German Sand, English sand, Danish sand.
