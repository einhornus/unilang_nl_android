droevig (5622)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch droevich. Equivalent to droef + | -ig.

Adjective

droevig (comparative droeviger, superlative droevigst)

  1. sad, melancholy
  2. gloomy

Inflection

Synonyms

  • treurig, triest, sip

Related terms

  • droef
  • droeven

  2. verdrietig gevoel veroorzakend
