doorkruist (29988)


Etymology 1

Verb

doorkruist

  1. second- and third-person singular present indicative of doorkruisen (“travel across”)
  2. (archaic) plural imperative of doorkruisen (“travel across”)

Participle

doorkruist

  1. past participle of doorkruisen (“travel across”)

Etymology 2

Verb

doorkruist

  1. second- and third-person singular present indicative of doorkruisen (“to cross out”) (when using a subclause)

Werkwoord

doorkruist
