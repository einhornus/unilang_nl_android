ovaal (46709)


Etymology

Borrowed from French ovale.

Adjective

ovaal (comparative ovaler, superlative ovaalst)

  1. oval

Inflection

Noun

ovaal n (plural ovalen, diminutive ovaaltje n)

  1. (geometry) oval, shape like an egg or ellipse

Bijvoeglijk naamwoord

ovaal
