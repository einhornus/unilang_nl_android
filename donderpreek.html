donderpreek (83933)


Etymology

Compound of donder (“thunder”) or donderen (“to thunder; to shout”) + | preek (“sermon”).

Noun

donderpreek f (plural donderpreken, diminutive donderpreekje n)

  1. (also figurative) A fire-and-brimstone sermon.

Zelfstandig naamwoord

donderpreek v/m
