inviteren (51602)


Etymology

From French inviter (“to invite”), a modern borrowing (rather than descendant) from Latin invītāre, present active infinitive of invītō.

Verb

inviteren

  1. (formal) to invite

Inflection

Synonyms

  • (neutral) uitnodigen
  • (dated) uitnoden
  • (archaic) laden

Werkwoord
