mees (81504)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch mese, from Old Dutch *mēsa, from Proto-Germanic *maisǭ (“chickadee, titmouse”).

Noun

mees f (plural mezen, diminutive meesje n)

  1. tit, bird of the family Paridae
