shit (831)


Etymology

Borrowed from English shit. Cognate to Dutch schijt.

Interjection

shit

  1. shit, darn

Usage notes

Not considered vulgar, unlike in some English lects.

Noun

shit m (uncountable)

  1. (slang) stuff
  2. (slang) trouble

Related terms

  • scheet
  • schijt
  • schijten
