commercie (32735)


Etymology

Borrowed from Middle French commerce, from Latin commercium (“commerce, trade”), from com- (“together”) + merx (“good, wares, merchandise”). See merchant, mercenary.

Noun

commercie f (uncountable)

  1. (business) commerce
