zandkasteel (50196)


Etymology

From zand (“sand”) + | kasteel (“castle”).

Noun

zandkasteel n (plural zandkastelen, diminutive zandkasteeltje n)

  1. A sandcastle, a sculpture made of sand resembling a fortication

Related terms

  • zandsculptuur
