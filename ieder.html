ieder (827)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch ieder, jeder, from Old Dutch *iowether, *iother. A compound of io (“always”) (from Proto-Germanic *aiw) + wether (“whether”) (from Proto-Germanic *hwaþeraz). Cognate with English either, German jeder.

Determiner

ieder

  1. each
  2. any
    In ieder geval
    In any case

Inflection

Synonyms

  • elk
  • alle

Pronoun

ieder m or f (genitive ieders)

  1. everybody, everyone
Afgeleide begrippen
  • eenieder, iedereen
