masseren (10865)


Etymology

From French masser, from Latin massare.

Verb

masseren (transitive)

  1. To (give a) massage
  2. (figuratively) To work on, prepare, position etc.; notably in billiards, give a head-shot with side-effect

Related terms

  • massage
  • masseur m

Werkwoord
