apenkooi (77354)


Etymology 1

From aap (“ape”, “monkey”) + | -en- + | kooi (“cage”).

Noun

apenkooi f (plural apenkooien, diminutive apenkooitje n)

  1. A place where apes and/or monkeys are kept, an apery.
  2. (sports, games, uncountable) A variation of tag, usually played in school gyms, where players are not allowed to touch the floor and can only move about via mats, vaulting horses, bars, ropes, etc. Anyone who is caught or touches the floor has to leave the field of play.

Etymology 2

See the etymology of the main entry.

Verb

apenkooi

  1. first-person singular present indicative of apenkooien
  2. imperative of apenkooien
