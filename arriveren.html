arriveren (9393)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch ariveren, from Old French ariver, from Vulgar Latin *arrīpō.

Verb

arriveren

  1. (intransitive) to arrive

Derived terms

  • arrivering

Werkwoord

arriveren

  1. ergatief de bestemming bereiken
