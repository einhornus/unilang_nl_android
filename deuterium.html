deuterium (35226)


Etymology

Coined by Harold Urey, an American chemist, from Ancient Greek δεύτερος (deúteros, “second”) + -ium.

Noun

deuterium n (uncountable)

  1. deuterium

Zelfstandig naamwoord

deuterium o
