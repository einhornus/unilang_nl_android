halssnoer (29916)


Etymology

Compound of hals (“neck”) + | snoer (“lace, cord; short for necklace”). Compare German Halsschnur, which might be the source.

Noun

halssnoer n (plural halssnoeren, diminutive halssnoertje n)

  1. necklace [from early 18th c.]

Synonyms

  • halsketting

Zelfstandig naamwoord
