wees (424)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch wêse, from Old Dutch wēso, from Proto-West Germanic *waiso. Cognate with German Waise, Middle Low German wêse, probably Old English *wāsa (in wuduwāsa).

Noun

wees m or f (plural wezen, diminutive weesje n)

  1. An orphan, who survived both parents
