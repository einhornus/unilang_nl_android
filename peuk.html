Noun

peuk m (plural peuken, diminutive peukje n)

  1. A cigarette butt, a stub.
    Synonym: sigarettenpeuk
  2. A cigarette, a fag, a smoke.
    Synonym: sigaret
  3. (rare) A cigar stub.
    Synonym: sigarenpeuk
  4. (archaic) A blunt end, a butt.
