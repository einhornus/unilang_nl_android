coffeeshop (47623)


Etymology

Borrowed from English coffee shop. A pseudo-anglicism.

Noun

coffeeshop m (plural coffeeshops, diminutive coffeeshopje n)

  1. An establishment that sells cannabis products and often also non-alcoholic refreshments.

Derived terms

  • coffeeshophouder
