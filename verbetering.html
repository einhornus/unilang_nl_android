verbetering (9624)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch verbeteringe. Equivalent to verbeteren + | -ing.

Noun

verbetering f (plural verbeteringen, diminutive verbeteringetje n)

  1. An improvement, amelioration
  2. A correction, rectification,
  3. A reparation, amendment
  4. The act or process of improving, correcting etc.
  5. (obsolete) A reprimand; correction, sanction
  6. A raise, promotion etc.

Related terms

  • verbeteraar m
  • verbeterhuis n
  • onverbeterlijk
