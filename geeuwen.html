geeuwen (69995)


Etymology 1

From Middle Dutch gewen, from Old Dutch *giwon, from Proto-Germanic *giwōną.

Verb

geeuwen

  1. (intransitive) to yawn
Inflection
Synonyms
  • gapen

See the etymology of the main entry.

Noun

geeuwen

  1. Plural form of geeuw

Werkwoord

geeuwen
