weigeren (3303)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch weigeren, from Old Dutch *weigaron, *wēgaron, from Proto-Germanic *waigarōną (“to be stubborn”), from the adjective *waigaraz (“stubborn”).

Verb

weigeren

  1. (intransitive, transitive) to refuse, to deny (not to allow)

Synonyms

  • afwijzen
  • ontzeggen

Derived terms

  • weigerachtig
  • weigerambtenaar
  • weigerig
  • weigering
  • weigerlijk
  • verweigeren
