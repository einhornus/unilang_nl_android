buizerd (49832)


Etymology

From earlier buysard, buzart, from Middle French busard, from Old French buison, busart, from Latin būteō.

Noun

buizerd m (plural buizerds, diminutive buizerdje n)

  1. buzzard, bird of prey of the genus Buteo
  2. common buzzard, Buteo buteo

Zelfstandig naamwoord

buizerd m
