sarren (32328)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch serren. Template:etysub

Verb

sarren

  1. (transitive) to pester, to bother, to torment
  2. (transitive, archaic) to tease, to rib

Inflection

Werkwoord

sarren

Synoniemen
