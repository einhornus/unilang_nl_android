vingernagel (52822)


Etymology

From vinger (“finger”) + | nagel (“nail”). Compare English fingernail, Dutch vingernagel, Danish fingernegl, Norwegian fingernegl, Swedish fingernagel, Icelandic fingurnögl.

Noun

vingernagel m (plural vingernagels, diminutive vingernageltje n)

  1. fingernail
