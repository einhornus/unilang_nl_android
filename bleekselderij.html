bleekselderij (108791)


Etymology

Compound of bleek or bleken + | selderij.

Noun

bleekselderij f (plural bleekselderijen)

  1. (chiefly uncountable) Celery, a cultivar derived from wild celery cultivated for its stems, Apium graveolens var. dulce.
