vechten (652)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch vechten, from Old Dutch fehtan, from Proto-Germanic *fehtaną, from Proto-Indo-European *peḱ-.

Verb

vechten

  1. (intransitive) to fight

Inflection

Werkwoord

vechten
