gezang (14142)


Etymology

From Middle Dutch gesanc. Equivalent to a verbal noun from zingen.

Noun

gezang n (plural gezangen, diminutive gezangetje n)

  1. song (the act of singing)
  2. song (sung piece of music)
    1. (in particular, religion, especially Protestantism) A hymn; a religious song, usually excluding psalms

Synonyms

  • lied
