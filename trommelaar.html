trommelaar (67053)


Etymology

from the root of trommelen 'to drum, ruffle' (from trommel 'a drum') + -aar '-er'

Noun

trommelaar m (plural trommelaars, diminutive trommelaartje n)

  1. A drummer, percussionist
  2. Someone who drums (nervously) with his fingers
  3. (figurative) An animal which drums, drills or makes a similar sound, notably woodpecker, cockroach and a pigeon specious

Synonyms

  • (percussionist) drummer m, slagwerker m, trommelslager m

Related terms

  • getrommel n
