zoiets (457)


Etymology

Univerbation of zo + | iets.

Pronoun

zoiets

  1. something like that, such a thing
    Zoiets mag je niet zeggen!
    You can't say such a thing!
  2. (with a partitive adjective) such a ... thing
    Ik heb nog nooit zoiets moois gezien!
    I've never seen such a beautiful thing.
  3. something like that, something similar, the like
    Ik heb niet zo'n honger. Heb je ook appelsap of zoiets?
    I'm not that hungry. Do you also have apple juice or something like that?
    Synonym: iets dergelijks
