rolluik (48577)


Etymology

From rollen (“to roll”) + | luik (“shutter”) or from rol (“roll, scroll”) + | luik (“shutter”).

Noun

rolluik n (plural rolluiken, diminutive rolluikje n)

  1. roller shutter
